* 1 In The Inner World ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the inner world

War-mongers are

Pitiful fear-hostages.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 Man's Familiar War-Excuse: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Man's familiar war-excuse:

I was not responsible.

He, only he.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 A Man Of War ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A man of war

Does not actually need anything

But wants everything immediately.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Alas, The Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas, the heart

Cannot understand

Why and how

The mind's war-insanity

#+END_VERSE


* 5 We Do Not Need ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We do not need

Heaven-born saints

But just earth-born seekers

Who believe in peace

And want to live in peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 War-Mongers Are First-Class ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War-mongers are first-class

Insecurity-coward-singers.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 He Who Loves War ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He who loves war

Is undoubtedly

The abysmal terrorist.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 God-Lovers Long For ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God-lovers long for

Ever-advancing peace.

War-lovers desire

Ever-multiplying destruction.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 A War-Lover, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A war-lover,

No matter how long

He chases after satisfaction-deer,

Will never succeed.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 It Is The Height Of Absurdity ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

It is the height of absurdity

When the war-mongers interpret

In their own way

God's all-fulfilling Peace-Delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 No Human Stupidity ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No human stupidity

Can take the place

Of the animal brutality:

War.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 War Enjoys ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War enjoys

Helplessness-cries.

Peace enjoys

Fulness-smiles.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 A Man Of War Thinks ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A man of war thinks

It is beneath his dignity

To visit a mental asylum

Although he is absolutely

The perfect patient.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 War Is The Victory-Drum ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War is the victory-drum

Of the animal consciousness.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 War Is The Stupid Winner ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War is the stupid winner

Of futility

And the unavoidable loser

Of dignity.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 A Man Of War Is, Indeed,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A man of war is, indeed,

The worst possible

Self-doubt-sufferer.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 A Man Of War ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A man of war

Does not care for his heart

As long as he knows that

He has a world-devouring mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 War Is At Once ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War is at once

Mind the thief

And heart the grief.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 War Shows Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War shows me

The utter helplessness of earth

And the shocking indifference

Of Heaven.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 War-Enjoyers Cannot Answer ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War-enjoyers cannot answer

The simplest question,

Which any child can:

"What is life?"

#+END_VERSE


* 21 A Man Of War ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A man of war

Has the face of shameless pride

And the heart of deathless insecurity.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 War Is Man's ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War is man's

Mind-prison-slavery.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 A War-Monger Is Destined ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A war-monger is destined

To be a sorrow-millionaire.

A peace-lover eventually becomes

A joy-billionaire.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 In The Inner World ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:51
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the inner world

War-lovers are

Insecurity-dancers.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 War-Mongers Do Not Respond ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:52
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War-mongers do not respond

Even to God's

Urgent emergency Calls.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 War-Lovers Are Never Permitted ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:53
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War-lovers are never permitted

To enter into the heart-kingdom.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 War Is Man's ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WMAA:54
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

War is man's

God-Satisfaction-promise-devourer.

#+END_VERSE
