* 1.


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My aspiration-heart has covered
    The faraway land
Of Immortality’s Nectar-Dreams.

#+END_VERSE


* 2.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s Heart hungers
    Every day
For my heart’s morning prayers.

#+END_VERSE


* 3.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart sleeplessly cries,
Not for my mind’s immediate demise,
But for my mind’s full illumination.

#+END_VERSE


* 4.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind, I am telling you
That you will not be able to deny
My soul’s life-transforming delight
    Indefinitely.

#+END_VERSE


* 5.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The outer advice
    Is utterly useless.
The inner prayer
    Is absolutely fruitful.

#+END_VERSE


* 6.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

    I call it
My heart-expansion.
    God calls it
His Satisfaction-Delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 7.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved Supreme,
Your single Touch
Has brought me back
My inspiration-mind,
My aspiration-heart
    And
My dedication-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 8.


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Desire comes to torture my heart.
Desire comes to kill my life.
Desire comes to take me away
From my Absolute Lord Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you really love God,
Then you must never, never
Be pleased with yourself
    In your own way.
God’s Way is the only Way
For you to be pleased with yourself.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:

What now I have
Is an earthly mind,
But I must become
A Heavenly heart.

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:

His Compassion-Smiles

  :GENRE: prayer
