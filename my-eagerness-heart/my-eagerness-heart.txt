* 1 My Eagerness-Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My eagerness-heart
And my self-giving life
Always win
The inner race.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 Alas, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
When can I put an end
To my confrontation
With self-doubt?

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Although My Prayers ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Although my prayers
And meditations
Do not reach
Their usual height,
I am basking in the Lord's
Constant Forgiveness-Light.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Alas, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
What can I do with the mind
That is always hostile
To change?

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Something ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Something
Supremely important:
My oneness-willingness-heart
With the Will
Of my Lord Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 If There Is No Positive Effort ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If there is no positive effort
To change our life,
How can we ever fulfil
Our God-manifestation-dreams?

#+END_VERSE


* 7 Small-Minded People ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Small-minded people
Are always finding fault
With everything
And everyone.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 You Cannot Always ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You cannot always
Avoid suffering,
But you can always try
To transform suffering
Into happiness.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 In My Silence-Dream ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In my silence-dream
I become a citizen
Of the perfection-land.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 What Do I Pray For?...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What do I pray for?
I pray for an elevator ride
To God's
Compassion-flooded Floor.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 The Outer World Demands....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The outer world demands.
The inner world commands.
I am totally lost in between.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 In Vain ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In vain
My heart every day
Tries to establish
A close friendship
With my mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 I Want To Love God....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I want to love God.
That is all.
I do not want to know
Why and how.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 If You Have A Self-Giving Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you have a self-giving heart
For the world,
Then the happiness
Of your Beloved Supreme
Will be yours.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 An Inner Pilgrimage ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

An inner pilgrimage
Without love, devotion
And surrender
Will be utterly disastrous.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 You Think ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You think
That God's Wisdom
Cannot speak
Through all human beings.
But you are mistaken.
His Wisdom-Light can speak
Even through
The worst possible fool.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 When My Life ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When my life
Is in my division-mind,
No matter what I choose,
Eventually
I am totally frustrated.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 When My Life ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When my life
Is in my oneness-heart,
Not only do I have everything,
But also
I become everything.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 My Lord Supreme, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Supreme,
Since the entire universe
Belongs to You,
You can give me shelter
Anywhere You want to.
But I would like to remain
Only in
Your Compassion-Heart
Or at
Your Forgiveness-Feet.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 God Tells Us ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God tells us
That there is only one way
For us to be cured
Of our ignorance-disease,
And that is by
Consciously and sleeplessly
Loving Him more.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 A Surrender-Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A surrender-heart
Is the most perfect place
To embark
On our highest soul-journey.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 The Frequent Visits ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The frequent visits
Of self-doubt
Have made us
Not only helpless
But also useless.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 When You Become ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When you become
Your willingness-mind
And eagerness-heart,
Perfection
Fast approaches you.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 Our Greatness ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Our greatness
Is in climbing up
To the top of the tree.
Our goodness
Is in becoming
The tree itself.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 O God-Satisfaction-Dreams, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O God-satisfaction-dreams,
Fly far beyond
Humanity's happiness-horizon!

#+END_VERSE


* 26 The Very Nature Of Love ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The very nature of love
Is to forgive,
And forgiveness is
God's ever-transcending
Universal experience.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 What I Need ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What I need
Is the purity
Of goodness,
And not the prosperity
Of greatness.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 Only A Surrender-Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only a surrender-heart
Will have the confidence
To march bravely
Into the land unknown.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 When The Newness-Mind ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When the newness-mind
Serves the fulness-heart,
Life grows into
Harmony, peace
And perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 You Are Displeasing God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are displeasing God
By cherishing
Your doubting mind.
You are disappointing God
By not using
Your confidence-light.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 My Bossy Ego ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My bossy ego
And my busy ego
Move together to arrive at
A hallucination-created
Destination.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 Ignorance-Lethargy-Sleep, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ignorance-lethargy-sleep,
I shall prove to you
From now on
That I can and I shall be
Your absolutely worst foe.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 To A Divinely Peaceful ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To a divinely peaceful
Oneness-heart,
No door can remain closed.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 My God-Hunger-Hour ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My God-hunger-hour
Is the right time,
And
My God-nourishment-heart
Is the right place.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 Two Things I Truly Love: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Two things I truly love:
My life's
Prayer-mountain
And my heart's
Meditation-fountain.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 O Audacity-Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O audacity-mind,
I am not afraid of you.
O tenacity-vital,
I am not afraid of you.
O God the Power,
I am not afraid of You.
O God the Compassion-Tears,
I am indeed afraid of You.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 The Heart-University Teaches ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The heart-university teaches
Both the unity and diversity
Of life
To the humanity-students.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 A Life Without Fear Of Death ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A life without fear of death
Comes only from
The beauty and fragrance
Of a God-surrendered breath.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 God's Compassion-Eye ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's Compassion-Eye
Teaches me.
God's Satisfaction-Heart
Rewards me.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 O Heart Of Optimism, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O heart of optimism,
I love you immensely.
O mind of pessimism,
I detest you vehemently.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 If You Do Not Spend ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you do not spend
Your heart's time
In prayer and meditation,
Then there will be
No Heaven-home for you.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 In The Depth ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MEH:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the depth
Of each human life
There is a fulfilling breath
Of the Lord Supreme.

#+END_VERSE
