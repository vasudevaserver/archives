/Kuantan, Malaysia/



* 1 God-Realisation ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3212
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God-realisation
Is never
For the weak.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 God-Realisation ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3213
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God-realisation
Is also never
For the strong.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 God-Realisation ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3214
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God-realisation
Is only
For God-surrendered
God-seekers.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 If I Give God Anything ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3215
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If I give God anything --
Just a little,
An insignificant little --
Lovingly, cheerfully
And self-givingly,
God gets immense joy.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 When I Pray To God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3216
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I pray to God
For the fulfilment of my desires,
God says to me,
“I am ready to fulfil your desires,
But will you be ready
For the day I choose?
I am sure you will have
No patience to wait.
Your desire-fulfilment-day
Will take a very, very long time.
By that time
Your desire will disappear.”

#+END_VERSE


* 6 God Says To Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3217
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God says to me,
“Your success
Gives Me very little joy.
Your progress
Gives Me much more joy.
Your love for Me,
Your concern for Me,
Your satisfaction
In My Satisfaction --
My Way is your way --
Give Me boundless
And boundless Bliss.”

#+END_VERSE


* 7 When We Speak Ill Of The World ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3218
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When we speak ill of the world
Almost on a daily basis,
We undoubtedly give
A most painful headache
To God.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 If I Lose My Faith In God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3219
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If I lose my faith in God
And at the same time feel
That out of sheer Concern for me
God will give me abundant faith,
This kind of hope or expectation
Will never, never succeed.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 God Never Thinks Ill Of Us ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3220
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God never thinks ill of us --
Never!
It is we who are quite apt
To think ill of ourselves.
The more we think ill of ourselves,
God’s Golden Shore sails away
Far, farther, farthest.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3221
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God,
Are You pleased with me?
“My child,
If you are pleased with yourself,
Yes!
If you are not pleased with yourself,
Then I shall never be pleased
With you.”

#+END_VERSE
