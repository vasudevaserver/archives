* 2101 You Have The First Place

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: FF:2101
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You have the first place
In my aspiration-life.

My sweet Lord,
You are the first choice
Of my surrender-heart.
