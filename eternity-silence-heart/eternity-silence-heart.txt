* 1. Save time, choose time

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:1
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My soul chose time
For my God-realisation.
I am choosing time
For my Light-manifestation.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. The heart of a God-lover

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:2
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
You were the proud arms
Of a conqueror.
Today
You are the proud eyes
Of an emperor.
Tomorrow
You will be the surrendered heart
Of a God-lover.

#+END_VERSE


* 3. You have broken the law

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:3
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You have broken
Both God-Law
And
Man-law.
You have broken God-Law
By speaking to ignorance-night.
You have broken man-law
By undeservedly mixing
With ignorance-height.

#+END_VERSE


* 4. The Delight of God-Life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:4
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The Vision of God-Light
She was.
The Light of God-Soul
She is.
The Delight of God-Life
She will become.

#+END_VERSE


* 5. Birthmark

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:5
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Some are born with aspiration-seed.
I am born with desire-tree.
Some are born with realisation-flower.
I am born with suspicion-fruit.

#+END_VERSE


* 6. Each has a way

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:6
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Heaven has a way
Of crushing my pride.
Earth has a way
Of increasing my folly.
God has a way
Of magnifying earth-capacity
And
Heaven-divinity.

#+END_VERSE


* 7. In my case

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:7
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In your case
You do not flourish;
Yet you have no complaint,
You are divine.
In his case
He does not flourish;
Therefore he complains.
He is human.
In my case
I unmistakably flourish;
Yet I constantly complain.
I am an animal,
Alas, alas!

#+END_VERSE


* 8. When I live in the heart

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:8
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When
I live in the mind
I think I know
More than I really know.
When
I live in the heart
I know I am loved
More than I really love.

#+END_VERSE


* 9. Nothing dies

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:9
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing dies,
Not even a fleeting second.
A fleeting second lives
In the silence-heart
Of Eternity
Unerringly,
Divinely
And
Supremely.

#+END_VERSE


* 10. Planet sun, inner sun

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:10
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The planet sun
Grows old and cold.
The inner sun
Remains birthless and deathless.
My body-life
Is under the planet sun.
My soul-life
Is within my inner sun.

#+END_VERSE


* 11. Doubt-role

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:11
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do you know
Why You hate yourself?
You hate yourself
Because
Doubt plays the most blighting role
In your subconscious.
I am sure sooner or later
You will know it.
And then you will admire
My wisdom-light.

#+END_VERSE


* 12. Fear-role

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:12
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do you know
Why
He hates himself?
He hates himself
Because
Fear plays the most damaging role
In his unconscious life.
I am sure sooner or later
He will feel it,
And then he will admire
My wisdom-light.

#+END_VERSE


* 13. Falsehood-role

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:13
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do you know
Why
I hate myself?
I hate myself
Because
Falsehood plays the most dangerous role
In my conscious life.
I am sure sooner or later
You and the whole world
Will know it.
And then you will admire
My confession-might.

#+END_VERSE


* 14. The Supreme's Perfection-Dream

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:14
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Fame is the flow
Of the mind's uncertainty-river.
Success is the noise
Of the vital's life-deafening drum.
Progress is the glow
Of the Supreme's Perfection-Dream.

#+END_VERSE


* 15. Your life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:15
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
Your life was your
Incessant expectation.
Today
Your life is your
Incessant frustration.
Tomorrow
Your life shall be God's
Incessant Compassion.

#+END_VERSE


* 16. I marvel

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:16
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Master,
I marvel at your patience-tree.
Daughter,
I marvel at your sincerity-flower.
Master,
I marvel at your forgiveness-height.
Daughter,
I marvel at your gratitude-depth.

#+END_VERSE


* 17. A God-failure

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:17
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I remove my earth-divinity
I am a God-failure.
I see it
But
I ignore it.
When I remove my Heaven-reality
I am a God-failure,
But
I deny it.
When I remove my God-necessity
I am a God-failure.
I don't deny it.

#+END_VERSE


* 18. We love flatterers

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:18
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You love flatterers,
But you don't realise it.
He loves flatterers,
But he does not admit it.
I love flatterers,
But I cleverly manage
To hide it.

#+END_VERSE


* 19. Spending my time

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:19
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I have spent
Most of my time
Looking and dreaming.
I am spending
Most of my time
Looking and crying.
I shall spend
Most of my time
Looking and smiling:
Looking at the vision-tree,
Smiling at the realisation-fruit.

#+END_VERSE


* 20. No limit

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:20
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There is a limit
To my ignorance-dream.
But
There is no limit
To my divinity-scheme
And
My perfection-gleam.

#+END_VERSE


* 21. Soul-life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:21
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mind-life:
I see it as an obscurity-life.
Heart-life:
I feel it as an opportunity-life.
Soul-life:
I know it as a prosperity-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 22. An unknown man

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:22
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
I was an unknown man;
Yet I was really happy.
Today
I am a known man;
Yet I am unmistakably unhappy.

#+END_VERSE


* 23. Conscience of freedom

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:23
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Conscience of freedom
My Heaven-life is.
Freedom of conscience
My earth-life needs.

#+END_VERSE


* 24. Yet you starve

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:24
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Emotion of the inferior man
You see and forgive.
Emotion of the superior man
You see and hide.
Emotion of the equal man
You see and expose.
Your own emotion you feast on,
Yet you starve.

#+END_VERSE


* 25. Something more

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:25
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God guides me
Invisibly.
I guide mankind
Ostensibly.
I do something more:
I love God
Undeniably.

#+END_VERSE


* 26. Soul-light, silence-life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:26
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Abundant soul-light:
That is what you have,
Do you know that?
Perhaps you do not know.
Abundant silence-life:
That is what you are.
Do you know that?
Perhaps you do not know.

#+END_VERSE


* 27. Don't feel embarrassed

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:27
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Don't feel embarrassed
At your fear of him.
Even the loftiest mountains
Tremble before him.
Don't be proud
Of your friendship with him.
Even the feeblest ant
Dines with him regularly.

#+END_VERSE


* 28. Darkness and light

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:28
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Darkness wants
To devour light.
Light wants
To transform darkness.
God says to darkness:
"Darkness, stop!"
God says to light:
"Light start!
Lo, you have won the goal."

#+END_VERSE


* 29. Four faces of God

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:29
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Justice-God
Watches.
Kindness-God
Blesses.
Compassion-God
Embraces.
Oneness-God
Loves.

#+END_VERSE


* 30. Three things I like

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:30
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There are three things in him
I like unreservedly:
The sound of his voice,
The beauty of his choice,
The power of his noise.

#+END_VERSE


* 31. My will

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:31
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My will to survive
Feeds the weak man in me.
My will to thrive
Feeds the strong man in me.
My will to strive
Feeds the divine man in me.

#+END_VERSE


* 32. Then and now

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:32
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He was good;
Therefore
He was always accessible
Always reasonable
Always amiable.
He is now great;
Therefore
He is inaccessible,
Unreasonable
And
Unbearable.

#+END_VERSE


* 33. My God

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:33
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My God in Heaven
Watches me.
My God on earth
Perfects me.
My God in my love-surrender-life
Immortalises me.

#+END_VERSE


* 34. Three inner messages

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:34
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My aspiration tells me
That you are a chosen son of God.
My intuition tells me
That you are a good friend of God.
My realisation tells me
That you are none other than
God Himself.

#+END_VERSE


* 35. The garment of life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:35
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Simplicity
Is the garment of life.
Sincerity
Is the body of life
Spontaneity
Is the heart of life.
Immortality
Is the soul of life.

#+END_VERSE


* 36. Embrace, ascend and claim

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:36
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Embrace the grass
Of humility.
Ascend the hill
Of divinity.
Claim the sun
Of immortality.

#+END_VERSE


* 37. You certainly can

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:37
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You desire temptation.
How can you deserve
My Compassion-Light?
You desire aspiration.
How can you not deserve
My Perfection-Height?
You can,
You certainly can.

#+END_VERSE


* 38. Four lives

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:38
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My society-life
Is proud but empty.
My solitude-life
Is sweet but empty.
My service-life
Is beautiful but empty.
My gratitude-life
Is not only soulful but also fruitful.

#+END_VERSE


* 39. Earth-life, heaven-life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:39
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My earth-life
Is the oblivion of human love.
My Heaven-life
Is the remembrance
Of God's blossoming Light
And
Immortality's Delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 40. What we have

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:40
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What you have
Is opinion-flood.
What he has
Is conviction-sea.
What I have
Is realisation-flame.
What God has
Is Perfection-Sun.

#+END_VERSE


* 41. Gratitude

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:41
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Gratitude
Is my friend;
Therefore
Heaven-pride I have,
God-Smile I am.

#+END_VERSE


* 42. He loved

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:42
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He loved himself;
Therefore
He was compelled to live
In the cave of Eternity.
He loved humanity;
Therefore
He was invited to live
In the house of Eternity.

#+END_VERSE


* 43. Tomorrow I shall believe

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:43
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
I believed in God-Compassion.
Today
I believe in earth-aspiration.
Tomorrow
I shall believe
In my life-perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 44. No humour, no rumour

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:44
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There is no humour
In hell.
There is no rumour
In Heaven.
There is no power
On earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 45. He emptied his earth-glory

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:45
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He emptied his earth-glory
Before
His God-realisation.
He emptied his God-realisation
Before
His God-manifestation.

#+END_VERSE


* 46. False alarm

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:46
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

False alarms of love
Ruined
The beginning of his life.
False hope of love
Is ruining
The evening of his life.

#+END_VERSE


* 47. Three enemies

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:47

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I had an enemy:
Ignorance.
I have an enemy:
Doubt.
I shall have an enemy:
Ingratitude.

#+END_VERSE


* 48. O dream of my joy

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:48
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O dream of my joy,
I needed you.
O joy of my dream,
Earth needs you.

#+END_VERSE


* 49. Hope

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:49
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I hoped to be
My Pilot's Heart;
I unmistakably hoped.
I hope to see
My Pilot's Face;
I unreservedly hope.
I shall hope to touch
My Pilot's Feet;
I shall unconditionally hope.

#+END_VERSE


* 50. Found among Light-thieves

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: ESH:50
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
I was found
Among Light-thieves.
Today
I am found
Among Light-distributors.
Tomorrow
I shall be found
Among Light-owners,
The real owners.

#+END_VERSE
