* 1 My Mind ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3282
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind
Does not like duty
At all.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3283
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart
Not only loves duty
But also is eager
To have every day
A newly added duty.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3284

  :END:

Are always found together.

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3285
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

Love God and please God.

  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3286

The mind, the vital and the body

To the heart.
