* 1.  Reject and accept

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:1
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God, I am rejecting man.
Why?
Because he has the same
Weaknesses that I have.

Man, I am accepting God.
Why?
Because He has the same
Perfection that I have.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. Mutual support

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:2
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God and I support each other soulfully.
I support God
With my adoration-leaf.
God supports me
With His Compassion-Tree.

God and I support each other unerringly.
I support God
With my devotion drop.
God supports me
With His Illumination-Sea.

#+END_VERSE


* 3. Completion

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:3
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To complete my earth-life
I shall become surrender-delight.

To complete my Heaven-life
I shall become silence-light.

#+END_VERSE


* 4. What I see in them

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:4
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You ask me
What I can possibly see
In man.
I see in man
Eternity's hope-river.

You ask me
What I can possibly see
In God.
I see in God
Immortality's assurance-sea.

#+END_VERSE


* 5. The ideal man

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:5
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He is the ideal man
In his own right.
In the outer world
He sees himself as he is.
In the inner world
He enjoys himself as he is.

#+END_VERSE


* 6. His own world

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:6
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Unlike others
He has only two
Theoretical problems:
Nobody cares for him;
God does not love him.

Unlike others
He has only two
Practical problems:
Since he does not
Belong either to this world
Or to any world,
He needs a world created by himself
And not those
Already created by God.

#+END_VERSE


* 7. Decide spontaneously

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:7
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Decide spontaneously!
Make an immediate decision!
The beauty of the world-body-life
Is dancing before you.
The divinity of the soul-goal-light
Is crying for you.
Make your own choice,
But delay not.

#+END_VERSE


* 8. God agrees with me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:8
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You say
I always drive others
And on rare occasions myself
Too hard.
I do not agree with you.
Neither does my God.

He says
I am always demanding,
I am always domineering.
I do not agree with him.
Neither does my God.

I say
I love God and God alone.
You do not agree with me.
He does not agree with me.
But my God does;
Therefore
He is my Eternity's All
And not you, not he.

#+END_VERSE


* 9. What you love and enjoy

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:9
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You say
You love and enjoy
Patience-light.
But I clearly see
What you actually
Love and enjoy.
You love sleeping,
You enjoy sleeping
In the lap of procrastination-night.

#+END_VERSE


* 10. Charming

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:10
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are not charming.
Why?
Because you lead
An intellectualised life.

He is not charming.
Why?
Because he leads
A complicated life.

I am charming.
Why?
Because I lead
God's Love-Reality life.

#+END_VERSE


* 11. A great man, a good man

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:11
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, who is a great man?
"Son, he who initiates
Is unfortunately a great man."

Lord, who is a good man?
"Son, he who executes
Is fortunately a good man."

#+END_VERSE


* 12. Can he ever be good?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:12
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He is great.
He loves God
At others' expense.

He is great.
He reveals God
At others' expense.

He is great.
He manifests God
At others' expense.

Can he ever be good?
Certainly he can.
But when?
Only when he totally
Unlearns his mind-inventions.

#+END_VERSE


* 13. God desperately needs him

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:13
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He takes life
Philosophically;
Therefore
God likes him.

He smiles at the world
Unhurriedly;
Therefore
God loves him.

He accepts the world-calamities
Eternally;
Therefore
God desperately needs him.

#+END_VERSE


* 14. Relax!

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:14
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are nervous
Because
You are restless.

You are restless
Because
You are nervous.

Relax, God is coming to you
With His decision-making Vision.
Relax, you can easily go to God
With your tension-frustration capacity
And your suppression-aggression capacity.
Try, you easily can.

#+END_VERSE


* 15. You have succeeded

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:15
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You have succeeded
But others have failed.
Do you know why?
Unlike others,
You have not pressured God,
And you do not love yourself
As much as you love God.

#+END_VERSE


* 16. We love you and need you

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:16
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Don't waste time;
Don't exploit opportunity.
God the Dreamer loves you;
God the Lover needs you.
I love you and need you too.
I love you
Because you are going to be pure.
I need you
Because you are going to be sure.

#+END_VERSE


* 17. Regression

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:17
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
I was a spectator
At life's drama.

Today
I have developed envy.

Tomorrow
I shall die with world-futility
And
Sleepless stupidity.

#+END_VERSE


* 18. Your choice

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:18
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Tolerance
Is your choice;
Therefore
Your heart is satisfaction-sky.

Forgiveness
Is your choice;
Therefore
Your life is perfection-sun.

#+END_VERSE


* 19. You know what to do

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:19
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You know what to do.
You wait and watch God
Drinking His Nectar-Sea.

You know what to do.
You admire and adore
God's capacity.

You know what to do.
When God asks you
To drink with Him,
You always accept His invitation;
You never decline.

You know what to do.
Therefore
You are God's first choice;
You are God's best voice.

#+END_VERSE


* 20. Our respective roles

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:20
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, let us fulfil
Our respective roles.
You be
My decision-maker
In Heaven
And let me be
Your best perfection-builder
On earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 21. My world and God's world

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:21
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Father, do organise
My shapeless world.

Son, do feed
My hungry world.

Father, my world
Needs You
Desperately.

Son, My world
Loves you
Unreservedly.

#+END_VERSE


* 22. Can and can't

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:22
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, I am affected.
Can You not forgive me?
Try, You certainly can.

Lord, I am suffering.
Can You ignore me?
Try, You invariably can't.

#+END_VERSE


* 23. He avoids confrontations

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:23
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He avoids confrontations
Not because he is weak,
Not because he is wrong,
But because he knows
That human arguments
Are worse than futility itself.

#+END_VERSE


* 24. My future life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:24
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
I was confidence-life.
Today
I am action-life.
Tomorrow
I shall be either perfection-life
Or
Total frustration-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 25. God's Vision-Promise

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:25
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Heaven-confidence
I had.

Earth-hesitation
I have.

God-Compassion
I shall have.

God-Perfection
I shall reveal.

God's Vision-Promise
I eternally am.

#+END_VERSE


* 26. They tell the truth

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:26
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEY TELL THE TRUTH

My American friend
Tells me how great he is.

My Indian friend
Tells me how pure he is.

My Canadian friend
Tells me how tolerant he is.

My English friend
Tells me how intelligent he is.

My Scottish friend
Tells me how friendly he is.

My Puerto Rican friend
Tells me how soft he is.

My Irish friend
Tells me how energetic he is.

My Swedish friend
Tells me how visionary he is.

My Swiss friend
Tells me how solid he is.

My Italian friend
Tells me how sweet he is.

My French friend
Tells me how polite he is.

My Japanese friend
Tells me how sincere he is.

Needless to say, they all
Tell the truth.
Of course, their own version
Of the truth.

#+END_VERSE


* 27. My secret life, my open life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:27
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You want to know
About my life.
I tell you secretly:
I am a big nothing.
A beggar desires
To be a chooser.

I tell you openly
Mine is a life
Of eternal sacrifice
For the Truth
Of the ever-transcending Beyond.

#+END_VERSE


* 28. One thing I shall never know

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:28
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I know where I am going.
I know what my goal is.
I know who my God is.
But one thing
I shall never know:
If God really needs
My service-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 29. As they see you

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:29
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You do not see yourself
As Heaven sees you.
Heaven sees you
As a God-promise.

You do not see yourself
As earth sees you.
Earth sees you
As a God-failure.

You do not see yourself
As God sees you.
God sees you
As another God.

#+END_VERSE


* 30. Don't go to extremes

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:30
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Don't go to extremes.
A cave-life
Is not meant for you.

Don't go to extremes.
A pleasure-life
Is not meant for you.

What is meant for you
Is God-discovery
In self-mastery.

#+END_VERSE


* 31. Divine friends

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:31
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

An unhurried mind
And
An unworried heart
Are divine friends.
I always enjoy
Their soulful company.

A surrendered life
And
A satisfied soul
Are divine friends
I always enjoy
Their fruitful company.

#+END_VERSE


* 32. I need

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:32
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Patience I need
To feed the earth-body in me.

Speed I need
To feed the Heaven-soul in me.

Surrender I need
To feed the God-Eye in me.

#+END_VERSE


* 33. Never leave me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:33
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Faith, my faith,
Never leave me.
You know, without you
I am a rudderless boat.

Surrender, my surrender,
Never leave me.
You know, without you
I shall reach a goalless shore.

#+END_VERSE


* 34. The life you live

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:34
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas, yesterday you lived
A superficial earth-pleasure life.

Today you are living
A non-essential darkness-pressure life.

Tomorrow you will try to live
The most essential God-Treasure life.

#+END_VERSE


* 35. He gives everything away

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:35
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He gives everything away
Save and except one thing:
Advice.

He accepts everything from others
Save and except one thing:
Admiration.

He feels his advice will be
Both meaningless and fruitless.
He feels others' admiration will be
Worse than useless.

#+END_VERSE


* 36. Our dreams

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:36
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You belong to earth;
Therefore
You dream of fame and glory.

He belongs to Heaven;
Therefore
He dreams of love and service.

I belong to God;
Therefore
I dream of compassion and perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 37. Success and progress

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:37
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Move forward!
Movement is success,
Movement is progress
Success you want;
Progress you need.

Success is your assessment
Of your dormant and bound life.
Progress is God's assessment
Of your awakened and illumined life.

#+END_VERSE


* 38. I am happy

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:38
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am happy
Because
My body is obedience.

I am happy
Because
My vital is confidence.

I am happy
Because
My mind is concentration.

I am happy
Because
My heart is dedication.

I am happy
Because
My soul is satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 39. A disciplined life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:39
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A disciplined life
Is a perfect life.
A perfect life
Is a God-blossoming dream,
An earth-nourishing reality
And
A Heaven-manifesting divinity.

#+END_VERSE


* 40. Now that I am trying

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:40
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I was aloof,
God forgave me.

When I was reserved,
God forgave me.

When I was cynical,
God forgave me.

When I was skeptical,
God forgave me.

Now that I am trying,
Trying to be good and divine,
God is examining me.
Alas, alas,
What kind of fate I have!

#+END_VERSE


* 41. How to end conflicts

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:41
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You want to end conflicts
Through compromises.
What a fool you are!

He wants to end conflicts
Through brutal physical strength
What a fool he is!

I want to end conflicts
Through helpless surrender.
What a fool I am!

There is only one way to end conflicts:
Love the world before you,
Love the world around you,
Soulfully and unreservedly.

#+END_VERSE


42. It amazes me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:42
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

It amazes me
That you never offend anybody.

It amazes me
That he never dislikes anybody.

It amazes me
That I never need anybody.

#+END_VERSE


* 43. Favours

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:43
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Why do I not like you?
I do not like you
Because
You ask favours.

Why do I not like him?
I do not like him
Because
He grants favours.

Why do I not like myself?
I do not like myself
Because
I do not know what favours
Actually mean.
Who needs favours?
Not I, definitely not I!

#+END_VERSE


* 44. From surprise to surprise

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:44
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My earth-ignorance leads Heaven
From one painful surprise to another.

My Heaven-light leads earth
From one cheerful surprise to another.

My God-love leads my life
From one fruitful surprise to another.

#+END_VERSE


* 45. Changes

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:45
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Changes, even for the better,
You unreservedly hate.

Changes, even for the worse,
He sincerely loves.

Changes, even for a second,
I desperately need.

#+END_VERSE


* 46. I shall become

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:46
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Heaven-dreamer I was,
I really was.

Earth-doer I am,
I truly am.

God-lover I shall become,
Unerringly I shall become.

#+END_VERSE


* 47. Our respective knowledge

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:47
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God knows how to work;
I know how to play.
Neither is inferior.

God knows how to give;
I know how to receive.
Neither is superior.

#+END_VERSE


* 48. The birth of self-destruction

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:48
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A corrupted mind
Is the birth of self-deception.

A corrupted heart
Is the birth of self-confusion.

A corrupted life
Is the birth of self-destruction.

#+END_VERSE


* 49. They must be endured

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:49
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What cannot be cured
Must be endured:
Your lack of purity-vital.

What cannot be cured
Must be endured:
His lack of sincerity-mind.

What cannot be cured
Must be endured:
My lack of security-heart.

What cannot be cured
Must be endured:
The world's lack of spirituality-body.

#+END_VERSE


* 50. Easy and difficult

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: GDVP:50
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Easy to lead!
Therefore
I lead my heart.

Easy to drive!
Therefore
I drive my mind.

But difficult to reach!
Therefore
I cry;
Therefore
I sigh.

#+END_VERSE
