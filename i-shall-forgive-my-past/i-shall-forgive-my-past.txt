  :DATE: None
  :cite-key: FMP:1

  :DATE: None
  :cite-key: FMP:2
  :END:

#+BEGIN_VERSE

     Of beauty’s day,

     Of your ruthless might.

* O World of Peace

  :LANGUAGE: en
  :DATE: None
  :cite-key: FMP:4
  :END:

#+BEGIN_VERSE

O world of peace,
O world of light,
How long shall I
With darkness fight?
How long my heart
Shall cry in vain?
This eyeless world
Has nothing but pain.

#+END_VERSE


* O Lightless Eyes of Mine

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: song
  :LANGUAGE: en
  :DATE: None
  :cite-key: FMP:5
  :END:

#+BEGIN_VERSE

O lightless eyes of mine,
I say, starve not, starve not, just cry.
The golden hour shall dawn
In the feeble mind of desert dry.
Your hope-life shall not die,
Your doleful fate is bound to change.
Your cry shall fly and fly
To the cosmic Spirit’s Vision-range.

#+END_VERSE
