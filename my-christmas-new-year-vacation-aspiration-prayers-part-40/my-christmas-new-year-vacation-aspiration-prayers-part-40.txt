* 1 God’s Heart-Doors ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2994
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s Heart-Doors
Do not believe in
Even one single lock.

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2995

  :END:

Dazzles.
The heart’s aspiration
Radiates.

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2996
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

I am not a beginner

In the world-service-game.

#+END_VERSE

  :CITE-KEY: APR:2997

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2998

I shall not succeed
In any field of God-manifestation
Here on earth.
