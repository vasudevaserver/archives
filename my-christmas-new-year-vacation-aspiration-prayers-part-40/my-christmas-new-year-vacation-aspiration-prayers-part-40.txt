* 1 God’s Heart-Doors ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3233
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s Heart-Doors
Do not believe in
Even one single lock.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 The Mind’s Brilliance ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3234
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The mind’s brilliance
Dazzles.
The heart’s aspiration
Radiates.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3235
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To my greatest joy,
I am not a beginner
But an adept
In the world-service-game.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3236
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only to the sleepless self-givers
God gives the golden opportunity
To shoulder His Responsibilities.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 I Need Help ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3237
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I need help,
And I get inner Help from God
And outer help from man.
Left to myself,
I shall not succeed
In any field of God-manifestation
Here on earth.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3238
  :END:

And every evening
I pray to be a foot soldier
Of my Lord Supreme.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3239
  :END:

The success of the world.
The heart embraces
The progress of the world.

#+END_VERSE

A perfect perfection-blossom.
