  :GENRE:  poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: AP:16301

#+BEGIN_VERSE

O Lord Supreme,
May we say the right thing,
May we do the right thing
And may we become the right thing.
The right thing for us to say:
"We love You, God."
The right thing for us to do:
Serve God.
The right thing for us to become:
God's perfect children.

#+END_VERSE
