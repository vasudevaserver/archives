  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem

Your heart will be played below, above.

  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:6

  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:7
  :KEYWORDS:

#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you delay, if you delay,
Your heart, your breath, your all will stray.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 My Mind Travels Along ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:8
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind travels along
The bumpy road of thought.
Alas, my soulful breath
By Titan's frown is caught.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 Be God's Sleepless Willingness-Toy....

  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:9
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be God's sleepless willingness-toy.
For you His Heart's Marathon-Joy.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 God Has Revived My Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:10
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God has revived my heart
With purity-oxygen.
His Vision-Splendour-Sky
My heart can now open.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 O Worthless, Shoreless Chattering Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:11
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O worthless, shoreless chattering mind,
Silence-fulness never will you find..

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Heaven Is Heaven's ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:12
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Heaven is Heaven's
Descending Compassion-Dreams,
And earth is earth's
Ascending perfection-schemes.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Be Calm, Be Calm, My...

  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:13
  :KEYWORDS:

#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be calm, be calm, my mind,
My monkey-mind, be calm.
Our Lord Himself will strike
Your supreme Victory-Drum.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 My Heart Is My Life's God-Hunger-Cry....

  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:14
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart is my life's God-hunger-cry.
Perfection-banner-delight I fly.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 Teeming Doubt-Clouds ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MSJ:15
  :KEYWORDS:

Have blinded my mind.
My rainbow-heart-joy
Nowhere I find.
