* 301 In You I See...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:301
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

IN YOU I SEE

O sobbing sighs
Of the morning star,
In you I see the Beauty
Of the Lord Supreme.

O blossoming smile
Of the morning sun,
In you I feel the Duty
Of the Lord Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 302 The Green Vast Fields...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:302
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE GREEN VAST FIELDS

The green vast fields
Of my aspiring heart
Are waiting for God's Hour
In my surrendering heart of night.

The blue vast skies
Of my revealing soul
Are offering the power of love
In the fulfilling life of Light.

#+END_VERSE


* 303 My Consciousness-Tree...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:303
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY CONSCIOUSNESS-TREE

Yesterday I loved
My sapling consciousness-tree
Because
It embodied my journey's start.

Today I love
My youngling consciousness-tree
Because
It reveals my journey's speed.

Tomorrow I shall love
My climbing, glowing and spreading consciousness-tree
Because
It will fulfil my journey's promise.

#+END_VERSE


* 304 Silence-Face, Forgiveness-Smile...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:304
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SILENCE-FACE, FORGIVENESS-SMILE

Life is but a day.
Therefore
I try to finish
All my aspiring
And
All my loving
In the short span
Of one single day.

Life is but Eternity,
Therefore
I sleep and dream,
I sing and dance
And
Dance and sing
In the Silence-Face, Forgiveness-Smile
Of the birthless and deathless Day.

#+END_VERSE


* 305 No More!...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:305
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

NO MORE!

I was my heart's
Sleeping rocks.

I was my heart's
Brooding clouds.

I was my heart's
Burning anguish.

I was my heart's
Sinking heights.

But no more!
I repeat:
No more!
Again I repeat:
No more, No more!

#+END_VERSE


* 306 I Breathe In, I Breathe Out...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:306
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I BREATHE IN, I BREATHE OUT

Quickly and lovingly
Smilingly and triumphantly
I breathe in
The blossoming breath
Of my golden morn.

Quickly and steadily
Helplessly and unreservedly
I breathe out
The fading breath
Of my folding eve.

#+END_VERSE


* 307 The Music Of The Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:307
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE MUSIC OF THE HEART

The music of the heart
Is the silence-light
Of Perfection-height.
The aspiration-world has discovered
This truth sublime.

The music of the vital
Is the elephant-ignorance
Of destruction-night.
The desire-world shall discover
This truth infallible.

#+END_VERSE


* 308 Not Born For Death...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:308
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

NOT BORN FOR DEATH

Not one but three discoveries supreme
I have already made:

My heart of redolent gratitude-blossoms
Was not born for death.

My soul of perfect oneness-vision
Was not born for death.

My life of constant manifestation-light
Was not born for death.

#+END_VERSE

MY EYES NEED

My closed eyes
Need
God the Beauty.

God the Duty.

My half-open and
half-closed eyes
Need
God the Sovereignty.

#+END_VERSE


* 310 Vital Night And Psychic Light...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:310
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

VITAL NIGHT AND PSYCHIC LIGHT

When my vital night
Replaces my psychic light,
I die before I sink.

When my psychic light
Replaces my vital night,
I fly before I try.

#+END_VERSE


* 311 I Love...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:311
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I LOVE

I love the dancing
Of teeming clouds
Because
Of their thunder-power.

I love the singing
Of passing clouds
Because
Of their diamond-wisdom.

I love the dreaming
Of weeping clouds
Because
Of their sacrifice-soul.

#+END_VERSE


* 312 When I Woke Up...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:312
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHEN I WOKE UP

Before I went to sleep
I saw my death's dateless night.

During my sleep
I saw my life's endless light.

When I woke up
I found Mother Earth's Compassion
In my aspiration-heart,
And Father Heaven's Perfection
Around my dedication-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 313 Wait A Minute, God, Please!...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:313
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WAIT A MINUTE, GOD, PLEASE!

Wait a minute, God, please!
I have more to tell You
About my inner pangs.

Wait a minute, God, please!
I have more to tell You
About my outer triumphs.

Wait a minute, God, please!
I have more to tell You
About my surrender-light.

#+END_VERSE

#+BEGIN_VERSE

HIS IS THE SOUL THAT LOVES

His is the vital that loves
The imperious waves.

The gracious sun.

His is the soul that loves
The precious moon.

#+END_VERSE


* 315 A Good Price...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:315
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A GOOD PRICE

The voice of a singing rivulet
I had.
I sold it at a very reasonable price.

The voice of smashing thunder
I have.
I am begging ignorance to buy it from me.

The voice of the liberating God
I shall have.
I shall give it to Mother Earth
At a good price -
Free,
Absolutely free.

#+END_VERSE

#+BEGIN_VERSE

O PILGRIM SOUL

O pilgrim soul,
Divinity
Is in your untiring feet.

O pilgrim soul,
Infinity
Is in your unblinking eyes.

O pilgrim soul,
Eternity
Is in your unbending head.

O pilgrim soul,
Immortality
Is in your unthinking heart.

#+END_VERSE

#+BEGIN_VERSE

I AM TELLING YOU THE TRUTH

The power of poise-sky
I had.
I am telling you the truth.

I am telling you the truth.

The power of compassion-moon
I shall have.
I am telling you the truth.

#+END_VERSE


* 318 They Shall Go And Return...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:318
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEY SHALL GO AND RETURN

My heart shall go to Heaven
And return.
My heart shall return with more
Compassion-power.

My mind shall go to Heaven
And return.
My mind shall return with more
Perfection-light.

My vital shall go to Heaven
And return.
My vital shall return with more
Justice-height.

My body shall go to Heaven
And return.
My body shall return with more
Patience-length.

#+END_VERSE


* 319 They Do Not Want Me...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:319
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEY DO NOT WANT ME

I have wasted my time;
Therefore
Bliss does not want me.

I have neglected my duty;
Therefore
Peace does not want me.

I have rejected truth;
Therefore
Light does not want me.

#+END_VERSE


* 320 Enough!...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:320
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

ENOUGH!

O hope, O eyeless hope,
Cling not.
Enough of your tenacity!

O reality, O vast reality,
Sleep not.
Enough of your cruelty!

O God, O mighty God,
Demand not.
Enough of Your authority!

#+END_VERSE


* 321 My Silver Key, My Golden Key...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:321
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY SILVER KEY, MY GOLDEN KEY

My silver key
Opens the door
Of my wakeful and tearful heart
For God to come in
And my ignorance to go out.

My golden key
Opens the door
Of God's Nectar-Smile
For me to talk to God,
To sit on His Throne,
To become His Crown.

#+END_VERSE


* 322 Is It Fair?...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:322
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

IS IT FAIR?

God, is it fair
That I am doomed to stay
With my lifeless body?

God, is it fair
That I am doomed to walk
With my cheerless vital?

God, is it fair
That I am doomed to think
With my thoughtless mind?

God, is it fair
That I am doomed to dance
With my soulless heart?

God, is it fair
That I am doomed to dream
With my goalless soul?

#+END_VERSE


* 323 God Will Be Arriving Soon...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:323
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD WILL BE ARRIVING SOON

O my unclean body,
Be careful.
God the Purity
Will be arriving soon.

O my unruly vital,
Be careful.
God the Power
Will be arriving soon.

O my unthinking mind,
Be careful.
God the Light
Will be arriving soon.

O my unaspiring heart,
Be careful.
God the uncompromising King
Will be arriving soon.

#+END_VERSE

I AM A CHILD OF GRACE

I am a child of Grace
When I pray soulfully.

When I meditate silently.

I am a child of Delight
When I contemplate selflessly.

* 325 The Supreme Truth...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:325
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE SUPREME TRUTH

God is a tiny insect.
This is a strange truth.

God is a huge elephant.
This is a normal truth.

God is a devouring tiger.
This is a peculiar truth.

God is a speedy deer.
This is an infallible truth.

God is a loving cow.
This is the supreme Truth.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem

THEY DECLARE

Silently
My soul declares
The Vision of the Supreme.

Tenderly
My heart declares
The Compassion of the Supreme.

Clearly
My mind declares
The Light of the Supreme.

Boldly
My vital declares
The Power of the Supreme.

Unerringly
My body declares
The Love of the Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 329 Do You Know?...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:329
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

DO YOU KNOW?

Do you know that my heart
Is a sacred island?
Perhaps you know.

Do you know that my mind
Is a secret island?
Perhaps you know.

Do you know that my vital
Is an ignored island?
Perhaps you know.

Do you know that my body
Is a treasure-island?
Ah, you didn't know?

Then learn it once and for all.

#+END_VERSE


* 330 I Believe...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:330
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I BELIEVE

I whole-heartedly believe
God is always great and aloof.

I half-heartedly believe
God is always good and kind.

With some difficulty I believe
God cares for me.

With no difficulty I believe
God deserves my criticism.

#+END_VERSE


* 331 My Ancient Dream...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:331
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY ANCIENT DREAM

O Lord Supreme,
Teach my mind
How to sleep without a futile dream.
O Lord Supreme,
Teach my heart
How to regain my ancient dream -
The dream that revealed
You and I are one,
The dream that distributed
Our respective tasks:
You the Monarch of Heaven,
And I the Monarch of Earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 332 The Thunder...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:332
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE THUNDER

The thunder of the green earth
Teaches my life.

The thunder of the blue sky
Examines my life.

The thunder of the gold Heaven
Declares my triumphant results.

The thunder of the black hell
Desires my achievement-treasure.

#+END_VERSE


* 333 I Wish To Remain A Practical Man...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:333
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I WISH TO REMAIN A PRACTICAL MAN

I love like God.
Therefore
I am an equal partner
Of God the Lover.

I think like God.
Therefore
I am an equal partner
Of God the Thinker.

I dream like God.
Therefore
I am an equal partner
Of God the Dreamer.

But
I act not like God the Doer,
Because
I wish to remain
A practical man.

#+END_VERSE

* 342 When I Sang The Silence-Song...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:342
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHEN I SANG THE SILENCE-SONG

When God sang the sound-song,
I told Him that was not fair:
He was singing my song.

When I sang the silence-song,
God happily and unconditionally
Told me that
Since I had learned His song
He could peacefully retire.

#+END_VERSE


* 343 The Tiniest Drop...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:343
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE TINIEST DROP

The curse of Time
I can easily endure,
The strokes of the Almighty
I can easily brave.
But
The tiniest drop of ignorance-sea
Can easily destroy
The blue-gold dreams of my heart
And
The green-red realities of my life.

#+END_VERSE

* 350 I Saw The Burial...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: WL:350
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I SAW THE BURIAL

I saw the burial of my body.
I laughed and laughed.

I saw the burial of my vital.
I laughed and laughed.

I saw the burial of my mind.
I laughed and laughed.

I saw the burial of my heart.
I cried and cried
Because
What I really had,
What I really claimed
As my own,
Was my sole tiny heart.

#+END_VERSE
