* 1 I Meditate ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MST:1
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I meditate
So that when
"The Hour of God"
Strikes I shall not
Be late in my
Unconditional
Surrender-examination.

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MST:2

Cannot complicate
My life.
