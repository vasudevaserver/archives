/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Qingdao, China in December 2004./

/24 December 2004/

/Qingdao, China/



* 1 May My Life Be A Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2918
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

May my life be a heart
Of humility-flower.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 My Lord ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2919
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
How can we ever deserve
Your Love,
How can we?

#+END_VERSE


* 3 My Lord ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2920
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
You do not need me
For anything,
But to give me importance
In Your Mission,
You make me feel
That I am indispensable.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Not Through Mere Words ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2921
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Not through mere words,
But by constant selfless actions
I must please my Lord.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 My Lord Wants Me To Communicate ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2922
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord wants me to communicate
With my brothers and sisters
Of the world
With tremendous affection,
Love and concern.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Every Day ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2923
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
A new lotus petal
Is blossoming
In my heart of aspiration.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 Every Day ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2924
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must give a most beautiful smile
To my Beloved Supreme,
And also receive from Him
His most beautiful Smile.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2925
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
Can be a golden opportunity
To transform my mind
And purify my life.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: prayer
