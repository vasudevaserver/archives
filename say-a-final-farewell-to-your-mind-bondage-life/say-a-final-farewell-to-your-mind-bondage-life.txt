* 1 Stop Your Friendship...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Stop your friendship
With your mind-fantasies
If you want to enjoy
Your heart-aspiration-ecstasies.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 It Is High Time To Revolt...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

It is high time to revolt
Against the reign
Of your tyrannical and ferocious
Tiger-mind!

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Every Time I Soulfully Pray, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every time I soulfully pray,
I see the blossoming Light
Of God's sacred Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 I Am So Grateful To My Soul,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so grateful to my soul,
For it is helping me
So compassionately
To journey towards
Infinity's Peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 O Seeker, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O seeker,
What else is sheer stupidity
If not the greater love you have
For your mind
Than for your heart?

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Each Time I Nourish A Good Thought,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each time I nourish a good thought,
I give a surprising victory
To my aspiration-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 Hesitation And Indecision...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Hesitation and indecision
Ruthlessly plague
Even sincere minds
And pure hearts.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Once We Enter Into The Spiritual Life,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Once we enter into the spiritual life,
Our possibility-fulfilment-life
Becomes infinitely easier.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 My God-Aspiration-Hope...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My God-aspiration-hope
Can once more be rekindled
Only by God's Compassion-Eye-Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 I Shall Not Allow My Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I shall not allow my life
Anymore to remain
An undisciplined blunder.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 I Find No Truth...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I find no truth
In the curiosity
Of my mind's God-search.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 O My Mind, Suspect Nothing...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind, suspect nothing
If happiness is what you want.
O my heart, fear nothing
If happiness is what you want.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Idleness Wants To Be...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Idleness wants to be
A world-possessor
Without being
A self-mover.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 Finally I Have...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Finally I have
The indomitable courage
To jump off my mind's
Jealousy-train.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 If We Ignore God The Pilot, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If we ignore God the Pilot,
How will we be able to enjoy
His Boat Ride?

#+END_VERSE


* 16 My Heart Flies...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart flies
Higher than the highest
When I lose considerably
My mind's desire-pounds.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 A Disciplined Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A disciplined life
Is the best freedom-enjoyer
In the inner world.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 O My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind,
Why do you allow yourself
To suffer from
Desire-bound thought-disease?

#+END_VERSE


* 19 How Can We Ever...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

How can we ever
Make God happy
If we do not feed
Our own God-hunger?

#+END_VERSE


* 20 If You Belittle...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you belittle
Others' gratitude-tears,
God-Satisfaction-Smiles
Will avoid you.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 Be Not Satisfied...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be not satisfied
Only with your outer self.
Explore your teeming inner selves.
Only then can you ever
Be truly fulfilled.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 My Lord, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
I need Your very special Blessings
To cancel once and for all
All my world-complaints.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 God Wants...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God wants
My life-surrender-sea
And not my surrender-drop
To satisfy Himself.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 When You Most Powerfully...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When you most powerfully
Say a final farewell
To your mind's bondage-life,
Your soul will treasure you
And utilise you
In God's own Way.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 Every Day I Wash...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day I wash
My Lord's Compassion-Feet
With my heart's devotion-tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 Is There Anybody Who Does Not Think...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Is there anybody who does not think
That his problems are the worst
And that he is hopelessly drowning
In the sea of helplessness?

#+END_VERSE


* 27 You Cannot Irritate God, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You cannot irritate God,
Even with your shameless impatience
And empty receptivity.
You just delay God's Arrival!

#+END_VERSE


* 28 At Every Moment...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

At every moment
God's Love grows and glows
Inside silence-hearts.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 If You Do Not Forgive And Forget...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you do not forgive and forget
The world's dreadful injustice,
You will not be able
To make the progress
That you want.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 No More Do I Carry...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No more do I carry
My power-hungry past.
I live only in
My oneness-hungry present.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 On The Strength Of...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

On the strength of
My unconditional surrender,
I feel that to please God
Every step of the way
Is not a difficult task.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 Ignorance Is Obscure....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ignorance is obscure.
Ignorance is impure.
Ignorance is death-preparation.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 If You Live In The Soul, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you live in the soul,
With the soul
And for the soul,
You have impeccable God-credentials.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 A Determination-Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A determination-mind
And an aspiration-heart
Are treasured by God's divine Pride.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 Today You Are...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Today you are
A disobedience-king.
Tomorrow you will be forced to be
The possessor of a flood of tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 When I See God, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I see God,
I always make the same request:
That He visit me
As often as possible.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 From The Outer World...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

From the outer world
You may at times escape.
But from the inner world
You can never escape,
Never!

#+END_VERSE


* 38 My Lord Supreme Comes...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Supreme comes
When He is prayerfully needed
And not when He is
Whimsically called.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 Ignorance Can Neither...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ignorance can neither
Know nor understand
Why it eventually becomes
A total failure.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 May My Love Become A Strong Point,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

May my love become a strong point,
My devotion a stronger point
And my surrender the strongest point
In my aspiration-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 God Looks Into Every Corner...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God looks into every corner
Of my heart-room
To see if there are
Aspiration-flames available.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 Be Proud Of Your Patience....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be proud of your patience.
Your patience will ultimately arrive
At the Goal
And make God exceedingly happy.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 My Heart Knows...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart knows
Only one way:
The way of unconditional surrender.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 A Desire-Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A desire-mind
Is
An obscurity-insincerity-slave.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 O My Mind, "No!"...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind, "No!"
A thousand times, "No!"
To your desire-hunger-fulfilment.

#+END_VERSE


* 46 Self-Worshippers Eventually...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Self-worshippers eventually
Cry and cry.
God-worshippers unmistakably
Fly and fly.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 Strength And Tolerance...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Strength and tolerance
Must be eternal partners
To be victorious in life.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 When We Do Something Good...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When we do something good
And when we love
The world of self-giving,
God is infinitely more pleased
Than we can ever imagine.

#+END_VERSE


* 49 If You Can Dare To Laugh At...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you can dare to laugh at
Your hopeless helplessness,
God will bless you
With His most powerful
And most proud Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 With Every Child...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SFFY:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

With every child
God creates a new hope
And a new promise
For Himself.

#+END_VERSE
