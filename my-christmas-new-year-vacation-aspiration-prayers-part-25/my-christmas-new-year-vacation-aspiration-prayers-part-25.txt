/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Solo and Yogyakarta, Indonesia in December 2003./

/13 December 2003/

/Solo, Indonesia/



* 1 Today I Am Determined ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1868
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Today I am determined
To do everything
In God’s own Way.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 To Have Purity ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1869
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To have purity,
The seeker has to feel
That he is a child
Of the Eternal Now.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 If You Are Searching For God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1870
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are searching for God
With a genuine thirst and hunger,
Then God will without fail
Appear before you.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 My Life Is In Between ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1871
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My life is in between
God’s Compassion-Arms
And His Forgiveness-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 God’s Will Does Not Operate ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1872
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s Will does not operate
In a doubting mind.
God’s Will operates only
In a self-giving life.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 It Is Far Beyond The Capacity ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1873
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

It is far beyond the capacity
Of the mind
To think that God
Is all Forgiveness.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 The Heart Feels ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1874
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The heart feels
That God is sleepless
And unconditional Forgiveness.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Shut Up, My Mind! ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1875
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Shut up, my mind!
My heart and I are ready
To fly
On our aspiration-wings.

#+END_VERSE

  :KEYWORDS:

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1893

#+END_VERSE


* 27 When The World Tells Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1894

  :END:

That God loves the world,
I tell the world,
“I need no proof,
For God has repeatedly told me
That loving
Is the only thing He knows.”

#+END_VERSE
