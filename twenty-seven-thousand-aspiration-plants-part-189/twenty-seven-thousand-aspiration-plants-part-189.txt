  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: AP:18880
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each man is a problem
 Unto himself.
He may solve other problems
But he as a problem
Remains always unsolved
Until a higher Light descends.

#+END_VERSE

  :KEYWORDS:

#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's Compassion wanted me to be
 A member of
The family of achievers.

 A member of
The family of givers.

#+END_VERSE
