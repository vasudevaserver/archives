* This Series Of Rhyming Poems Was Commenced...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: note
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:1e1
  :END:


This series of rhyming poems was commenced on Sunday, 14 June 1998 in New York. The first volume was completed in three days. It is Sri Chinmoy's ardent aspiration to offer 7,000 "Sail My Heartbeat Sail" poems to his Lord Beloved Supreme.



* 1 Sail...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SAIL

Sail my heartbeat sail, sail
To clasp our Lord's Nectar-Mail.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 2 Choice Hour...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

CHOICE HOUR

How can I not love
The beauty of a flower!
How can I not rise
At my sweet Lord's choice Hour!
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Rainbow-Dream...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

RAINBOW-DREAM

To devour the Dust-Gold-Feet
Of my Lord Supreme
Every morn my heart becomes
A rainbow-dream.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Perfection-Dream...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

PERFECTION-DREAM

To be a chosen child
Of my Lord Supreme,
My crying-smiling heart's
Perfection-dream.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 5 God-Climbing Fire...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD-CLIMBING FIRE

Once more I shall become
My heart's God-climbing fire.
My eyes shall see nowhere
The face and body of mire.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Impurity-Insecurity...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

IMPURITY-INSECURITY

Sleeplessly I keep my mind
Free from impurity-ties.
Breathlessly I keep my heart
Empty of insecurity-cries.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 7 The Right To Choose...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE RIGHT TO CHOOSE

Oh, what I have and what I am,
Am I ready to lose?
If so, my Lord Supreme tells me:
Him I have the right to choose.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY DREAM

Mine is a birthless
And deathless dream:
To breathe the Heart
Of my Lord Supreme.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

MIND-TEARS VERSUS HEART-SMILES

My newness-mind-tears
Keep me glowing.
My fulness-heart-smiles
Keep me sailing.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 10 My Gratitude-Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY GRATITUDE-HEART

The fragrance
Of my God-gratitude-heart
Has smashed
My mind's ego-pride-dart.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

ECSTASY'S ENDLESS ROAD

I see no more my earth-bound mind's
Despair-gloom-cave.
Ecstasy's endless road my life
Is ready to pave.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:12

NEGATIVITY-MIND

Negativity-mind
In every
Corner of my heart I find.
14 June 1998

* 13 Silence...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SILENCE

From the Silence Unknowable
To the Sound knowable I came
To play and sing and dance
With a tiny, fragile frame,
And build a rainbow-bridge
Between Heaven and earth
For my sweet Supreme to travel
And flood all-where His Birth.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem

  :cite-key: SMHS:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HEART-SMILE

Whom I seek
Is within me.

Heart-Smile to be.
15 June 1998

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HALT

Not my mind-cherishing life,
But my heart-blossoming breath
I need, I love, I treasure
To halt the pride of death.
15 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

EMPTINESS

Empty of heaviness-darkness
My mind, my heart today;
Behold, my soul-ecstasy
And I together play!
15 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

My sports-life

MY TENNIS

My tennis

MY PRAYER

My prayer
Tells me Whom to beg only,
Deep within,
Wide without, sempiternally.
15 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:23

#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY MEDITATION

My meditation
Is compelled by God to be His Voice,
And only Him
To fulfil all-where His express Choice.
15 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:24

#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY ASPIRATION

My aspiration
Tells me I can reach God's Eye,
And also God's Feet
In prayer-tears I can tie.
15 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :cite-key: SMHS:25

#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY DEDICATION

My dedication,
My God-manifestation-bloom
To reserve a seat
In God's hallowed Heart-Room.
15 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 26 My Dear, Sweet Lord...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY DEAR, SWEET LORD

My dear Lord,
How can I live
Without Your Life's Compassion-Eye?
My sweet Lord,
How can I breathe
Without Your Heart's Forgiveness-Sky?
15 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

#+BEGIN_VERSE

FORWARD AND BACKWARD

I look forward
To see my Lord's Face.
I look backward
To garner my disgrace.
16 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

#+BEGIN_VERSE

SOOTHSAYERS AND TRUTH-PRAYERS

16 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

16 June 1998

LIBERATION-SHORE

No relaxation
Until you have reached the Liberation-Shore.

16 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

  :cite-key: SMHS:31

#+BEGIN_VERSE

FAST FORWARD

Your mind's
Ceaseless, worthless worries fast forward
If you
Desire from God His Peace-Award.
16 June 1998

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

IGNORANCE-LIFE-TREE

Certainly we know
God loves the world through you and me.
We needs must fell
Our venomed ignorance-life-tree.
16 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Detachment-Songs...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

DETACHMENT-SONGS

In the morning,
My life and my heart together sing
Detachment-songs
From world-castigation.
In the evening,
My life and my heart together sing
Detachment-songs
From world-ovation.
16 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

SOUL DIVINE AND LIFE HUMAN

Every day
My soul divine
Endlessly flies and flies and flies.
Every day
My life human
Pitifully cries and cries and cries.
16 June 1998

#+END_VERSE

16 June 1998

HIS NECTAR-RAYS

16 June 1998

MIND-MART

INDIFFERENCE-ABSENCE
