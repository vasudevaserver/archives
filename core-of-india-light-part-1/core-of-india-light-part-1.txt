* 1. Nama-Rupa

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Idea And Form, Essence And Substance
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Idea is what I am. Form is what I have. Essence is my God-Silence. Substance is my God-Sound.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. Deva

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: A Self-Luminous And Self-Amorous God-Existence
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A /deva/ is he who is at once a never-changing Reality and an ever-transcending Beauty. He is an experience of God-hunger on earth and a realisation of God-feast in Heaven.

#+END_VERSE


* 3. Ahimsa

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Non-Violence
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What is non-violence? Heaven’s soul-flower and earth’s heart-fragrance.

#+END_VERSE


* 4. Svarajya

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Freedom
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I live in the night of my body, I cry for my ego’s dictatorship. I think that only freedom of my lower nature can fulfil me.

When I live in the light of my soul, I discover that only God-rule over my entire earth-field is my real freedom, my divinity’s eternal freedom.

#+END_VERSE


* 5. Karma

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Action
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A self-giving action is a God-manifesting dream.

A self-indulging action is a Satan-strangling reality.

#+END_VERSE


* 6. Kama

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Body-Pleasure
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Body-pleasure is death-construction and life-destruction, earth’s success-suspension, Heaven’s progress-procrastination and God’s Love-consumption.

#+END_VERSE


* 7. Dharma

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: The Inner Code Of Human Life
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I need only God. He is my Eternity’s All in everything that I say, do and become. Self-discovery is life-recovery. God for God’s sake. I am of God’s eternal Cry. I am for God’s universal Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 8. Tat Tvam Asi

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: That Thou Art.
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Thou art the Silence-Smile-Transcendence. Thou art the Sound-Cry-Immanence.

#+END_VERSE


* 9. Yato Dharmastato Jayah

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Where There Is Dharma, There Is Victory.
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

/Dharma/ is God-invocation and God-acceptance in God’s own Way. Victory is life-perfection and self-transcendence.

#+END_VERSE


* 10. Paramahamsa

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Liberated Soul
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

/Paramahamsa/ is a liberated soul. He is liberated from the meshes of ignorance.

/Paramahamsa/ is a realised soul. He has discovered and realised his conscious and constant oneness with the Absolute Supreme.

/Paramahamsa/ is a peerless Yogi. He is the supreme instrument of God.

/Paramahamsa/ is an Avatar. He is God’s highest and most powerful representative on earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 11. Avidya-Vidya

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Ignorance-Knowledge
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

/Avidya/ is ignorance-desire-world.

/Vidya/ is knowledge-aspiration-world.

/Avidya/ tells us what we are: death’s dole. /Vidya/ tells us what we are: Immortality’s Life-Flow, Infinity’s Love-Light, Eternity’s Silence-Height.

#+END_VERSE


* 12. Purusha

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: The Unmanifest
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

/Purusha/ is the dweller in the body, the form. /Purusha/ is the dweller in the soul, the spirit.

From /Purusha/ in the body we discover the Universal Life. From /Purusha/ in the soul we discover the Transcendental Breath.

#+END_VERSE


* 13. Prakrti

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Mother-Nature
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

/Prakrti/ is Mother-Nature. /Prakrti/ is God the Mother.

/Prakrti’s/ Love-Light is humanity’s emancipation-perfection and Divinity’s Satisfaction-Manifestation.

#+END_VERSE


* 14. Sat-Chit-Ananda

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Existence-Consciousness-Bliss
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Existence is Eternity. Consciousness is Infinity. Bliss is Immortality.

Eternity is the Compassion-Face of the Supreme. Infinity is the Perfection-Smile of the Supreme. Immortality is the Oneness-Light of the Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 15. Abhava

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Out Of Existence
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday I saw God going out of my existence. Today I see ignorance going out of my existence. Tomorrow I shall see God alone in my awakened existence. The day after tomorrow I shall without fail see my unconditionally surrendered presence in God’s immortalising Delight-Existence.

#+END_VERSE


* 16. Abhayam

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Fearlessness
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Man has no fear of the true truth that his body-reality is of God-Consciousness. He has fear because he is not aware of the true truth that his vital-incapacity wants to stay without God-Consciousness. He has fear because he is not aware of the true truth that his mind-concentration is not on God-Consciousness. He has fear because he is not aware of the true truth that his heart-love is not for God-Reality.

#+END_VERSE


* 17. Achala

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Motionless
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If we really need God’s infinite Love, Peace, Light and Delight, then an inner motionless life we must embody and an outer life of dynamic motion we must reveal. How can we do that? We can do it only by meditating on God in divinity and by serving God in humanity.

#+END_VERSE


* 18. Acharya

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Spiritual Teacher
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

An ordinary teacher gives to his students what he has: knowledge. A spiritual Teacher gives to his disciples not only what he has but also what God has and what God is. What the Teacher has for his disciples is concern. What God has for his disciples is Compassion-Light. What God is for his disciples is Perfection-Delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 19. Achynta

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Unfallen
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We want always to remain unfallen from our divine and supreme nature which is God-Oneness in God-Perfection; therefore, we must always remember that we are the hero-warriors of the Supreme for the Supreme. /Achyuta/ is also an epithet of Vishnu or Krishna.

#+END_VERSE


* 20. Adesa

  :PROPERTIES:
  :SUBTITLE: Inner Command
  :GENRE: aphorism
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: CIL:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s inner Command to a supremely sincere seeker is: “My child, declare to the world at large that you are not only of My Transcendental Reality and for My Universal Reality but you are also My eternal partner, a co-sharer of our cosmic Dream-Reality.”

#+END_VERSE
