* The Following Twenty Poems On Confidence Were...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: note
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:1e1
  :END:


The following twenty poems on confidence  were written on 14 December 1981.



* 1 Only A Self-Giving Devotee...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only a self-giving devotee

Can have ecstatic confidence

In the depths of his heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 If We Can Earnestly...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If we can earnestly

And soulfully meditate,

Then we can easily have

Cheerful confidence.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Perfect Confidence Is Not...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Perfect confidence is not

In earth-possession

But in self-illumination.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 To Acquire Victory's Crown, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To acquire victory's crown,

One must cultivate

Resolute confidence

In one's spiritual life.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 If A Seeker Is Not...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If a seeker is not

Continuously blessed

With purity-breath,

Then his life

Will unmistakably betray

Arrogant confidence.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Success On The Outer Plane...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Success on the outer plane

Does not remain a far cry

After the mind has achieved

Solid confidence.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 Before God-Realisation...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Before God-realisation

It is almost an impossible task

To have supreme confidence

In one's aspiration-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Devilish Confidence...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Devilish confidence

And destruction

Are immediate neighbours.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 To Have Complacent Confidence...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To have complacent confidence

Is the beginning

Of one's fast-approaching perdition

In one's inner life.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 If You Are A Good Seeker, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are a good seeker,

Then you will try

To earn confidence

In the depths

Of your aspiring heart

And not enjoy confidence

In your obscure and impure vital.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 A Disciplined Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A disciplined life

Can unmistakably

Register confidence

In the searching mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 When The Clever Mind Undermines...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When the clever mind undermines

Your spiritual confidence

In your life,

Do not be doomed to disappointment,

Even for a fleeting second.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Confidence In My Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Confidence in my mind

Wavers.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 Confidence In My Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Confidence in my heart

Soars.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 Confidence In My Soul...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Confidence in my soul

Prevails.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 My Aspiring Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My aspiring heart

Is

My intimate confidant.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 My Searching Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My searching mind

Is

My faithful confidant.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 My Striving Vital...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My striving vital

Is

My usual confidant.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 My Awakened Body...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My awakened body

Is

My receptive confidant.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 My Dedicated Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My dedicated life

Is

My serving and fulfilling

Confidant.

#+END_VERSE

* 34 When A Seeker Is Wanting...

  :PROPERTIES:

  :cite-key: TQCS:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When a seeker is wanting

In sincerity,

His aspiration-heart

And dedication-life

Are bound to become

Spiritually bankrupt

Sooner than at once.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 Sincerity...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sincerity

Is by far the best

God-Protection-assurance.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 A Sincerity-Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A sincerity-mind,

An openness-heart

And

A fulness-soul

Are most intimately

Inseparable.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 When I Become My Own...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I become my own

Sincerity-heart,

God becomes

His fastest and strongest

Magnet-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 The More I Value...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The more I value

My sincerity-breath,

The stronger becomes

God's Manifestation-Promise

In and through me on earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 In The Battlefield Of Life, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the battlefield of life,

We become the victors

Over ignorance-night

Only when

The sincerity of the mind,

The spontaneity of the heart,

The rapidity of the vital

And

The purity of the body

Work in perfect oneness.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 Sincerity, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sincerity,

I do love you and need you.

But I am telling you

In all sincerity

That I love and need

God's Will-Manifestation-Delight

On earth

Infinitely more.

Nay, God's Fulfilment-Smile

Is the only thing

That I sleeplessly need.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 Sincerity, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sincerity,

You are morality's

Loftiest height.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 Morality Is The Excellence...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Morality is the excellence

Of the mind, heart and life.

Although spirituality

Is in morality and for morality,

It goes far beyond

Morality's territory-confines.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 The Human In Us Needs...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The human in us needs

Sincerity's perfect perfection

And

Morality's complete satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 The Divine In Us...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The divine in us

Also needs the same.

But the divine in us,

Being consciously and constantly

One with God's Will,

At times does not abide

By morality's strict injunctions.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 Humanity's Complete Fulfilment...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Humanity's complete fulfilment

Can and shall dawn

Only when humanity prayerfully,

Bravely and self-givingly

Accepts Divinity

For the sake of Divinity.

#+END_VERSE


* 46 "Let Thy Will Be Done, "...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

"Let Thy Will be done,"

Him to fulfil in His own Way:

This is sincerity's sleepless dream

And breathless reality.

#+END_VERSE
