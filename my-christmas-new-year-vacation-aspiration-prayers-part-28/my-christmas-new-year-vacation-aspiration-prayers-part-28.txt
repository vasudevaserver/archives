/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Yogyakarta and Bali, Indonesia in December 2003 and January 2004./

/28 December 2003/

/Yogyakarta, Indonesia/



* 1 My Lord Takes Care ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2322
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord takes care
Not only of my divine qualities,
But also of my undivine qualities.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 My Lord Takes Care ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2323
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord takes care
Not only of my success-mind
And progress-heart,
But also of my failure-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 My Lord Takes Care ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2324
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord takes care
Not only of my soul’s
Strengthening smiles,
But also of my life’s
Tearing fears.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 My Lord Loves ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2325
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord loves
My body’s baby steps
And my soul’s giant steps
Equally.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 I Love ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2326
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I love
My Lord’s Thunder-Kicks
More than I love
My Lord’s Sweetness-Heart.
Why, why, why?
Because I desire
The fastest progress.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 I Love ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2327
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I love
My Lord’s Volcano-Eye
More than I love
My Lord’s soft and tender Caresses.
Why, why, why?
Because I desire
The fastest progress.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 Now That I Am At The Lotus-Feet ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2328
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Now that I am at the Lotus-Feet
Of my Lord Supreme,
My desire-life has come
To a complete and permanent halt.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2329
  :END:

My age-old stubborn mind
Is running towards
My Lord’s Heart-Home.

#+END_VERSE

#+END_VERSE
