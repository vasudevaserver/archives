* 1 The Future Of A Man...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:151
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE FUTURE OF A MAN

The future of a simple man:
Success.
The future of a sincere man:
Progress.
The future of a humble man:
Glory.
The future of a pure man:
Enlightenment serene.
The future of a concentrative, meditative
and contemplative man:
Perfection supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 O Bearer...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:152
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O BEARER

O my mind,
O bearer of my human searching,
Gracious is your life.
O my heart,
O bearer of my human longing,
Precious is your life.
O my soul,
O bearer of my human receiving
and achieving,
Magnanimous is your goal.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 His Desire To Please God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:153
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HIS DESIRE TO PLEASE GOD

His desire to please God
Ultimately failed.
His ambition to please God
Ultimately failed.
His aspiration to please God
Ultimately failed.
Finally
His surrender-heart pleased God
Openly,
Lovingly
And
Supremely.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 She And The World...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:154
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SHE AND THE WORLD

She plays merrily,
Therefore
The world likes her.
She speaks sweetly,
Therefore
The world loves her.
She sings melodiously,
Therefore
The world adores her.
She blesses divinely,
Therefore
The world constantly needs her.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Life Is Velocity...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:155
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

LIFE IS VELOCITY

Life is velocity.
Therefore
I want to run fast, very fast.
Love is necessity.
Therefore
I want to love more, ever more.
Truth is reality.
Therefore
I want to achieve, only achieve.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 When Will You Arrive?...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:156
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHEN WILL YOU ARRIVE?

Hope, my hope,
When will you arrive
At your goal?
Faith, my faith,
Have you not arrived
At your goal?
Love, my love,
When did you arrive
At your goal?
Light, my light,
You need the expansion
Of your goal.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 My Best Days...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:157
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY BEST DAYS

My best days
In hell:
When I smash the pride of Satan.
My best days
In Heaven:
When I cry for God-realisation.
My best days
On earth:
When I cry for God-manifestation.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Violations...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:158
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

VIOLATIONS

You violated
Your inner dignity.
Therefore
Reckless irresponsibility
Embraced you.
You violated
The secret teachings
Of the cosmic law.
Therefore
Even God did not near you
To save your life
Of untold deception.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 The Seed Of Perfection...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:159
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE SEED OF PERFECTION

Slowly grows
The seed of love.
Steadily grows
The seed of faith.
Unerringly grows
The seed of life.
Supremely grows
The seed of perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 What Is It?...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:160
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHAT IS IT?

Speed, what is it?
Beauty's light.
Love, what is it?
Duty's pride.
Delight, what is it?
Necessity's height.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 A Very Special Prayer...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:161
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A VERY SPECIAL PRAYER

Lamentation-force
Is a very special prayer.
Silence-force
Is a very special concentration.
Delight-force
Is a very special meditation.
Perfection-force
Is a very special contemplation.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Thee I Adore...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:162
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEE I ADORE

O Protector of poor souls,
Thee I adore.
O Saviour of wicked souls,
Thee I adore.
O Liberator of earthbound souls,
Thee I adore.
O Transformer of fallen souls,
Thee I adore.
O Fulfiller of aspiring souls,
Thee I adore,
Thee I love,
Thee I invoke.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Heavy And Light...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:163
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HEAVY AND LIGHT

Heavy
Is the weight of failure.
Heavier
Is the weight of success.
Heaviest
Is the weight of progress.
Light
Is the weight of hope.
Lighter
Is the weight of faith.
Lightest
Is the weight of love.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 I Am Not Alone...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:164
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I AM NOT ALONE

My progress is permanently
Protected by my Father's Will.
My faith is permanently
Protected by my Mother's Love.
I am not alone.
I am with my Father's
Dream-boat Reality.
I am not alone.
I am with my Mother's
Reality-fulfilled Dream.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 Devotion-Power...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:165
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

DEVOTION-POWER


AND


SURRENDER-POWER

Insecurity's length
And
Timidity's breadth
Comprised his little world.
Then, suddenly, devotion-power
Became the light-giver
To his ignorance vast,
And
Surrender-power
Made him one
With the Transcendence-goal
Of the ever-transcending Beyond.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 Your Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:166
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

YOUR LIFE

That was your life:
You deliberately forgot to aspire.
This is your life:
You spontaneously remember to aspire.
That was your life:
Hard you endeavoured to surpass God.
This is your life:
Hard you try to please God,
Even for a fleeting moment.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 Like It Or Not...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:167
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

LIKE IT OR NOT

Whether you like it
Or not,
God will tell you many things.
Whether he likes it
Or not,
God has no time to talk to him at all.
Whether I like it
Or not,
God wants to embarrass me
By placing me on
His Transcendental Throne.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 This Is Not My Birthday...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:168
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THIS IS NOT MY BIRTHDAY

This is not my birthday.
My birthday was yesterday,
When God and I sang and played.
This is not my birthday.
O earth-pilgrims,
Why do you torture me
With earthly gifts?
I need only one heavenly gift,
Love:
Love from God,
Love from God alone.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 In The Eyes Of The Saviours...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:169
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

IN THE EYES OF THE SAVIOURS

In the eyes of the Saviour Christ,
The world was an ignorance-night.
In the eyes of the Saviour Krishna,
The world was an ignorance-sea.
In the eyes of the Saviour Buddha,
The world was an ignorance-sky.
O ignorance-night,
Your futility hurts me.
O ignorance-sea,
Your insensibility hurts me.
O ignorance-sky,
Your indifference hurts me.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 The Opening...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:170
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE OPENING

Faith is the opening
Of the wisdom-eye.
Love is the opening
Of the oneness-heart.
Service is the opening
Of the immortality-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 Shun His Company...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:171
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SHUN HIS COMPANY

His vital is a mixture
Of impure vanity
And
Dark superstition.
Therefore,
Shun his company.
If you mix with him
You are bound
To welcome
Your inner death.
Alas, what a troublesome world
His earth-life represents!

#+END_VERSE


* 22 Fear, Doubt And Faith...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:172
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

FEAR, DOUBT AND FAITH

To me,
Fear is a pardonable defect
Because it dies;
Doubt is an unpardonable defect
Because it always survives;
Faith is a radiant smile
Because it overrules
All blind human opinion.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 Silence And Sound...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:173
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SILENCE AND SOUND

When the desire
To escape captured him,
He entered
Into a Himalayan cave.
Alas, peace and joy
Cared not for him.
He came back
To the sound-life
And discovered:
Silence is embodied perfection,
Sound is revealed manifestation.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 Journey Of Light And Delight...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:174
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

JOURNEY OF LIGHT AND DELIGHT

There was a time
When I endured
The indifference of ignorance-night.
But now ignorance
Endures my indifference-light.
There was a time
When I endured
The tortures of human boundaries.
But now they
Are no more.
They have disappeared
In my upward and inward journey
Of Light and Delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 O Builder...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:175
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O BUILDER

O builder of my soul,
Where are You?
O builder of my Goal,
How far are You?
O builder of my heart,
How kind are You?
O builder of my mind,
How fantastic are You?

#+END_VERSE


* 26 I Am Coming...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:176
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I AM COMING

O abyss of oblivion,
I am coming to you
With my information-mind.
Please wait.
O palace of illumination,
I am coming to you
With my aspiration-heart.
Please wait.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 Forgetfulness-Monarch...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:177
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

FORGETFULNESS-MONARCH

O stormy vital emotion,
From now on my name shall be
Forgetfulness-Prince.
O sombre mental frustration,
From now on my name shall be
Forgetfulness-King.
O foolish psychic destruction,
From now on my name shall be
Forgetfulness-Emperor.
Yet
I shall run,
But with a vital new,
with a mind new,
with a heart new,
Beyond the length
Of extremity's song,

#+END_VERSE


* 28 Sky Of Perfection-Dawn...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:178
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SKY OF PERFECTION-DAWN

From his tongue
Comes every possible
Absurdity.
From his eyes
Comes every possible
Futility.
Yet his ecstatic
Love-adoration for God
Saves him and lifts his life
Into the sky of Perfection-Dawn.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 Every Message...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:179
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

EVERY MESSAGE

Every secret message
Is a Thought of God.
Every sacred message
Is a Will of God.
Every divine message
Is a Consciousness-Sun of God.
Every supreme message
Is a Perfection-
Manifestation-
Reality
Of God.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 His Body's Shallow Strength...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:180
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HIS BODY'S SHALLOW STRENGTH

When he thinks,
He thinks from his mind's
Pygmy height.
When he speaks,
He speaks from his vital's
Unmeasured length.
When he acts,
He acts from his body's
Shallow strength.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 Silence-Sky, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:181
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SILENCE-SKY,


SOUND-SEA

My weeping heart
Teaches me
God is an indifferent King.
My singing soul
Teaches me
God is a jovial Friend.
My loving Goal
Teaches me
God gives two gifts-
Morning's silence-sky,
Evening's sound-sea.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 Conclusions...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:182
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

CONCLUSIONS

Human life is a dream.
Indeed,
This is your philosopher's conclusion.
Human life is a game.
Indeed,
This is your lover's conclusion.
Human life is a Reality.
Indeed,
This is your seeker's conclusion.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Secrets...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:183
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SECRETS

Top secrets
Everybody knows:
God is man's Light,
Man is God's night.
Open secrets
Nobody knows:
Man is another golden God,
Man is another black animal.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 When I Look At Him...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:184
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHEN I LOOK AT HIM

When I look at him
I see an aura of fear
Behind his head.
When I look at him
I see an aura of death
Around his face.
A mere mortal
He can never be.
An inner pain
Gnaws at him
Constantly.
His is a life of
Ill-fortune
And
Hermit-existence.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 They Learn More...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:185
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEY LEARN MORE

Children learn more
When they are loved.
Parents learn more
When they are ignored.
Humanity learns more
When it is appreciated.
Divinity learns more
When it is rejected.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 Our Commitment...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:186
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

OUR COMMITMENT

Life is our commitment
To equality.
Love is our commitment
To reality.
Peace is our commitment
To liberty.
Power is our commitment
To divinity.
God is our commitment
To Immortality.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 His Hunger For God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:187
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HIS HUNGER FOR GOD

His body's hunger for God
Was a stupid curiosity.
His vital's hunger for God
Was a dauntless curiosity.
His mind's hunger for God
Was a cautious curiosity.
His heart's hunger for God
Was an illumined curiosity.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 You Are Your Will...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:188
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

YOU ARE YOUR WILL

Always control yourself.
A moment of impatience
Is a movement of self-destruction.
You are your own adamantine will.
In you and for you
Is your transcendental Goal.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 A Perfect Fool...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:189
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A PERFECT FOOL

A perfect fool he is.
He began his life-journey
Badly and stupidly,
And it went on worse,
Strikingly worse.
Yet he fails to surrender
To reality's light
And divinity's height.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 Luminous, Precious, Gracious...

  :PROPERTIES:

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

LUMINOUS, PRECIOUS, GRACIOUS

Luminous was she
Within her heart.
Precious was she
In the heart of her life.
Gracious was she
In the soul of her Goal.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 You Have A Passion...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:191
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

YOU HAVE A PASSION

You have a passion for Truth.
Therefore
Truth is within you.
You have a passion for Light.
Therefore
Light enlightens you.
You have a passion for Love.
Therefore
Love immortalises you.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 A Great Competition...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:192
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A GREAT COMPETITION

God and I
Entered into a great competition.
He pitted His Love
Against my teeming doubts.
Alas, He won.
I pitted my ingratitude
Against His flowing Compassion.
Alas, this time too, He won.
God, since twice I have lost to You,
I shall muster no more
Courage-light
To challenge Your superiority.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 A Complete Innocence, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:193
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A COMPLETE INNOCENCE,


A COMPLETE PERFECTION

A complete innocence
Signifies an awakened life.
A complete perfection
Signifies an unconditional surrender-light
And transcendence-height
In the heart-nest
Of the seeker.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 He Became What He Was...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:194
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HE BECAME WHAT HE WAS

With bubbling enthusiasm
He launched into the path of yoga.
With doubting uncertainty
He walked along the path.
With his destructive impatience,
With his devouring personality,
He left the path,
Only to become once again
What he was before:
A monkey with a
Sesquipedalian tail.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 Divinity's Smile...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:195
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

DIVINITY'S SMILE


AND


IMMORTALITY'S DAWN

Doubt ends
When separativity ends.
Faith begins
When consciousness ascends
And reaches the free heights
Of divinity's Smile
And
Immortality's Dawn

#+END_VERSE


* 46 A Golden Link...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:196
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A GOLDEN LINK

Between
His heart of fear
And
His mind of doubt
A wide chasm creeps.
Between
His vital of aggression
And
His body of suppression
A wide chasm creeps.
But
Between his soul of service
And
His God of Love
No chasm creeps.
A golden link
Unites
God the Lover Supreme
And man the server divine.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 Smile And Go Away...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:197
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SMILE AND GO AWAY

Short are the hours of pleasure-life,
Yet man wants it.
Long are the hours of happiness-life,
Yet man wants it not.
Smile and go away
From the hours of pleasure-life
Cry and stay within
The hours of happiness-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 Three Voices...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:198
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THREE VOICES

A deep warning voice solemnly commanded:
"Don't speak to doubt.
Doubt is poison."
A deep loving voice smilingly whispered:
"Mix with faith.
Faith is reality's all."
A deep illumining voice soulfully revealed:
"Drink the fragrance of love.
Love is all."

#+END_VERSE


* 49 The Dawn...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:199
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE DAWN

Silence
Is the dawn of time.
Sound
Is the dawn of will.
Man-success
Is the dawn of aspiration.
God-progress
Is the dawn of Perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 The Golden Ones...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:200
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE GOLDEN ONES

The golden Boat
Is beauty's speed.
The golden Shore
Is divinity's crown.
The golden Dream,
Reality dreams.
The golden Pilot,
Eternity claims.

#+END_VERSE
