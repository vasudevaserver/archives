The poems in this volume were written in Pondicherry, India on July 6th and 7th, 1998.



* 71 Where?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:71
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Where am I coming from?
I am coming from my mind's doubt-funeral.
Where am I going to?
I am going to my Lord's singing Rehearsal.

#+END_VERSE



* 72 Even I hesitate

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:72
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Supreme begs me
To be His Heart-playmate.
My body-vital-mind-heart,
Even I hesitate.

#+END_VERSE



* 73 Run

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:73
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Run, run, my heart!
Run extremely fast.
Ignore, ignore
The voices of the past.

#+END_VERSE



* 74 Never retire

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:74
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart and I
Together aspire.
God's Message-Light:
"Never retire!"

#+END_VERSE



* 75 Complaint-seeds

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:75
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Wherever
My doubt-mind goes,
Complaint-seeds
Proudly it sows.

#+END_VERSE



* 76 God's chosen souls

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:76
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's chosen souls
Are His Poetry,
And also they are
His Pottery.

#+END_VERSE



* 77 I flounder

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:77
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When my aspiration is
Totally out of order,
Alas, helpless, hopeless,
My life and I flounder.

#+END_VERSE



* 78 Hope and promise

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:78
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My hope
Is my heart-land.
My promise
Is God's own Hand.

#+END_VERSE



* 79 Expectation

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:79
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Expectation
The less I need,
My Lord Supreme
The more I feed.

#+END_VERSE



* 80 Wisdom-strength

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:80
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord never measures
My stupidity's length.
He just tells me: "My child,
For you My Wisdom-Strength."

#+END_VERSE



* 81 Beauty's light

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:81
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every morning my eagerness-heart
Rockets my prayers to the summit-height.
Empty of darkness-night my life,
Within, without, His Beauty's Light.

#+END_VERSE



* 82 Heart-floor

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:82
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I kneel before
God's Forgiveness-Door.
"Come in, My child,
Dance on My Heart-Floor."

#+END_VERSE



* 83 Sadness-face

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:83
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind is the proof
Of my sweet Lord's Sadness-Face.
My heart is the winner
Of the longest Heavenward race.

#+END_VERSE



* 84 No more

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:84
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No more, no more
My pleasure-life.
Nowhere I see
My destruction-knife.

#+END_VERSE



* 85 Victory-drums

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:85
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The seeds of joy
I plant in my mind.
God's Victory-Drums
All-where I find.

#+END_VERSE



* 86 Multiplying misdeeds

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:86
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord eyeless remains
To my multiplying misdeeds.
No expectation-fruit -- 
From morn to eve my heart He feeds.

#+END_VERSE



* 87 Soul-rapture

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:87
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O stark death-blows,
My heart you cannot torture.
Never forget,
I am my soul-rapture.

#+END_VERSE



* 88 God-smiles

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:88
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mine is the joy
That ever shines at God's Golden Feet.
Mine is the task
To give God-smiles, whomever I meet.

#+END_VERSE



* 89 Frustration

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:89
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind's frustration weighs
Heavier than failure.
Alas, my heart and I
Know not how to endure.

#+END_VERSE



* 90 Utterly lost

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:90
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My doubting mind
Never touches God's Heart.
Utterly lost,
Within, without, death-dart.

#+END_VERSE



* 91 Heart-garden

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:91
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme
Was shocked
To see my heart-garden
All locked.

#+END_VERSE



* 92 Victory-bell

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:92
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every desire-bound thought
Is a tenebrous prison-cell.
Alas, I hear nowhere
My Lord's Victory-ringing Bell.

#+END_VERSE



* 93 Gratitude-heart-flame

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:93
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

From high Above,
My Lord's Compassion-Rain descends.
From low below,
My gratitude-heart-flame ascends.

#+END_VERSE



* 94 Ownership

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:94
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I sleep nowhere
Ignorance-sleep.
My Lord claims now
My ownership.

#+END_VERSE



* 95 Every Step

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:95
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O every step along the progress-road
Quickly lightens my heavy bondage-load.

#+END_VERSE



* 96 Service-life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:96
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My happy service-life
I dearly love.
Indeed, a Blessing-Boon
From high Above.

#+END_VERSE



* 97 God-atmosphere

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:97
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-atmosphere
Sleeplessly I need,
My roaring mind
To silence and lead.

#+END_VERSE



* 98 Pseudo-reality

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:98
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Within, without,
You have no divinity.
Your mind enjoys
Pseudo-reality.

#+END_VERSE



* 99 Sleeper versus leaper

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:99
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mine is a mind
That has become an indulgence-sleeper.
Mine is a heart
That has become a Heavenward leaper.

#+END_VERSE



* 100 Desert-mind versus paradise-heart

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:100
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There was a time
When my life was a desert-mind,
And now in me
Beauty's paradise-heart I find.

#+END_VERSE



* 101 Deathless spring

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:101
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The mind can be
A danger-thing.
The heart can be
A deathless spring.

#+END_VERSE



* 102 Oneness-reality

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:102
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart's
Perfection-dream perpetually glows.
My life's
Oneness-reality everywhere grows.

#+END_VERSE



* 103 Silence

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:103
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The inner silence
Is my soul's voice.
The outer silence
Is my only choice.

#+END_VERSE



* 104 Where my lord is

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:104
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My eyes know not
Where my Lord is.
My heart feels that
It is His Bliss.

#+END_VERSE



* 105 God-journey

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:105
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are not your sadness-life.
You are your gladness-heart.
You are not your failure-tears.
Start your God-journey, start.

#+END_VERSE



* 106 Hope-boat

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:106
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My life's hope-boat
Is bound to succeed.
My Lord's Vision-Eye,
Its promise and speed.

#+END_VERSE



* 107 Meeting-date

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:107
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O never, never mix with hate.
God will cancel His Meeting-Date.

#+END_VERSE



* 108 Pleasure-life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:108
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lo, at top speed
A stark repentance-knife
Is chasing today
My thoughtless pleasure-life.

#+END_VERSE



* 109 Pilgrim-soul

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:109
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I love my pilgrim-soul,
I love, I love.
My soul, my Supreme Lord's
All Fondness-Dove.

#+END_VERSE



* 110 Lord thrives

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:110
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O when gladness
Brightens our lives,
Fast, very fast,
Our sweet Lord thrives.

#+END_VERSE



* 111 Destiny-sounds

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: SMHS:111
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O God-seeker-heart,
Aspire, enjoy your inner epiphany.
Permit not your life
To be eclipsed by the sounds of destiny.

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: poem
