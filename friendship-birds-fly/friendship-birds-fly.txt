* Part I: Friendship-Birds fly

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: section
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:1e1
  :END:



* 1.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my friend,
Let us claim each other first.
Then let us walk together
Towards our destined goal.

#+END_VERSE


* 2.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Friendship is
No fragile human bond.
Friendship is
A strong oneness-bridge.

#+END_VERSE


* 3.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so proud of my friends,
For all of them are
Genuine peace-prophets.

#+END_VERSE


* 4.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each and every friend of mine
Rightly deserves
My heart's gratitude-tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 5.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A real friend may be
Far, very far in miles,
But near, very near
In heart-smiles.

#+END_VERSE


* 6.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sincerity-bud-beauty
Is my mind's safeguard-friend.
Purity-blossom-fragrance
Is my heart's safeguard-friend.

#+END_VERSE


* 7.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A new friend is a new hope-sea
Inside my heart.
A new friend is a new hope-sky
Inside my mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 8.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Friendship openly persuades our minds
To do the right thing
And secretly convinces our hearts
To become the right person.

#+END_VERSE


* 9.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Friendship-birds
Can fly
Only on the wings of sincerity.

#+END_VERSE


* 10.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Friendship is the mind's readiness,
The heart's willingness
and the life's oneness.

#+END_VERSE


* Part II: I am so happy

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: section
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:11e1
  :END:



* 1.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I have done
The right thing:
I have destroyed
My monarch-ego.

#+END_VERSE


* 2.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I have done
The right thing:
I have not allowed
My receptivity-heart
To fall asleep.

#+END_VERSE


* 3.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I am immersed
In my self-giving service.

#+END_VERSE


* 4.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I have not allowed science
To inflate my spiritual mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 5.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I do not have the smile
Of a world-conqueror.

#+END_VERSE


* 6.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I have the smile
Of a world-lover.

#+END_VERSE


* 7.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I do not immortalise
My mind's frustrations
And my mind's miseries.

#+END_VERSE


* 8.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I gladly accept
All the proposals of my heart
And execute them immediately.

#+END_VERSE


* 9.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I never allow my mind
To oppose my heart's
God-inspired and God-initiated proposals.

#+END_VERSE


* 10.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That my heart and I
Every day sing together
Our unconditional life-songs
To God.

#+END_VERSE


* 11.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That the ideal in me
And the real in me
Have most cheerfully
Become inseparable.

#+END_VERSE


* 12.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I have fortified
My aspiration-breath
With ever-increasing faith.

#+END_VERSE


* 13.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That my aspiration-heart
And my dedication-life
Sleeplessly long for the inner grace
And not for the outer space.

#+END_VERSE


* 14.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That at long last I have become
The big man who sympathises,
And that I am no longer
The little man who criticises.

#+END_VERSE


* 15.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That my desire-life
Has been completely
And permanently illumined
By my aspiration-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 16.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That my heart hears
Enchanting, illumining
And fulfilling God-melodies
Wherever I go.

#+END_VERSE


* 17.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That my mind is no longer a member
Of the doubt-society.

#+END_VERSE


* 18.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That my heart no longer lives
Inside an insecurity-cave.

#+END_VERSE


* 19.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I have a new name:
Gratitude-fragrance-heart-smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 20.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That at long last
I have the capacity
To claim only my Lord Beloved Supreme
As my own, very own.

* Part III: My mind and my heart

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: section
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:31e1
  :END:



* 1.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind tells me
That it is too soon
For me to realise God.


My heart tells me
That it is already late
For me to realise God.

#+END_VERSE


* 2.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind tells me
That what I need is a finish line.


My heart tells me
That what I need is a progress-run.

#+END_VERSE


* 3.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind wants me
To enjoy pleasure-harvest.


My heart wants me
To enjoy faith-feast.

#+END_VERSE


* 4.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind wants me
To go out
And be part of
The outer world.


My heart wants me
To come home
And remain a member of
The inner family.

#+END_VERSE


* 5.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Opportunity is the only thing
That my mind craves.


Preparation is the only thing
That my heart longs for.

#+END_VERSE


* 6.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind-teacher tells me
That time is a circle


My heart-teacher tells me
That time has no birth
And no death.

#+END_VERSE


* 7.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind is afraid
Of the aspiration-life.


My heart is afraid
Of the desire-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 8.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind loves
Daring intensity.


My heart loves
Illumining immensity.

#+END_VERSE


* 9.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind wants me
To be a world-shaking warrior.


My heart wants me
To be a world-elevating inspirer.

#+END_VERSE


* 10.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind's supremacy
Wants to change the world.


My heart's ecstasy
Wants to arrange the world.

#+END_VERSE


* 11.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Hesitation
Precedes my mind.


Satisfaction
Precedes my heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 12.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind loves to take
Confusion-extension exercises.


My heart loves to take
Confusion-extinction plus
Illumination-expansion exercises.

#+END_VERSE


* 13.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FBF:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind asks God,
"God, do You live for me?"


My heart asks God,
"My Lord, will I always
Live only for You?"

#+END_VERSE
