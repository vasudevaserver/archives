#+BEGIN_VERSE

Life is short.
Hope is long.
Promise is vast.
Promise-fulfilment is perfect perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 2.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Life is a journey.
Death is the continuation of the journey.
Heaven is a temporary rest
For the commencement and fulfilment
Of a new life, a new hope
    And a new promise.

#+END_VERSE
