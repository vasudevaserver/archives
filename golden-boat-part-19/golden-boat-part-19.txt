* 1 Hope Entered...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:901
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HOPE ENTERED




Hope entered.
He smiled and danced.
He became God's Vision-Race.
Hope departed.
He cried and slept.
He became Eternity's ignorance-face.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 God's Grace...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:902
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD'S GRACE




God's unconditional Grace:
My soul's illumining face.
God's unconditional Perfection-Show.
My heart's climbing tower.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 My Silence...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:903
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY SILENCE




My silence
Is the morning light
Of God's God-Vision.
In God-Vision I have become
The angel-eye
Of Infinity's Soul-Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 God Thanked Me...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:904
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD THANKED ME




I prayed to God.
He thanked me compassionately.
I meditated on God.
He thanked me smilingly.
I surrendered my gratitude-life to God.
He thanked me lovingly.
I declared my sovereign equality to God.
He embraced me unreservedly.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 My Dark Secret, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:905
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY DARK SECRET,


MY BRIGHT SECRET



I love myself only:
This is my dark secret.
I love God only:
This is my bright secret.
God blesses and perfects
My dark secret.
God fondles and treasures
My bright secret.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 My Eternal Sunrise...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:906
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY ETERNAL SUNRISE




My doubt's desire-life
Is my slow, gradual death.
I know it.
My faith's aspiration-life
Is my eternal sunrise.
I know it.
I love it.
I need it.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 My Beauty...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:907
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY BEAUTY




Yesterday
I was despair-beauty.
Today
I am hope-beauty.
Tomorrow
I shall become surrender-beauty.
The day after tomorrow
I shall become God-Beauty.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Who Are You?...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:908
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHO ARE YOU?




Jealousy, do you know
Who you are?
In case you do not know
I shall tell you who you are.
You are the shameless weakness
Of humanity.
Doubt, do you know
Who you are?
In case you do not know
I shall tell you who you are
You are the useless sickness
Of humanity.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 Three Worlds...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:909
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THREE WORLDS




A moment's pleasure
Your vital-world wants.
Eternity's joy
Your heart-world needs.
Immortality's satisfaction
Your soul-world is.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 My Identity...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:910
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY IDENTITY




Who was I?
Earth's idle dreamer.
Who am I?
Earth's slowest walker.
What shall I be?
My life's frustration
And failure collector.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 In Silence...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:911
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

IN SILENCE




In silence you suffered
Because
You were too great.
In silence you suffer
Because
You are too good.
In silence you will smile, sing and dance
Because
The Hour of God before long will strike.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 When I Pray And Meditate...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:912
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHEN I PRAY AND MEDITATE




When I pray
I enjoy the depth
Of God-Compassion
In my heart-world.
When I meditate
I enjoy the embrace
Of God-Concentration
In my soul-world.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 The Life Of Light...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:913
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE LIFE OF LIGHT




The life of night
Has only one destructive thing:
The poison of despair.
The life of light
Has not one,
Not two,
But three constructive things:
The love of light,
The path of progress,
The victory of aspiration.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 Only One Armour...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:914
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

ONLY ONE ARMOUR




There is only one armour
Against fate:
God's transcendental
Silence-Grace.
There is only one armour
Against God-satisfaction:
Man's ephemeral
Ingratitude-face.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 The Role Of Time...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:915
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE ROLE OF TIME




The role of Time:
Duty, constant duty.
The soul of Time:
Beauty, illumining beauty.
The goal of Time:
Love-reality
And
God-necessity.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 He Is Afraid Of Three Things...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:916
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HE IS AFRAID OF THREE THINGS




He is afraid of three things:
Silence, sound and perfection.
When he is afraid of silence
Earth loves him.
When he is afraid of sound
Heaven loves him.
When he is afraid of perfection
The animal in him loves him
Plus
The human in him loves him;
But
The divine in him pities him
And
The Supreme in him forgives him.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 Father And Sons...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:917
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

FATHER AND SONS




Prayer says to God:
"Father, I have come to You.
What will You give me?"
"Son, I shall give you joy.
Look, I am massaging your feet."
Meditation says to God:
"Father, You have come to me.
What can I offer You?
What will give You joy?"
"Son, you can massage My Head."

#+END_VERSE


* 18 Two Blessings...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:918
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

TWO BLESSINGS




Earth-ignorance
Blesses me
By forgetting me.
I am grateful.
Heaven-Justice
Blesses me
By forgiving me.
I am grateful.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 The Flame Of Courage...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:919
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE FLAME OF COURAGE




The flame of courage
Illumines me, my earth's life.
I am grateful.
The flame of love
Fulfils me, my soul's role.
I am grateful.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 Only One Food...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:920
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

ONLY ONE FOOD




His is the body
That cries for only one food:
Night, brooding night.
His is the vital
That cries for only one food:
Might, controlling might.
His is the mind
That cries for only one food:
Light, glowing light.
His is the heart
That cries for only one food:
Delight, flowing delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 The Ladder Of Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:921
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE LADDER OF LIFE




The ladder of humility
Leads us to God-Heights of
Man-emancipation
And
Man-perfection.
The ladder of pride
Is uncertainty's song,
Imperfection's dance
And
Futility's hush.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 Two Secrets...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:922
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

TWO SECRETS




A heart-shattering secret:
My God-realisation
Will not take place
In this lifetime.
A life-illumining
And
Life-immortalising secret:
God's Eternity
Loves me supremely
And
Needs me constantly.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 A Never-Ending Now...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:923
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A NEVER-ENDING NOW


AND


AN EVER-ENDING NOW



He lives
In a never-ending Now
And
In an ever-ending now.
A never-ending Now
Is God's choice for him
An ever-ending now
Is the voice of his treasured
Ignorance-night.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 In Spite Of What I Am...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:924
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

IN SPITE OF WHAT I AM




A daily miracle:
The human in me prays to God
In spite of what I am.
A constant miracle:
God-Compassion dreams in me,
God-Joy loves me
And
God-Reality needs me
In spite of what I am.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 Leave Me Not Alone...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:925
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

LEAVE ME NOT ALONE




O earth, leave me not alone.
I need you.
I need your heart's
Aspiration-cry
To see the Face of God.
O Heaven, leave me not alone.
I need you.
I need your soul's
Divinity-smile
To discover the Beauty of God.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 Be Not Proud...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:926
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

BE NOT PROUD




Death, be not proud;
Life is aspiring.
Life, be not proud;
Death is approaching.
Death and life,
Be not proud;
The Lord Supreme is watching.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 I Love My Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:927
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I LOVE MY HEART




My heart of love
Is divinely beautiful.
I love my beautiful heart.
My heart of devotion
Is eternally soulful.
I love my soulful heart.
My heart of surrender
Is supremely fruitful.
I love my fruitful heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 They Need Her...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:928
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEY NEED HER




She embodies
The Eye of Heaven;
Therefore
Earth constantly needs her.
She embodies
The heart of earth;
Therefore
Heaven unreservedly needs her.
She heralds
God-perfection on earth;
Therefore
God supremely needs her.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 Doubt-Cure...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:929
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

DOUBT-CURE




If you are a doubter,
Then your faith in yourself
Will one day
Come to the fore
And cure you.
But
If you are doubt itself,
Then God alone
Can cure you
And that, too, He will do at His choice Hour
And not at your hopeful hour.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 Love Me Not, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:930
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

LOVE ME NOT,


LEAVE ME NOT



My Lord,
Love me not
If You do
Want to.
My Beloved,
Leave me not
Even if You
Want to.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:931
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

YESTERDAY, TODAY, TOMORROW




Yesterday
My life was a thorn-crown.
I endured it.
Today
My life is a clown-frown.
I endure it.
Tomorrow
My life shall be a God-town.
The whole world will treasure it.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 God And We...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:932
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD AND WE




God has no Hands
Of His own.
We are His hands.
We have no eye
Of our own.
God is our Eye.
God and we
Have one thing
In common:
Oneness-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Yet God Loves Us...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:933
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

YET GOD LOVES US




You are an artist
Who cannot draw;
Yet
God loves you.
Why?
Because you are pure.
He is a singer
Who cannot sing;
Yet
God loves him.
Why?
Because he is humble.
I am a seeker
Who cannot seek;
Yet
God loves me.
Why?
Because I am sincere.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 My Body And My Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:934
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY BODY AND MY HEART




My body says to my heart:
"Beloved, I have only you."
My heart says to my body:
"My sweet little chariot,
For you alone
God's transcendental Crown
And
God's universal Throne."

#+END_VERSE


* 35 Mine...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:935
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MINE




Mine is the hope-sky
That keeps me alive.
Mine is the faith-moon
That guides me safely.
Mine is the promise-sun
That fulfils me unreservedly.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 A Broken Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:936
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A BROKEN LIFE




Yesterday
I embodied a heap
Of broken hopes.
Today
I reveal the thoughts
Of an arid brain.
Tomorrow
I shall sit down
Quietly
And
I shall weep
Helplessly.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 When He Died...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:937
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHEN HE DIED




When
His body-vital-mind died,
There was silence in Heaven.
When
His heart died,
There was sound in Heaven.
When
He himself died,
Earth gave him earth's heart-cry
And
Heaven gave him Heaven's soul-smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 God's Hospitality...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:938
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD'S HOSPITALITY


AND


MAN'S HOSPITALITY



God's Hospitality
Tells me:
I need not shout my faith.
Man's hospitality
Tells me:
I must show
Not only
My heart of gratitude
But also
My soul of admiration.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 When I Am Alone...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:939
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHEN I AM ALONE




Believe it or not,
My eyes do not see
When I look alone.
Believe it or not,
My heart does not breathe
When I live alone.
Believe it or not,
My God does not need me
When I meditate alone.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 My Pride...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:940
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY PRIDE




My yesterday was proud
Of its wild disbelief.
My today is proud
Of its blind unbelief.
My tomorrow shall be proud
Of its spontaneous belief.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 O Silence...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:941
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O SILENCE




O Eye of Silence,
Your Eye is beautiful.
I need your transcendental Eye.
O Heart of Silence,
Your Heart is beautiful.
I need your universal Heart.
O Life of Silence,
Your Life is beautiful.
I need your immortal Life.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 Your Teachers...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:942
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

YOUR TEACHERS




Let the sky
Become
Your teacher.
You will learn
How to serve.
Let the moon
Become
Your teacher.
You will learn
How to love.
Let the sun
Become
Your teacher.
You will learn
How to become.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 The Voice...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:943
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THE VOICE




Yesterday
I was the trembling voice
Of earth's cry.
Today
I am the illumining voice
Of Heaven's smile.
Tomorrow
I shall become the fulfilling voice
Of God's Promise.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 Like Yesterday...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:944
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

LIKE YESTERDAY




God cares for me;
Therefore,
Like yesterday,
Today for me
Is another aspiration-experience.
I care for God;
Therefore,
Like yesterday,
Today for God
Is another satisfaction-dawn.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 Hidden Failure...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:945
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HIDDEN FAILURE


AND HIDDEN SUCCESS



My hidden failure:
My dark ingratitude.
My hidden success:
My climbing love for God.

#+END_VERSE


* 46 Reality Of Love...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:946
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

REALITY OF LOVE




Intensity of love
My heart of gratitude
Embodies.
Immensity of love
My life of fortitude
Reveals.
Divinity of love
My soul of plenitude
Manifests.
Reality of love
My God of infinitude
Eternally is.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 Three Gods...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:947
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THREE GODS




You are, at your best,
A different God.
He is, at his best,
An indifferent God.
And
I am, at my worst,
A transparent God.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 Heaven-Silence And World-Sound...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:948
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HEAVEN-SILENCE AND WORLD-SOUND




My life's world-sound
Is my success-plant
Threatened and frightened
By earth-storms.
My heart's Heaven-silence
Is my progress-tree
That shelters and feeds
Earth's excruciating pangs.

#+END_VERSE


* 49 They Are Dead...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:949
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEY ARE DEAD




You have deserted your honesty.
Lo, God's Duty-Tree
Which has been pleasing you
Is dead.
You have completely deserted your humility.
Lo, God's Beauty-Flower
Which has been enlightening you
Is unmistakably dead.
You have deserted your purity.
Lo, God's Spontaneity-Fruit
Which has been nourishing you
Is helplessly dead.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 Victory...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :CITE-KEY: GB:950
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

VICTORY




Human victory
Is always precarious.
Divine victory
Is always prosperous.
God's Victory
Is always
Precious,
Gracious
And
Advantageous.

#+END_VERSE
