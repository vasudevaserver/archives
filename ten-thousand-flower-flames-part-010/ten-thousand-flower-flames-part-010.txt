* 901 The Real In Me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:901
  :END:

Oneness.
Humility strengthens the real in me:
Perfection.
Gratitude enlightens the real in me:
Satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE

Of self-encouragement."

To my self-giving heart?

What it must know,

Satisfaction-sun.

  :cite-key: FF:913

I am helpless
That I am senseless?

You do not need Light."
