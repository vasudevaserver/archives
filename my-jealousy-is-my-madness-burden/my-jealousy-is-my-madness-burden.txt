* 23 The Seeker Says To Jealousy, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MJMB:23

#+BEGIN_VERSE

The seeker says to jealousy,
"Jealousy where are you?"
Jealousy says to the seeker,
"O seeker, are you blind?
I am everywhere!
Especially in the mind
And in the vital
Of every desire."
