* 1 Don't Think, Don't Sleep...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:51
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

DON'T THINK, DON'T SLEEP

When he was a child,
One day
His father said to him:
"Don't think, my child.
God never thinks."
Since then, he never cared
To learn the art of thinking.
When he was a child.
One day
His mother said to him:
"Don't sleep, my child.
God never sleeps."
Since then, he never cared
To learn the art of sleeping.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 Inspiration-Seed, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:52
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

INSPIRATION-SEED,


REALISATION-PLANT,


MANIFESTATION-TREE

Yesterday
In my soul-house
I kept my inspiration-seed.
Today
In my heart-room
I keep my realisation-plant.
Tomorrow
At my body-door
I shall keep my manifestation-tree.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 O Seeker...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:53
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O SEEKER

O seeker of silence-beauty,
I long to fly with you.
O seeker of soul-smile,
I long to expand like you.
O seeker of God-Reality,
I long to be
The Son-Light of the absolute King
Like you, with you.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Tears...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:54
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

TEARS

O clever tears
Of my eyes,
Stop descending.
O secret tears
Of my heart,
Start ascending.
God is awaiting your great arrival.
O sacred tears
Of my soul,
I love you;
I love you only.
Not because you have the world-treasure,
Not because you are the world-treasure,
But because you have chosen me
As your very own.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Although I Sleep...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:55
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

ALTHOUGH I SLEEP

Although I sleep
During my morning meditation,
God appreciates my attempt.
Therefore
I thank God.
Although I sleep
During my evening meditation,
I am proud of my attempt.
Therefore
I love myself.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 In One Day...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:56
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

IN ONE DAY

In one day
I became old:
When earth refused to accept
My help.
In one day
I became young again:
When Heaven offered me
His Friendship.
In one day
I fed earth's ageless hunger:
When earth showed me
Her sunken sun.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 God's Plans...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:57
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD'S PLANS

Music that I wish to hear,
God is planning to compose.
Love that I wish to feel,
God is planning to create.
Joy that I wish to become,
God is planning to offer.
Perfection that I wish to see,
God is planning to plant.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 A Man Of God-Substance...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:58
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A MAN OF GOD-SUBSTANCE

What was he?
A man of fabulous divinity!
What is he?
A man of imprisoned pride!
What is he going to be?
A man of God-substance,
A man of love-essence.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 Quarrels With God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:59
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

QUARRELS WITH GOD

You quarrel with God
Because
God has not granted you
Realisation.
He quarrels with God
Because
God has not given him
Aspiration-kite.
I quarrel with God
Because
God has not given me
The capacity to manifest Him
Completely on earth.
God quarrels with Himself
Because
God feels that He could have created
A far better world.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Therefore You Failed...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:60
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEREFORE YOU FAILED

It was a poor preparation.
Therefore
You failed.
It was a bad calculation.
Therefore
You failed.
It was an abrupt depression.
Therefore
You failed.
It was an unwarranted surrender.
Therefore
You failed.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 Your Inner Weather...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:61
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

YOUR INNER WEATHER

Your inner weather
Is always foul
Because
You do not dream
High realities.
Your inner weather
Is always foul
Because
You are afraid to face
Your lifeless shadow
Alone.
Your inner weather
Is always foul
Because
You are fond of living
In your puny
Imagination-temptation world.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Expectations...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:62
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

EXPECTATIONS

As long as men are mortal
I expect nothing from them.
However, one thing I expect:
Their gratitude-flames.
As long as the gods are immortal
I expect many things from them.
But perhaps one thing will do:
Constant concern.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Do Doubt, Do Question!...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:63
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

DO DOUBT, DO QUESTION!

O my doubtful eyes,
When I think I am
Perfect perfection,
Do doubt my unprecedented discovery!
O my questioning mind,
When I act like a god,
Do question my authority!

#+END_VERSE


* 14 Teach Me...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:64
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

TEACH ME

O energetic ant,
Do be my master sweet.
Teach me not to waste time.
Teach me how to walk along
The patience-road.
Teach me simple, fruitful reality,
Misnamed stupid simplicity.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 My Friend, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:65
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY FRIEND,


MY FELLOW BEGGAR

When I am
With my Friend, God,
I come down
From Heaven to hell.
With my Friend
I change the face of hell.
When I am
With my fellow beggar, man,
I go up
From hell to Heaven.
With my fellow beggar
I look to see
If the sleeping God is awake.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 You Have Ruined My Reputation...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:66
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

YOU HAVE RUINED MY REPUTATION

Dear depression,
You have ruined my
Inspiration-reputation.
Dear frustration,
You have ruined my
Aspiration-reputation.
Dear delusion,
You have ruined my
God-perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 My Courage Supreme, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:67
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY COURAGE SUPREME,


MY GOD-DREAM

God needs me.
Therefore,
Today my courage supreme is reborn.
I need God.
Therefore,
Today my God-Dream becomes man-reality.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 Here They Are...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:68
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HERE THEY ARE

Truth is where you see it,
Love is where you feel it,
Delight is where you find it,
Power is where you invoke it,
Peace is where you need it,
Light is where you claim it.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 Alas!...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:69
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

ALAS!

Alas!
My brave Divinity-day
Has sunk in the chasm of night.
Alas!
My years have hastened
To their destined end.
Alas!
The Teacher of all teachers,
My inner faith,
Forgets to teach me
Regularly,
Divinely
And
Supremely.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 Whom Shall I Serve?...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:70
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHOM SHALL I SERVE?

Whom shall I serve?
A mortal master?
"Never!"
Whom shall I serve?
An immortal Master?
"Always!"
But where is He?
"He is inside your heart-cave."
Who is He?
"He is your active partner."
Who are you?
"His Eternity's sleeping partner."

#+END_VERSE


* 21 God's Attention...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:71
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD'S ATTENTION

Fear invokes
God's attention immediate.
Courage expects
God's attention immediate.
Doubt invites
God's attention immediate.
Faith treasures
God's attention eternal.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 They Have The Answer...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:72
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THEY HAVE THE ANSWER

Was God ever born?
My heart has the answer.
Did God ever die?
My soul has the answer.
Will God ever be fulfilled?
My dream-boat has the answer.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 Never Give Up...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:73
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

NEVER GIVE UP

Your life can be
Grown to perfection.
Don't give up.
Your soul
May replace God.
Don't give up.
God definitely shall make you
Another God.
Never give up.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 Unvisited...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:74
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

UNVISITED

Human fear
Is unvisited by God.
Divine courage
Is unvisited by human fear.
Human love
Is unvisited
By the seeker's fulfilling success.
Divine Love
Is unvisited
By the seeker's animal past.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 My School Lesson...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:75
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY SCHOOL LESSON

From the school
Of simplicity
I learned:
God is simple
And within my easy reach.
From the school
Of diversity
I learned:
Incomplete uncertainty
Is God's entire creation.
From the school
Of complexity
I learned:
God is extremity's
Futility.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 O My Dream-Boat, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:76
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O MY DREAM-BOAT,


O MY REALITY-SHORE

O Dream-boat of my joy,
Ultimately
You will reach the Reality-shore.
O Reality-shore of my joy,
Ultimately
You will make me another God.
Therefore
You ask me
To check my tornado-impatience,
And to enjoy
My earth-transformed
And
Heaven-born
Freedom.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 His Mistakes...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:77
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HIS MISTAKES

He sleeps too much.
Therefore
He desires.
He thinks too much.
Therefore
He cries.
He fears.
Therefore
He fails so soon.
He doubts.
Therefore
He dies so soon.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 God And I Are Even...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:78
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD AND I ARE EVEN

God and I are even.
I defeated God
By outliving Him
In the world of ignorance-sea.
God defeats me
By outliving me
In the world of gnostic-sun.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:79
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

LIFE

Harmless and noiseless life
I accepted.
Loveless and soulless life
I rejected.
Thoughtless and senseless life
I protested.
Godless and truthless life
I arrested.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 Futile Thoughts...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:80
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

FUTILE THOUGHTS

In the life of a desiring man
Satisfaction is a futile hope.
In the life of an aspiring man
Depression is a futile topic.
In the life of God-realised man
Life-negation is a futile task.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 Because...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:81
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

BECAUSE

Because
Of his diseased ambition,
He is drinking pain.
Because
Of his deceased goal,
God is inspiring him
And saying to him,
"Your destiny is blossoming.
Hear the clarion call!"

#+END_VERSE


* 32 He Hates...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:82
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HE HATES


AND


HE LOVES

He hates prosperity
Because
Prosperity harmed his humility.
He hates success
Because
Success ruined his progress.
He loves sincerity
Because
Sincerity never deserted him.
He loves humility
Because
Humility is God's universal
Oneness-light,
Oneness-might,
Oneness-flight.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 He Did Not Appreciate...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:83
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HE DID NOT APPRECIATE

He received joy
But
Forgot to appreciate it.
He achieved success
But
Neglected to appreciate it.
He received love
But
Refused to appreciate it-
Consciously
Deliberately
Shamelessly.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 My Food...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:84
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

MY FOOD

O desire-life,
Do you want
To eat my food?
Doubt-sighs are my food.
O aspiration-life,
Do you want
To eat my food?
Progress is my food.
O Realisation-life,
Do you want
To eat my food?
Oneness is my food,
My only food.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 O Emptiness...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:85
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O EMPTINESS

O emptiness of ages,
I eat your silence-food.
Therefore
To you I offer my gratitude-heart.
O silence of Eternity's Vision-dawn
You fulfil your sound-manifestation
Through my life's inner cry.
Therefore
To you I offer my certitude-soul.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 God Treasures...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:86
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

GOD TREASURES

Your mind's unparalleled
Vastness,
God secretly treasures.
Your heart's unparalleled
Sacrifice,
God soulfully treasures.
Your life's unparalleled
Love,
God sacredly treasures.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 O My Aspiration...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:87
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O MY ASPIRATION


O MY ASPIRATION

O my scorching desire,
Are you not my shameless disgrace?
"Yes, I am.
But God still loves me."
O my burning aspiration,
Are you not my fulfilling promise to God,
My eternal Father?
"No, I am not.
I am the mere Grace-servant
And Reality-son of God."

#+END_VERSE


* 38 Only That...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:88
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

ONLY THAT

Only what is permanent
Is free.
Only what is free
Is divine.
Only what is divine
Is fulfilling.
Only what is fulfilling
Is life-giving.
Only what is life-giving
Is God-becoming.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 Talkers...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:89
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

TALKERS

Body, I had no idea
That you were such a useless talker.
Vital, I had no idea
That you were such a great talker.
Mind, I had no idea
That you were such a secret talker.
Heart, I had no idea
That you were such a silent talker.
Soul, I had no idea
That you have never been a talker.
God bless you!

#+END_VERSE


* 40 World Plans...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:90
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WORLD PLANS

Your head houses
World-shaking plans.
Your eyes contain
World-devouring plans.
Your heart embraces
World-illumining plans.
Your soul treasures
World-surrendering plans.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 She Loves The World...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:91
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

SHE LOVES THE WORLD

She loves
The ungrateful world
Because
She knows that
The gratitude-flower
Is not to be found on earth.
She loves
The imperfect world
Because
She knows that
The perfection-moon
Is not to be found on earth.
She loves
The unaspiring world
Because
She knows that
It takes time
For aspiration to come into
existence-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 When They Are Poor...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

WHEN THEY ARE POOR

When education
Is poor,
Life is stupidity's struggle.
When illumination

* 44 Another Name...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

ANOTHER NAME

Mind, don't you know
That you have another name:
Insatiable curiosity.
Heart, don't you know

Soul, don't you know
That you have another name:

#+END_VERSE


* 45 Unworthy Ones...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:95
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

UNWORTHY ONES

Your unworthy disciples
Give you their ignorance,

#+END_VERSE


* 46 Thieves...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:96
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

THIEVES

O sweet thief in my heart,
Do steal away not only
What I have, but also
What I am.
From now I shall have
Only one need:
You, only you.
O swift thief of the world around
Who came to rob me,
Don't act like a fool
Don't empty
My venom-cup.
Your life of light shall die
Before it begins.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 Perhaps They Will...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:97
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

PERHAPS THEY WILL

Since
It is a good day to die,
Ask your old ignorance-friend
To die for you.
Perhaps he will.
Since
It is a good day to study,
Ask your Knowledge-brother
To study for you.
Perhaps he will.
Since
It is neither a good day nor a bad day,
Ask your Compassion-brother
To fulfil you.
Certainly she can and she will.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 Two Friends...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:98
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

TWO FRIENDS

When I sit
On the summits of my mind,
My old friend, Joy, leaves me immediately.
The joyless beggar
Helpless comes down.
When I play
In the depths of my heart,
My old friend, God, appears before me.
Two friends together drink
Nectar-light,
Two friends together become
Oneness-soul of Peace,
Oneness-goal of Bliss.

#+END_VERSE


* 49 Excuses...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:99
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

EXCUSES

God, we were fully awake,
But our pilot slept
All the way.
Therefore we are late.
God, our pilot was fully awake,
But we slept
All the way.
Of course he had
Our inner support
And
Outer approval
To place us
At Your Feet
On time.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 He Smiles On Me...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GB:100
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

HE SMILES ON ME

When I sleep
After my prayer,
My Lord smiles at my child-heart.
When I sleep
After my concentration,
My Captain smiles at my soldier-will.
When I sleep
After my meditation,
My Beloved smiles at my surrender-life.
When I sleep
After my contemplation,
My Friend smiles at my oneness-soul.

#+END_VERSE
