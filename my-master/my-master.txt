* Section One, My Master

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: section
  :CITE-KEY: MM-e1

#+BEGIN_VERSE

#+END_VERSE


* 1 My Heart Dies...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:1
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart dies
The moment it sees
My Master's sadness-tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 My Heart Sings, Plays...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:2
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart sings, plays
And dances
In ecstasy supreme
When it sees my Master's
Gladness-smiles.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Time Salutes And Stops...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:3
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Time salutes and stops
When my Master proudly expresses
His love, affection, sweetness
And fondness for me.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Every Morning...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:4
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every morning
My Master helps my heart
Wake up
With his heart's sunrise-beauty.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 My Master Cautioned Me...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:5
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Master cautioned me
And warned me
Of the tragic descent
Of my disobedience-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 O My Impurity-Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:6
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my impurity-mind,
Do not whine!
O my insecurity-heart,
Do not cry!
Behold, our Master,
With his compassion-flooded eye,
Is fast approaching us.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 When The Master Appears, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:7
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When the Master appears,
He immediately says
To my frustration-mind, vital and body:
"Stop!
Enough of your stupidity-pride!"
He then says to my heart:
"Enjoy no more ignorance-sleep!
I want you to accept
Some of my
World-transformation-responsibilities."

#+END_VERSE


* 8 The Master Appears And Says:...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:8
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The Master appears and says:
"I am sure this time
My iron rod will work,
Since up until now
My sweetness-flute
Has sadly failed."

#+END_VERSE


* 9 My Master Does Not Come Alone....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:9
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Master does not come alone.
He carries with him
His heart-home
So that his children
Do not wander any more
Inside their mind-jungles.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Master, Master, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:10
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Master, Master,
What is the difference
Between my faith in you
And your faith in me?
"My child, your faith in me
Depends on your sweet
Hope-fulfilment by me.
My faith in you
Entirely depends on
Your heart's love-strength
And
Your life's surrender-length."

#+END_VERSE


* 11 Master, Master, Over The Years...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:11
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Master, Master, over the years
I have given you so many things.
It seems to me that you have shown me
No sense of appreciation.
Master, this time I have
Two very special gifts for you.
I am sure you will enjoy them.
"My child, tell me, tell me.
I am all eagerness."
Master, this time I shall give you
My gratitude-heart
And my surrender-life-
Beauty-fountains.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Master, Master, Please Tell Me...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:12
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Master, Master, please tell me
How you love me
More than I love you.
"My child,
Your heart needs repair.
Leave it with me just for an hour.
I will do the needful."

#+END_VERSE

* 13 A God-Realised Soul...

#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-realised Soul
Is
Divinity's Lion-Prince
But
Humanity's donkey-slave.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 The Soul Of A God-Realised Person...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:14
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The Soul of a God-realised Person
Tries with God
And
The Heart of a God-realised Person
Cries with God
For the transformation
Of humanity.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 A God-Realised Soul...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:15
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-realised Soul
Is at once
God's Responsibility-Partner
And
Man's responsibility-frustration-reservoir.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 A God-Realised Person...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:16
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-realised Person
Is
Ninety-nine per cent underestimated
And
One hundred per cent misunderstood
By the elephant-ignorance-pride
Of humanity.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 A God-Realised Person...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:17
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-realised Person
Receives two gifts
From humanity:
Crucifixion-torture
From the desire-world
And
Glorification-rapture
From the aspiration-world.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 The Silence Of The Inner World...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:18
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The silence of the inner world
Tells the God-realised Soul:
"Wake up! Tarry not! Hurry up!
Run up and down
While serving God
Inside the heart of humanity."
The sound of the outer world
Tells the God-realised Soul:
"Shut up! Shut up!
Stay not here!
Pack up!
Go elsewhere!
Not here, not here!"

#+END_VERSE


* 19 The Immortal Motto...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:19
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The immortal Motto
Of a God-realised Soul:
Tomorrow's Dawn never begins.
Yesterday's Night never ends.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 A God-Realised Person...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:20
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-realised Person
Is at once
Humanity's thunder-kick-
Football-sufferer
And
Divinity's Bowling-Ball-
Winner-Dreamer.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 A God-Realised Soul...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:21
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-realised Soul
Swims in the sea
Of Divinity's Tears
And
Drowns in the ocean
Of humanity's frowns.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 Humanity Does Not Need...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:22
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Humanity does not need
A God-realised Soul
Because
He is not useful in humanity's eyes.
Divinity opens Heaven's Door
For a God-realised Soul
Only after He has accomplished
His supreme Task on earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 Divinity...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:23
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Divinity
Encourages a God-realised Soul
In His God-Manifestation-Hunger
By thunderous clapping.
Humanity
Discourages a God-realised Soul
In His God-Manifestation-Hunger
By ferocious slapping.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 The Heart Of A God-Realised Soul...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:24
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The Heart of a God-realised Soul
Blossoms inside
God's Immortality-flooded Smile.
The Life of a God-realised Soul
Dies inside
Humanity's jealousy-dragon-eye.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 A God-Realised Soul...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:25
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-realised Soul
Does not know how to
Tarnish and punish.
He only knows how to
Cry and try, again and again,
To see Divinity's Sun
In humanity's sky.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 Consolation Prize...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MM:26
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

CONSOLATION PRIZE

Finally, the Absolute Supreme
Tells a God-realised Soul:
"My child,
You are My own
Eternity's Vision-Eye
And
You are My own
Immortality's Reality-Heart."

#+END_VERSE
