* 32 Unlike Other Religions, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MLBG:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Unlike other religions,
The Hindu religion has a number
Of scriptures:
The four Veda-oceans,
The one hundred and eight
Upanishad-forests,
Teeming Puranas,
The Chandi (the Mother-glory),
And the Bhagavad Gita.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 But If You Ask The Same Hindu,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MLBG:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

But if you ask the same Hindu,
Of all these scriptures,
Which one touches the very depths
Of his aspiration-heart
And dedication-life,
In no time his answer will be:
The Bhagavad Gita,
The Song Celestial.

#+END_VERSE
