* 2 Power Devours....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power devours.
Love feeds.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Power Says To Me, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power says to me,
"Come here!"
Love says to me,
"Let us be together,
Always together."

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Power Says To Me, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power says to me,
"Go away!"
Love says to me,
"We are our Eternity's oneness."

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Power Does Not Know...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power does not know
What it is doing
And
Why it is doing.
Love knows
What it is doing
And
Why it is doing.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Power Is Superiority's Head....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power is superiority's head.
Love is equality's heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 Power And Truth...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power and truth
Are often strangers
To each other.
Love and truth
Are two mutual
Admirers and supporters.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 The Hands Of...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The hands of
Power
Are often destructive.
The hands of
Love
Are always creative.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 Power's Blindness...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power's blindness
Power is rarely aware of.
Love's oneness
Love is always conscious of.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Power, Name And Fame...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power, name and fame
Together march.
Destination: nowhere.
Love, oneness and fulness
Together fly.
Destination: Heaven's smile-blossoms.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 Power Is...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power is
Heaviness-haughtiness-mind.
Love is
Sweetness-fondness-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Power Is...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power is
Duty-emptiness.
Love is
Duty-willingness.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Power Is Narrow....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power is narrow.
Power is shallow.
Power is shallow.
Love is auspicious.
Love is gracious.
Love is precious.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 Power Does Not Think;...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power does not think;
Therefore, it sinks.
Love sings God's Songs;
Therefore, it is a God-bliss-singer.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 Power Is...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power is
Self-aggrandisement.
Love is
God-enlightenment.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 Ultimately, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ultimately,
Power and frustration
Are compelled to walk along
The destruction-road.
Eternally,
Love and fulfilment
Are favoured by God Himself
To walk along
The satisfaction-road.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 Power Tries To...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power tries to
Conceal its weakness.
Love spontaneously not only
Reveals its strength,
But also God's Power.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 Power Used;...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power used;
Gone, completely gone.
Love used;
Expands, multiplies.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 Power Keeps God's Grace...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power keeps God's Grace
At a distance.
Love not only values
But also treasures
Sleeplessly and breathlessly
God's Grace.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 Power...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power
Does not want to carry anybody
But wants to be carried
By the whole world.
Love
Carries each and every human being
From the cradle to the grave.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 When The World Suffers, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When the world suffers,
Power does not shed
Even a single tear.
When the world suffers,
Love becomes
A sea of tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 Power Is...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power is
Complicated-mind's wild adventure.
Love is
Simple-heart's cheerful journey.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 There Is Not A Single Day...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There is not a single day
That Power does not give trouble
To poor God.
There is not a single day
That love does not give joy
To its Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 When Power Wants To Speak To God,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When Power wants to speak to God,
God tells power,
"I do not have much time,
Tell Me everything in a nutshell."
When love wants to speak to God,
God tells love,
"Since Eternity is at My disposal,
I am eager to hear from you in detail."

#+END_VERSE


* 25 Power's Promises...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power's promises
Can never be taken seriously.
Love's promises
Are doubtless fulfilments.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 Power Takes A Tremendous Start, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power takes a tremendous start,
But never wins the inner race.
Love, no matter how it starts,
Always wins the race.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 The Stupidity Of Power...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The stupidity of Power
Wants to interview God.
The wisdom of love
Is always eager to pursue God.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 Unfortunately, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Unfortunately,
The very nature of outer power
Works unwisely, hurtfully
And maliciously.
Fortunately,
The very nature of inner love
Works lovingly, graciously
And self-givingly.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 When Power Conducts Me, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When Power conducts me,
I see God's Feet nowhere.
When love leads me,
I see God's Feet everywhere.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 Power Quenches The Thirst...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power quenches the thirst
Of my mind's eye.
Love feeds the hunger
Of my life's heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 Because Of Power's Supremacy, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Because of Power's supremacy,
Our world is heavy
With evening sighs.
Because of love's intimacy,
Our world is light
With morning songs.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 Power Misused Is As Useless...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power misused is as useless
As a handful of ashes.
Love properly used is as beautiful
And useful
As the starlit sky.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Power Is...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power is
Man's sweet dream-slayer.
Love is
Man's sweet dream-fulfiller.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 Every Day...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
Power brings embarrassments to God.
Every day
Love brings refreshments to God.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 Power Does Not Know...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power does not know
What self-offering is.
Love's very existence
Is founded upon self-offering.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 Power Has No Power To Change...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power has no power to change
The darkness of the world.
Love hurls not only defiance
At the darkness of the world,
But is absolutely certain
In transforming
World-darkness into world-light.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 Power Flies To Meet...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power flies to meet
With power's allies: super-powers.
Love travels to meet
With love's only Friend: God.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 Power's Bible...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power's Bible
Is for God's Justice-unbelievers.
Love's Gospel
Is for God's Justice-believers,
God's Compassion-dreamers
And for
God's Forgiveness-treasurers.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 Power Is...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power is
A mind-sophisticated frown.
Love is
A God-intoxicated smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 Power Lives Inside...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power lives inside
Its own volcano-laughter.
Love lives inside
God's sunshine-Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 Each New Beginning Of Power...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each new beginning of Power
Is world-punishment.
Each new beginning of love
Is world-enlightenment.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 Power's Palace...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power's palace
Has no God-entry.
Love's cottage
Has only a God-entrance all around.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 Power Dies...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power dies
In Power's own hands.
Love lives
At God's own Feet.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 Power:...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Power:
Who are you?
Love:
You I am.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 God's Justice...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: PWLV:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's Justice
Demands Power's
Immediate presence.
God 's Delight
Blessingfully offers love
Its immediate embrace.

#+END_VERSE
