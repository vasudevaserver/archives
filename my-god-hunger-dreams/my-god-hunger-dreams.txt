You cannot change your mind,

God-Realisation.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GHD:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No God,
No God.
Openly and proudly
He says.
God lives.
God is.
Secretly and sacredly
He feels.

#+END_VERSE

Go forward,
If you aspiration-heart
Is pure.
Go backward,
If your suspicion-mind
Is impure.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GHD:6

God the Justice
Unconditionally
Surrenders to
God the Compassion

ALWAYS.

Can we not adore
God's Forgiveness-Feet
Exactly
The same way He admires
Our sincerity-hearts?

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Deception ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GHD:8
  :END:

Deception
Is not something
New to the mind.
Aspiration
Is not something
New to the heart.
Illumination
Is not something
New to the soul.
Compassion
Is not something
New to the God.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:

  :keywords:

Satisfaction-smile from my heart
God is ready to spend
His astronomical
Compassion-Tears.

#+END_VERSE

God the Transformation-Hand.

#+END_VERSE

For the asking.

Of man.

* 55 My Lord Supreme, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GHD:55
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My soul-reality's All,
Countless times
I have withdrawn from You.
I have withdrawn
My promise-oneness.
I have withdrawn
My willingness-oneness.
I have withdrawn
My soulfulness-oneness.
I have withdrawn
My surrender-oneness-bliss.

Withdrawn from me.
You remain
Your Eternity's Compassion-Eye.
You remain
Your Infinity's Forgiveness-Heart.
You remain
Your Immortality's
Vision-Fulfilment-Cry.
