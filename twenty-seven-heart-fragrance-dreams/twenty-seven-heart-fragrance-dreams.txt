* One

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: HFD:1
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved Supreme,
What will I do
If You ever cancel
Your Forgiveness-Compassion-Credit Cards?

#+END_VERSE


* Two

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: HFD:2
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My love of God
Has many rivals
But no critic.

#+END_VERSE


* Three

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: HFD:3
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday God came to me
As Hope-Seed.
Today God has come to me
As a Promise-Tree.
Tomorrow God will come to me
As a Fulfilment-Fruit.

#+END_VERSE


* Four

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: HFD:4
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only my soul,
The baby-God in me.
Has the capacity
To properly love my Supreme,
The Father-God in me
And around me.

#+END_VERSE

* Seven

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: HFD:7
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind’s sound-prayer
Is my first resort.
My heart’s silence-meditation
Is my last resort.

#+END_VERSE

  :CITE-KEY: HFD:8

  :END:

Makes strict rules.

Makes innumerable exceptions.

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: HFD:11
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

Two things the mind
Does not want:
It does not want
To step back;
It does not want
God to lead.

#+END_VERSE

  :CITE-KEY: HFD:24
  :KEYWORDS:

An anxiety-invaded mind.

A peace-expanded heart.

#+END_VERSE


* Twenty-Five

  :PROPERTIES:

  :CITE-KEY: HFD:25
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

In my heart-industry.
But I always love to live
In my mind-factory.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: HFD:26
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God has forgiven me
So that I can once more
Love myself.

#+END_VERSE


* Twenty-Seven

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: HFD:27
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God is extremely proud of Himself
Only when He can run to and fro
Between my heart’s gratitude-tears
And my life’s surrender-smiles.

#+END_VERSE
