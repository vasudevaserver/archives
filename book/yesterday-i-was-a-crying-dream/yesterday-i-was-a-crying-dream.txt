* 1. Yesterday I was a crying dream

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   Yesterday
I was a crying dream;
   Therefore
God the Compassion loved me.

   Today
I am a smiling dream;
   Therefore
God the illumination loves me.

   Tomorrow
I shall be a flying dream;
   Therefore
God the Satisfaction will love me.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. Another name

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   Cheerfulness
Is another name for confidence.

   Confidence
Is another name for self-discovery.

   Self-discovery
Is another name for life-mastery.

#+END_VERSE


* 3. My inner and outer eyes

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   My inner eye
      Is callous.
It does not care
For the ignorance of my outer life.

   My outer eyes
      Are treacherous.
They told me they would
Take me to God-Light.
Alas, they have brought me
To death-night, instead.

#+END_VERSE


* 4. When I stand

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   When I stand
In front of divine eyes
   I become nervous
      Because they are so powerful.

   When I stand
In front of human eyes
   I become jealous
      Because they are so beautiful.

   When I stand
In front of God's Eyes
   I become pious
      Because they are so pure.

#+END_VERSE


* 5. All have failed

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

All my exciting plans
   Have failed.

All my stimulating ideas
   Have failed.

All my elevating ideals
   Have failed.

   Why? Why? Why?
Because I have not done
   The first thing first:
I have not loved God.

#+END_VERSE


* 6. O my mind

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my racing mind,
   Slow down.

O my shouting mind,
   Calm down.

O my suspecting mind,
   Stop your suspicion-flight
   Forever.
Then I shall claim you
   As my peerless treasure.

#+END_VERSE


* 7. Success and failure

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Success, I hate you;
I hate your shameless pride.
You have unimaginably decreased
My reliance on my Beloved Supreme.

Failure, I love you;
I love your glowing humility.
You have tremendously increased
My reliance on my Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 8. A closer bond with God

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Through my prayer
   I tried to make
A closer bond with God.
   I failed.

Through my meditation
   I tried to make
A closer bond with God.
   I failed.

Through my surrendering heart
   I tried to make
A closer bond with God.
   I succeeded.
      Not only that,
      But my surrendering heart
         Was embraced by God's Satisfaction-Life.

#+END_VERSE


* 9. Two things ruined

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Two things I have done wrong:

I have ruined
   The brilliance of time
With my unceasing indulgence.

I have destroyed
   The power of love
With my undying somnolence.

#+END_VERSE


* 10. Some have not failed

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   My other helpers have failed,
But not my faith.

   My other lovers have failed,
But not my sincerity.

   My other servers have failed,
But not my humility.

#+END_VERSE


* 11. The art of life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   Self-illumining
Is the art of divine life.

   Self-binding
Is the art of human life.

   Self-giving
Is the art of Eastern life.

   Self-transcending
Is the art of Western life.

#+END_VERSE


* 12. Thought-diseases

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   Impurity
Is a disease of thought.

   Insecurity
Is a disease in thought.

   Individuality
Is a disease with thought.

   Insincerity
Is a disease around thought.

#+END_VERSE


* 13. Action

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Action, action, action, action.
Action is aspiration-might.
Action is realisation-light.
Action is manifestation-right.
Action is perfection-height.

#+END_VERSE


* 14. His descending sun

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   I saw
His ascending sun.
It was so pure and sure;
Yet it has failed.

   I saw
His descending sun.
Earth and Heaven alike
Did not care for it.
And, to my surprise,
Neither did he himself.

#+END_VERSE


* 15. Too late

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I was young
Everything was within
My easy reach,
   But I made no attempt.
The older I grow
The more I know
What I could have done,
   But alas, it is too late.

#+END_VERSE


* 16. Allow me not to lose one thing

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Young I was;
I am now old.

Beautiful I was;
Ugly now I am.

Lord, I have lost everything.
But one thing, Lord,
Allow me not to lose:
   My love of You.

#+END_VERSE


* 17. Every day

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day my mind
   Is growing better.
Every day my heart
   Is growing brighter.
Every day my soul
   Is getting richer.

#+END_VERSE


* 18. They want

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   I knew
Poverty wants much.

   I know
Plentitude wants more.

   I shall know
Infinitude wants most.

#+END_VERSE


* 19. You know one thing

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   You know one thing:
Your human love
   Fails to grasp anything.

   You will know something else:
Your divine love
   Will far exceed
      All earth-Heaven grasp.

#+END_VERSE


* 20. Two giant apostles

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Two giant apostles of atheism:
   Doubt and insecurity.

I hate doubt
   Because
He thinks that I am not perfect.

I hate insecurity
   Because
She thinks that God does not care for me.

#+END_VERSE


* 21. Just because you are always forgiving

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   My sweet Lord,
I am terrible at remembering names.
   In Heaven I forget
Earth's sacred name: sacrifice.
   On earth I forget
Heaven's secret name: compassion.
But just because You are not terrible,
But are always forgiving,
I breathe in a shining corner
Of Your creation vast.

#+END_VERSE


* 22. You and he love war

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You and he love war,
   But both of you have
Different ideas about war.
Your idea about war is:
      Immediate destruction.
His idea about war is:
      Slow, steady, unending torture.

#+END_VERSE


* 23. God and I love peace

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   God and I love peace,
But both of us have
   Different ideas about peace.
My idea about peace is:
   Sleep -- an unbroken, endless sleep.
God's idea about peace is:
   Oneness inseparable,
   Oneness within,
   Oneness without.

#+END_VERSE


* 24. You are perfect

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My sweet Lord,
   You are great.
You have filled Your world
With ceaseless beauty.

My sweet Lord,
   You are good.
You have given me
The opportunity and capacity
   To enjoy it.

My sweet Lord,
   You are perfect.
You also care for me.

#+END_VERSE


* 25. I am totally exhausted

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, there was a time
   When I was totally exhausted.
My life was full of telephones
   To answer.
Lord, believe it or not,
   I did not get a chance
To catch one solitary moment
   To pray to You.

Lord, I am now really exhausted.
   I sincerely mean it.
My life is full of
   Exciting ideas
      And
   Tempting plans.
Lord, believe it or not,
   I do not get a chance
To catch one solitary moment
      To think of You and love You.

#+END_VERSE


* 26. Earth and Heaven

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   What is earth?
Earth is a place
Where I spend
   My action-filled days.

   What is Heaven?
Heaven is a place
Where I enjoy
   My satisfaction-filled dreams.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 A spark

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A spark of curiosity
Took me to God.

A spark of humility
Placed me at the Feet of God.

A spark of sincerity
Made me love and realise God.

#+END_VERSE


* 28. Stay wherever you want to

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   My sweet beloved Lord,
If You find it difficult
To stay in my mind,
Please stay at least in my heart.

If You find it difficult
To stay in my heart,
Please stay at least in my soul.

If You find it difficult
To stay in my soul,
Please stay wherever You want to.
I shall definitely
Come and meet You.
   I promise,
And I shall do it.

#+END_VERSE


* 29. I feel sorry for you

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yours is a quick and creative mind,
But awareness you lack.
   I feel sorry for you.

Yours is a meaningful and fruitful heart,
But purity you lack.
   I feel sorry for you.

#+END_VERSE



* 30. You created, you gave

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, my sweet Lord,
You created aspiration.
You gave me the opportunity to see it.

You created realisation.
You gave me the capacity to use it.

You created perfection.
You gave me the receptivity to have it.

#+END_VERSE


* 31. You say, I say

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You say that you enjoy loneliness;
I say that you enjoy selfishness.

You say that you enjoy isolation;
I say that you enjoy frustration.

You say that you enjoy a one-man show;
I say that you enjoy your insecurity-flow.

#+END_VERSE


* 32. The difference

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What is the difference
   Between
The life of art
   And
The art of life?

The life of art
Is man-aspiration
   And
Man-dedication.

The art of life
Is God-realisation
   And
God-manifestation.

#+END_VERSE


* 33. Welcome change

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Don't resist;
   Be flexible.
Don't be rigid;
   Be supple.
Welcome change!
Each change
   Is an illumining opportunity
To knock at the door
   Of fulfilling progress sweet.

#+END_VERSE


* 34. Everybody and nobody

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The difference
   Between
Everybody and nobody
In earth-bound consciousness
   Is this:
Everybody ceaselessly cries.
Nobody sincerely tries.

#+END_VERSE


* 35. Nobody and everybody

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The difference
   Between
Nobody and everybody
In Heaven-free consciousness
   Is this:
Nobody really becomes.
Everybody eternally is.

#+END_VERSE

  :cite-key: YWCD:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart, my heart,
We shall never part.

My mind, my mind,
We shall never bind.

My soul, my soul,
We shall ever remain in our Goal.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Teach me, teach me
   How to yearn.
Teach me, teach me
   How to learn.

Teach me, teach me
   How to face.
Teach me, teach me
   How to race.

Teach me, teach me
   Not to sigh.
Teach me, teach me
   How to fly.

#+END_VERSE


* 38. Divinely and supremely

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

   You can be divinely bold
Only when your heart is gold.
   You can be supremely sure
Only when your earth-life is pure.

#+END_VERSE


* 39. We can easily measure

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Heaven's soul-treasure
You can easily measure.

Earth's body-pleasure
He can easily measure.

Man's animal pressure
I can easily measure.

#+END_VERSE


* 40. Humility you need?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Humility you need?
   Then look at the dew drops.
Immensity you need?
   Then look at the blue-vast ocean,
Purity you need?
   Then look at the candle flame.
Sincerity you need?
   Then look at the child.
Duty you need?
   Then look at the moon.
Power you need?
   Then look at the sun.
Light you need?
   Then look at your heart.
Delight you need?
   Then sit at God's Feet.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Humanity is divinity
   Yet unrecognised.
Divinity is reality
   Yet unrecognised.
Reality is totality
   Yet unrecognised.

#+END_VERSE


* 42. I welcome a believer

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Why do I welcome a believer?
I welcome a believer
   Because
He knows that God is great.

Why do I welcome a lover?
I welcome a lover
   Because
He knows that God is good.

Why do I welcome a seeker?
I welcome a seeker
   Because
He knows and feels that God is perfect.

#+END_VERSE


* 43. Something between us

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are everything, my Lord.
I am nothing.
Can there be anything
Between You and me?

Yes,
My son, there can be something
Between you and Me.

What is it, my Lord?

My son,
   It is the union of your
      Surrender-height
         And
      My Satisfaction-Delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 44. The mission of an artist

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What is the vision of an artist?
The vision of an artist
   Is to offer a new life to the unborn.

What is the mission of an artist?
The mission of an artist
   Is to play with the unknown,
   Is to sing with the unknown,
   Is to dance with the unknown.

#+END_VERSE


* 45. What is surrender?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What is confusion
If not the sound of my pride?
What is hesitation
If not the sound of my weakness?
What is surrender
If not the acceptance of God-Light?

#+END_VERSE


* 46. God wants me to stay on earth

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Not because I love this life
God wants me to stay on earth,
   But because
He wants me to work for Him.

Not because He wants me to work for Him
God wants me to stay on earth,
   But because
He feels I have an inner need
   To love and serve and become one
      With the world of excruciating pangs.

#+END_VERSE


* 47. I shall cover you

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I shall cover You with appreciation.
I shall cover You with admiration.
I shall cover You with adoration.
I shall cover You with devotion.
Will You cover me with anything, God?

Yes, I shall cover you
With My Gratitude-Breath.

#+END_VERSE


* 48. I know how to sit at your feet

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, I know how to sit at Your Feet,
But I do not know
   How to live inside Your Heart.

Son, allow Me to remain inside your mind
   All the time.
If you allow Me to remain
   Inside your mind,
You are bound to feel Me
   Inside your heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 49. I prayed and I saw

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I prayed and I saw.
What did I pray for?
I prayed for God's Compassion-Light.
And what did I see?
I saw God's Compassion-Might
   And
Therein my perfection-height.

#+END_VERSE


* 50. I do not bow down to you

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: YWCD:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind bows down to you.
My heart bows down to you.
My soul bows down to you.
Even my God bows down to you.
Only I do not bow down to you.
      Why?
Because I know if I bow down to you
You will not sing
The song of self-transcendence.

#+END_VERSE
