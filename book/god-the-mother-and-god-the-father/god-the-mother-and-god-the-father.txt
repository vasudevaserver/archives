Of my nature

Of my nature
God the Father demands.

* 8.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: GMGF:8

#+BEGIN_VERSE

When God the Mother
Blessingfully comes to visit me,
She always carries with Her
Her Eternity's Patience-Depth.
When God the Father
Hurriedly comes to call on me,
He always carries with Him
His immediacy's Power-Height.

#+END_VERSE

"My child,
If you do not drink
Nectar-Delight,

"My son,
Like Me, you can and you must
Drink Nectar-Delight."

My son?"
