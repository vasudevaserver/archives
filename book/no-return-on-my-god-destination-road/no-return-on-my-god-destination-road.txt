* 1 Nothing Can Be Sweeter...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing can be sweeter
Than my life's
Unconditional God-surrender-sweetness.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 God-Love Is The Only Love...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God-love is the only love
That quenches
Our satisfaction-desperate thirst.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 If You Allow Your Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you allow your mind
To blindfold you,
How can you ever see
God's Compassion-illumination-Footprints
On your aspiration-life-shore?

#+END_VERSE


* 4 You Are Bound To Miss God's Boat...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are bound to miss God's Boat
Sailing to the Golden Shore
If your life is empty of
Smiling eagerness-surrender.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 O My Life's Service-Boat, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my life's service-boat,
On you sail at the lightning-speed
Of your willingness-mind
And your eagerness-heart!

#+END_VERSE


* 6 God Protects Me...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God protects me
From jumping out of His Boat
When I shun
My love of ignorance-choice.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 You Are A Rank Fool...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are a rank fool
If you want to love God
Only after waiting
For all other love
To fail you first.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 The Belief Of The Doubting Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The belief of the doubting mind
Is indeed bondage.
The belief of the self-giving heart
Is indeed oneness-freedom
And fulness-delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 For My Perfection-Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

For my perfection-life
I need only the God
Who reprimands my mind
And commands my heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 God Wants Me To Tell Him...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God wants me to tell Him
What I know about the world-heart
And not what I think of the world-mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 If You Are Happy...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are happy
With your fleeting moments,
Then God will make you happier
By blessing you
With His everlasting Day.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 The Courage To Live In God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The courage to live in God
I have.
What I now need is the courage
To live for God.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 If You Cannot Have Implicit Faith...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you cannot have implicit faith
First in God
And then in the spiritual life,
Sooner or later
You will collapse under the weight
Of worries and anxieties.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 God Does Not Know...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God does not know
The meaning of lending.
He knows only the meaning
Of giving, giving and giving.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 I Want To Live Between...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I want to live between
My soul's blessings
And my heart's gratitude.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 In The Spiritual Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the spiritual life
Every day what we need
Is the renewal of our
God-gratitude-fulness.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 My Lord, The Moment I Look...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord, the moment I look at You,
My desire-thoughts disappear
In Your Compassion-Light-Eye.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 The Human Childhood...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The human childhood
Does end,
But the divine childhood
Does not and cannot end.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 Our Desire-Mind Conceals...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Our desire-mind conceals
But our aspiration-heart reveals
The grandeur-face of our soul.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 My Lord, My Only Prayer...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord, my only prayer
Is to have You
In every heartbeat
Of my aspiration-breath.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 When I Please God In His Own...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I please God in His own Way,
He brings to the fore
A million Smiles from His Heart
To bless me.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 O My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind,
I want you to swim
Every day
In the sea of my life's
God-dedication-bliss.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 O My Seeker-Friend, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my seeker-friend,
You must realise
That your faith in God
Will definitely silence
Your mind's doubt-storms.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 Nothing Can Pierce...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing can pierce
The mind's happiness
As ruthlessly
As a negative thought!

#+END_VERSE


* 25 If You Are An Obedient Seeker, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are an obedient seeker,
Then God and God's Compassion
Will always remain
At your disposal.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 Every Day...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
God deposits His Compassion-Smiles
Inside my heart-bank,
And He never makes a withdrawal.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 How Can You Expect...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

How can you expect
God's Compassion-Eye
And His Satisfaction-Heart
To play with you
If you do not care
To obey Him?

#+END_VERSE


* 28 I Am So Happy...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I have reached at last
The point of no return
On my God-Destination-Road.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 O Pride, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O pride,
Do you have anything
To be proud of
Except your shameless stupidity?

#+END_VERSE


* 30 My Heart Says To My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart says to my mind,
"O mind, tell me
All about your plans.
In how many ways are you
Preparing yourself
To break my Master's heart?"

#+END_VERSE


* 31 I Am Fated To Doom...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am fated to doom
The moment I think of
Desire-fulfilment.
I am destined to bloom
The moment I meditate on
God-manifestation.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 God Has Himself Said...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God has Himself said
Many, many times
That His full manifestation on earth
Will definitely take place,
In spite of the blind unwillingness
Of unreceptive mankind.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Is There Any Human Being...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Is there any human being
Who does not want
Complete fulfilment
Without having to meet
All of God's requirements?

#+END_VERSE


* 34 When You Have A Free Access...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When you have a free access
To God's Heart,
Everything that you say and do
Will be from your firsthand
Experience-light.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 If You Reject Yourself All The Time,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you reject yourself all the time,
How can you be fulfilled?
Again, if you respect yourself unduly,
How can you be happy?

#+END_VERSE


* 36 God's Blessingful Smiles...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's blessingful Smiles
In our aspiration-life
Are always more numerous
Than we can ever imagine.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 If You Do Not Keep...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you do not keep
Your heart's aspiration-flames
Alive every day,
Then your soul's soaring dreams
Will fade into oblivion.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 O Seeker, Be Careful!...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O seeker, be careful!
If you want to continue
To please God in God's own Way,
Then never lose
Your aspiration-heart-door-key.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 If You Live Inside...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you live inside
Your doubting mind
And strangling vital,
Your God-manifestation-dreams
Will be completely shattered.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 No Desire Lives Forever....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No desire lives forever.
Therefore, do not surrender
To your desire-mind.
At God's choice Hour
Your own aspiration-heart
Will come to bless and embrace you.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 O Seeker, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O seeker,
God never wanted you to live
An unhappiness-attachment-life.
O seeker,
Since you are wallowing
In your weakness-enjoyment,
What right do you have
To criticise God?

#+END_VERSE


* 42 My Mind Has Given Up...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind has given up
Its bitterness.
It has invited the sweetness
Of my aspiration-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 God's Smile Does Not Want...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's Smile does not want
To destroy our mind.
It wants only
To illumine our mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 The Most I Can Do For God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The most I can do for God
Is remain silent
When the outer world
Does not understand
My love for Him.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 The Self-Giving Goodness...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The self-giving goodness
Of the heart
Is the pure beauty
Of life.

#+END_VERSE


* 46 The Sooner You Realise...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The sooner you realise
That expectation is your true enemy,
The better for you.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 O My Stupid Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my stupid mind,
How can you ever be happy
If you long for happiness
On your own terms?

#+END_VERSE


* 48 A God-Searching Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A God-searching mind
Needs sweetness.
A God-longing heart
Needs happiness.

#+END_VERSE


* 49 God-Necessity...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God-necessity
Will always be fed
By fulfilment-capacity.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 Do Not Forget To Return...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NRMG:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do not forget to return
God's Compassion-Calls
If you really value
His Eternity's
Golden Boat-Journey.

#+END_VERSE
