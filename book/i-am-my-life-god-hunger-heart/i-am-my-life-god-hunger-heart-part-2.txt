* 1 I Am...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:51
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am
My life's
God-hunger-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 Nothing Is More Important...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:52
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing is more important
Than my heart's sleepless cry
For God's Compassion-Eye,
His Protection-Feet
And His Forgiveness-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 My Lord, My Lord, My...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:53
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
From today on
I shall not cry anymore for Your attention;
No, not even for Your Compassion.
I shall cry only for my perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 My Lord, My Lord, My...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:54
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord, my Lord, my Lord,
I know that unless I satisfy You
In Your own Way,
My own satisfaction will always remain
A far cry.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Each Time My Mind Cherishes...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:55
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each time my mind cherishes
A divine thought,
God drops a Compassion-Drop
From His Fountain-Delight-Eye.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 No More Shall I Climb Up...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:56
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No more shall I climb up
My hope-mountain-heights.
From now on I shall live
Inside the heart of faith-fountain
And inside the breath of promise-sky.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 I Sail With Perfection-Oneness...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:57
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I sail with perfection-oneness
And satisfaction-peace
Only when I unconditionally love
My Lord Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 God Has Provided My Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:58
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God has provided my mind
With a thousand exits
To leave doubt-prison.
Alas, my mind simply does not want
To avail itself
Of this golden opportunity.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 Spiritual Awakening, Spiritual Feelings...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:59
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Spiritual awakening, spiritual feelings
And spiritual self-giving
Must be natural and spontaneous
And can never be forced.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 The Heart's Love For God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:60
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The heart's love for God
And the mind's obedience to God
Can and must go together.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 My Soul's Promise...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:61
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My soul's promise
And my heart's satisfaction
Walk, march and run together
Along the same road
To reach the Heart of the Unknowable.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Spirituality Is Not Self-Forgetfulness....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:62
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Spirituality is not self-forgetfulness.
Spirituality is self-awareness.
Spirituality is God-fulness.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 My Lord Beloved Supreme Loves...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:63
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme loves
Even my abysmal abyss Godless days
And my abysmal abyss Godless nights!

#+END_VERSE


* 14 O My Tormented Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:64
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my tormented mind,
I am my lover-heart.
Your days are numbered.
My protector-soul
Is fast approaching my lover-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 I Always Choose God's Needs...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:65
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I always choose God's Needs
Over my needs.
This is the only real way
To make myself and keep myself happy.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 I Never Want To Count...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:66
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I never want to count
My heart's aspiration-moments.
I just want to multiply
My life's dedication-hours.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 My Lord Supreme, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:67
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Supreme,
May I always be found
Between my prayer-life-beauty
And my aspiration-heart-fragrance.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 Ambition Kills Spirituality....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:68
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ambition kills spirituality.
Aspiration not only revives spirituality
But also energises and immortalises
Spirituality.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 My Life's Insecurity...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:69
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My life's insecurity
And my heart's satisfaction
Always love to remain
Strangers to each other.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 May My Prayer-Heart-Power...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:70
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

May my prayer-heart-power
Silence every day
My desire-mind-sound.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 I Am So Happy...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:71
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am so happy
That I now have the capacity
To compel my life to surrender
To my heart's
Secret and sacred God-hunger.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 God Wants Me To Be The Owner...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:72
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God wants me to be the owner
Of God's Smile-Beauty-Heart.
He does not want me to be the owner
Of my self-pity-tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 O Seeker, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:73
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O seeker,
You want to know what
Your insecurity, jealousy and impurity
Have done to you?
Your insecurity, jealousy and impurity
Have spread death-seeds
In your inner life of aspiration
And your outer life of dedication.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 I Am Divinely Proud...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:74
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am divinely proud
Of my mind
Because it is sleeplessly obedient
To God.

I am supremely proud
Of my heart
Because it is breathlessly grateful
To God.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 My Mind Thinks That Nothing...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:75
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind thinks that nothing
Has to change.
My heart feels that everything
Has to change.
My soul knows that everything
Is already changed in the inner world
And now is in the process of being changed
In the outer world.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 I Need God's Compassion Only, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:76
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I need God's Compassion only,
I need God's Affection only,
I need God's Love only
To be really, divinely
And supremely fulfilled.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 Even The Very Thought Of Gratitude...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:77
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Even the very thought of gratitude
Clears my mind,
Purifies my heart
And satisfies my life.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 Every Day...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:78
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
I keep my heart's door open
To impossibility's
Highest and brightest galaxies.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 My Prayer-Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:79
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer-mind
Is my miracle-power.
My meditation-heart
Is my satisfaction-tower.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 Each Human Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:80
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each human life
Has only two supreme necessities:
The God-foundation within
And the God-illumination without.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 Short, Shorter And Shortest...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:81
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Short, shorter and shortest
Is the span of my earthly fame.
Long, longer and longest -
Nay, birthless and deathless -
Is my soul's Heavenly God-oneness-game.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 While My Soul Is In This Body,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:82
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

While my soul is in this body,
I must learn the proper way
To love God, serve God and fulfil God
Here on earth and there in Heaven.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 O My Suspicious Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:83
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my suspicious mind,
I shall not allow you
To destroy the most precious
Faith-fragrance of my heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 True Love Lives...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:84
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

True love lives
Inside the life-garden.
True devotion lives
Inside the heart-flower.
True surrender lives
Inside the soul-fragrance.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 I Am Not Self-Indulgent...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:85
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am not self-indulgent
Only when I choose
God's Compassion-Protection-Feet.
Otherwise,
I am always self-indulgent.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 I Want To Be God's...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:86
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I want to be God's
Constant willingness-slave.
God wants me to be
His sleepless, breathless
Oneness and fulness-child.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 Every Morning...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:87
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every morning
My compassionate Lord Supreme
Tells me the same thing:
"My child, become an aspiration-grower
And not an expectation-collector.
I shall give you everything that I have
And that I am
At My choice Hour."

#+END_VERSE


* 38 Who Can Reverse The Course Of My...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:88
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Who can reverse the course of my life-river
So that I may reach the Golden Shore?
God the Compassion-Eye
And
God the Forgiveness-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 One God-Realisation-Hero...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:89
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

One God-realisation-hero
Absolutely can challenge and destroy
The pride of the world-ignorance-army.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 I Have Definitely Two Goals:...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:90
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I have definitely two goals:
My very short-term goal is self-giving.
My very long-term goal is God-becoming.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 Birthless Is The Heart-Beauty...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:91
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Birthless is the heart-beauty
Of my love
For my Lord Beloved Supreme.
Deathless is the soul-fragrance
Of my surrender
To my Lord Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 God Loses Faith In Us...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:92
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God loses faith in us
Only when our enthusiasm-mind
And eagerness-heart
Deliberately start limping.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 When I Was In The Desire-World, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:93
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I was in the desire-world,
I was forced by the undivine forces
To live in an abyss of nothingness.
Now that I am in the aspiration-world,
The divine forces are helping me
Climb up my realisation-pinnacles.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 God Laughs And Laughs...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:94
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God laughs and laughs
When I doubt Him to my heart's content.
God cries and swims in a sea of despair
When I cherish even an iota of doubt
About myself.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 God Loves Me Unconditionally...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:95
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God loves me unconditionally
Not because I have a great mind
And not because I have a good heart
But because, unlike human beings,
He simply does not know
And does not want to know
How to do anything else.

#+END_VERSE


* 46 It Is Never Too Late To Seek...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:96
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

It is never too late to seek newness.
It is never too late to achieve fulness.
What we need are
The heart's eagerness-tears
And the mind's enthusiasm-smiles.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 May My Faith-Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:97
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

May my faith-heart
Run faster than lightning speed
To arrive at my God-destination.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 God Wants Me To Simplify...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:98
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God wants me to simplify
My life-story,
God wants me to purify
My heart-breath,
And God Himself wants to glorify
My soul-song.

#+END_VERSE


* 49 At Least In My Case, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:99
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

At least in my case,
I am absolutely sure that
My endless fountain of creativity
Comes from only one Source
And that Source is
The unconditional Compassion-Eye
Of my Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 Only My Heart's Streaming Tears...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: IAML:100
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only my heart's streaming tears
Can and shall expedite
My Lord's Compassion-flooded Arrival.

#+END_VERSE
