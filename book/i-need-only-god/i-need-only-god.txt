* 1. I need only God

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I appreciate beauty
But
I need only God.

I admire greatness
But
I need only God.

I adore goodness
But
I need only God.

I love oneness
But
I need only God.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. That doesn't mean

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are pointed.
That doesn't mean
That you have to be piercing.

You are divine.
That doesn't mean
That you have to be demanding.

You are perfect.
That doesn't mean
That you have to be complaining.

#+END_VERSE


* 3. Do not abandon him

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do not abandon him.
If you abandon him
His ignorance will devour him.

Do not abandon his ignorance.
If you abandon the ignorance in him,
You are bound to see your beloved Lord
An unmistakable failure.

#+END_VERSE


* 4. There is no failure

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There is no failure in me
When God loves
My little world.

There is no failure in God
When I love
God's big world.

#+END_VERSE


* 5. Failure looms large

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Failure looms large
Only when
I withdraw my love from God
And
Allow not God to love me
In His own Way.

#+END_VERSE


* 6. You are divine

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

How will you know
If you are divine?
You will know
If you are divine
When you discover
That without your body's Himalayan achievements
The world can exist --
But not without your soul's
One little smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 7. Sworn to imagination-flight

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
I was sworn
To imagination-flight.

Today
I am sworn
To dedication-light.

Tomorrow
I shall be sworn
To Perfection-Delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 8. Strength

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Strength he desired
And strength he became.
And by becoming strength
What now he is within and without
Is light, boundless light.

#+END_VERSE


* 9. Duty

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, I shall do my duty:
I shall serve You unconditionally.
Lord, will You not do Your Duty?
Will You not love me unreservedly?

Lord, I shall do my duty:
I shall speak highly of Your Compassion.
Lord, will You not do Your Duty?
Will You not either conceal or illumine
My imperfection-night?

#+END_VERSE


* 10. Courage

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Courage, earth needs you.
Courage, Heaven loves you.
Courage, God blesses you.
Courage, I immortalise you,
The human and the divine in me
Immortalise you.

#+END_VERSE


* 11. God's sunrise

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday my soul
Was God's sunrise.

Today my heart
Is God's eternal day.

Tomorrow my life
Shall be God's Perfection-Light.

#+END_VERSE


* 12. God's pride

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Earth's height knows how to ascend.
Heaven's soul knows how to descend.
God's Pride knows how to run
Between
Heaven and earth
And
Earth and Heaven.

#+END_VERSE


* 13. A divine woman a divine man

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Her soulful tears
Proved that she is
A divine woman.

His powerful eyes
Proved that he is
A divine man.

#+END_VERSE


* 14. Neither matters

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Neither perfection nor imperfection matters
When God is with me.
Neither perfection nor satisfaction matters
When I am without God.

#+END_VERSE


* 15. Unworthy

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

"Lord, I am not worthy
To be loved."
Son, then you are not worthy
To be discussed.

"Lord, I am not worthy
To be remembered."
Son, then you are not worthy
To be considered.

#+END_VERSE


* 16. We are sad

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are forced to remain unheard;
Therefore
You are sad.

He is forced to remain unspoken;
Therefore
He is sad.

I am forced to remain underestimated;
Therefore
I am sad.

#+END_VERSE


* 17. An exception

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He is an exception.
His disciples meditate for him.
His disciples carry his messages to God.
His disciples forgive him
More than he forgives them.
He is an exception.

#+END_VERSE


* 18. Be true to them

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be true to God.
He is always yours.

Be true to man.
He can be yours, too.

Be true to yourself.
You need your true companionship badly.

#+END_VERSE


* 19. To perpetuate their memory

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To perpetuate the memory of God-Compassion
I still exist on earth.

To perpetuate the memory of man-perfection
I still exist on earth.

To perpetuate the memory of my life-liberation
I still exist on earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 20. The root of love

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The root of beauty is purity.
And
The root of purity is sincerity.
And
The root of sincerity is love.
But
The root of love is love.

#+END_VERSE


* 21. Be not afraid of him

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be not afraid of him.
Although his is a huge body
It will not harm you.

Be not afraid of him.
Although his is a powerful vital
It will not dominate you.

Be not afraid of him.
Although his is a vast mind
It will not ridicule you.

Be not afraid of him.
Although his is a pure heart
It will never, never ignore you.

#+END_VERSE


* 22. Another name

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Beauty is another name for Infinity.
Duty is another name for Eternity.
Reality is another name for Immortality.
God is another name for Satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 23. I am not sure

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I think my soul is uncertain
But I am not sure.

I think my heart is weak
But I am not sure.

I think my mind is blind
But I am not sure.

I think my vital is wild
But I am not sure.

I think my body is useless
But I am not sure.

#+END_VERSE


* 24. Only one Master

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I had many more lives.
I shall have many more lives.
But I have only one Master
And that is what I always want.
Who is my Master?
My own higher Self,
My Eternity's All.

#+END_VERSE


* 25. Do not speak ill of them

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do not speak ill of Eternity's Silence.
Eternity's Silence does need you.

Do not speak ill of Infinity's Light.
Infinity's Light does love you.

Do not speak ill of Immortality's Life.
Immortality's Life does shine in you.

#+END_VERSE


* 26. How is it?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

How is it that
You have
A popular heart
And
An unpopular mind?

How is it that
You have
A beautiful soul
And
An ugly life?

How, and also why?

#+END_VERSE


* 27. Superiors

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My soul has only one superior:
God.
My God has two superiors:
Forgiveness-white
And
Compassion-blue.

#+END_VERSE


* 28. Alone, I cannot

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alone, I cannot see God-Truth.
Alone, I cannot enjoy God-Peace.
Alone, I cannot love God-Beauty.
Alone, I cannot fulfil God-Duty.
Alone, I cannot be God-Satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 29. He is extremely rich

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the inner world
He is extremely rich;
Therefore
He can easily afford
To cry every day for God.

In the outer world
He is extremely rich;
Therefore
He can easily afford
To smile every day at man.

#+END_VERSE


* 30. I am ready to go

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am ready to go,
But I do not know
Where to go,
And nobody tells me --
No, not even God.
Alas, alas!
I am really ready to go.

#+END_VERSE


* 31. You are the only one

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My saviour, whom else do I talk to?
You are the only one I talk to.

My saviour, whom else do I love?
You are the only one I constantly love.

My saviour, whom else do I need?
You are the only one I eternally need.

My saviour, whom else can I call my own?
You are the only one
I unconditionally dare to call my own.

#+END_VERSE


* 32. Open your secrets to me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Open your heart-secrets to me.
I shall love you infinitely more.

Open your mind-secrets to me.
I shall immediately help you
To conceal and kill them.

#+END_VERSE


* 33. You are reality's choice

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are reality's choice.
What does it mean?
It means
That you are Divinity's voice
And
Immortality's Life.

#+END_VERSE


* 34. How can I hate you?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I shall leave you
If I have to.

I shall forget you
If I have to.

I shall forgive you
If I have to.

But I shall not hate you
Even if I have to.

How can I hate you,
Whom I have loved so devotedly
And so unreservedly?

#+END_VERSE


* 35. I really feel sorry

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, I do not feel sorry
That You do not know me.
But
I really feel sorry
That I do not know You,
You who are my only choice
In life and death.

#+END_VERSE


* 36. Lord, I love you divinely

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, I love You divinely
Not because You are divinely great.
Lord, I need You supremely
Not because You are supremely good.
I love You and need You
Because
You are my humanity's choice,
You are my divinity's voice.

#+END_VERSE


* 37. God's illumining eye

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's illumining Eye:
Where is it?
It is inside man's crying heart.

God's fulfilling Eye:
Where is it?
It is inside man's surrendering life.

#+END_VERSE


* 38. Determination

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Determination
Is the flower-moon
Of a dynamic vital
And the fruit-sun
Of a serving body.

#+END_VERSE


* 39. Listen to the Master

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Listen to the Master
If you really love yourself.
Listen to the Master
If you really want to own
The reality within you
And
The divinity that you are.

#+END_VERSE


* 40. Your reluctance-life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He is always ready to give you Ocean-Bliss,
But you fail to accept it.
You are afraid of its enormity,
You are afraid of its capacity.
Your reluctance-life maintains
Your old,
Untransformed
And undying
Breath.

#+END_VERSE


* 41. God's bark

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Hark, hark, God's Bark!
In its mouth is my ecstasy-sea.
In its voice is my liberation-sky.
In its life is my Perfection-Goal.

#+END_VERSE


* 42. Your biography

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What you were:
You were your body's might.

What you are:
You are your heart's love.

What you shall be:
You shall be your soul's flight.

#+END_VERSE


* 43. Heart's gratitude

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Your life's quietude
Is not heart's gratitude.

Your love's plenitude
Is not heart's gratitude.

Your surrender's unconditional infinitude
Is heart's gratitude.

#+END_VERSE


* 44. Your glory's height

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You do not have to go far.
Earth will devour
Your physical glory's height.
Earth will devour
Your vital glory's height.
Earth will devour
Your mental glory's height.
But earth will claim and treasure
Your psychic glory's height,
Which is another name for
God's Satisfaction-Might.

#+END_VERSE


* 45. Death's call

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Doctor, when your patient is dying
Do you hate death's call
Or do you enjoy death's call?
If you hate death's call,
The evolving will
In you and in your patient, prosper.
And if you love death's call,
The evolving will
In you and in your patient, suffer.

#+END_VERSE


* 46. Nature's Beauty Supreme

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A beautiful woman is nature's beauty.
A cheerful child is nature's beauty.
A soulful flower is nature's beauty.
A thoughtful man is nature's beauty.
But
A trance-bound silence
Is nature's Beauty Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 47. Where is your God?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O morning child, where is your God?
"My God is in my beauty's cry."

O afternoon child, where is your God?
"My God is in my purity's smile."

O evening child, where is your God?
"My God is in my satisfaction-face."

#+END_VERSE


* 48. Silence-life, sound-life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

"Lord, what is the difference
Between
Your Silence-life
And
Your Sound-life?"
Your life of soul
Is from My Silence-Life
And
My Sound-Life
Is for your life of goal.

#+END_VERSE


* 49. Plenitude

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When he walks,
Earth-plenitude he shares with the world.

When he sits in silence,
Heaven-plenitude he invokes and achieves.

When he sleeps,
He plays hide-and-seek with God-plenitude.

#+END_VERSE


* 50. His fire-silence

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: NOGD:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Energy we receive
From his fire-heart.

Speed we receive
From his fire-eye.

Goal we reach
With his fire-silence.

#+END_VERSE
