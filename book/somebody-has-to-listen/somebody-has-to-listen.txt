Your body's lethargy
And mind's frustrations
Are your
Most dangerous enemies.

It is infinitely easier
For Him to respond
To my aspiring heart
Than to answer
My questioning mind.

  :cite-key: SHL:24

The heart loves
Because to love
Is to become
The heart of goodness.
The mind loves
Because to love
Is to own
The thunder-sound
Of greatness.

#+END_VERSE

It is an impossible task.

Wants to unite the world,
It feels that
God has already done it.

#+END_VERSE
