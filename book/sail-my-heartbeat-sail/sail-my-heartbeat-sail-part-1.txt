* Introduction

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: dedication
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:1
  :END:


This series of rhyming poems was commenced on Sunday, 14 June 1998 in New York. The first volume was completed in three days.

It is Sri Chinmoy's ardent aspiration to offer 7,000 /Sail my heartbeat sail/ poems to his Lord Beloved Supreme.


* 1. Sail[fn:: SMHS 1-13. 14 June 1998]

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sail my heartbeat sail, sail
To clasp our Lord's Nectar-Mail.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. Choice hour

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

How can I not love
The beauty of a flower!
How can I not rise
At my sweet Lord's choice Hour!

#+END_VERSE


* 3. Rainbow-dream

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To devour the Dust-Gold-Feet
Of my Lord Supreme
Every morn my heart becomes
A rainbow-dream.

#+END_VERSE


* 4. Perfection-dream

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To be a chosen child
Of my Lord Supreme,
My crying-smiling heart's
Perfection-dream.
14 June 1998

#+END_VERSE


* 5. God-climbing fire

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Once more I shall become
My heart's God-climbing fire.
My eyes shall see nowhere
The face and body of mire.

#+END_VERSE


* 6. Impurity-insecurity

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sleeplessly I keep my mind
Free from impurity-ties.
Breathlessly I keep my heart
Empty of insecurity-cries.

#+END_VERSE


* 7. The right to choose

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Oh, what I have and what I am,
Am I ready to lose?
If so, my Lord Supreme tells me:
Him I have the right to choose.

#+END_VERSE


* 8. My dream

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mine is a birthless
And deathless dream:
To breathe the Heart
Of my Lord Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 9. Mind-tears versus heart-smiles

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My newness-mind-tears
Keep me glowing.
My fulness-heart-smiles
Keep me sailing.

#+END_VERSE


* 10. My gratitude-heart

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The fragrance
Of my God-gratitude-heart
Has smashed
My mind's ego-pride-dart.

#+END_VERSE


* 11. Ecstasy's endless road

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I see no more my earth-bound mind's
Despair-gloom-cave.
Ecstasy's endless road my life
Is ready to pave.

#+END_VERSE


* 12. Negativity-mind

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A ruthless
Negativity-mind
In every
Corner of my heart I find.

#+END_VERSE


* 13. Silence

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

From the Silence Unknowable
To the Sound knowable I came
To play and sing and dance
With a tiny, fragile frame,
And build a rainbow-bridge
Between Heaven and earth
For my sweet Supreme to travel
And flood all-where His Birth.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Whom I seek
Is within me.
I pine His
Heart-Smile to be.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:16
  :END:

Not my mind-cherishing life,
But my heart-blossoming breath
I need, I love, I treasure
To halt the pride of death.

#+END_VERSE

  :cite-key: SMHS:17

#+BEGIN_VERSE

Empty of heaviness-darkness
My mind, my heart today;
Behold, my soul-ecstasy
And I together play!

#+END_VERSE

Tell me what they are.
Their message
I share near and far.

My poetry
Tells me about its Heaven-Source.
Its sorrows
And joys -- my journey's course.

  :END:

My music
Tells me where to dive
And fly
To clasp ecstasy's drive.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:

  :END:

My sports-life
Shows me the pride of speed;

Transcendence-together lead.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:22

To be my Lord's choicest dove.

#+END_VERSE


* 22. My prayer

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer
Tells me Whom to beg only,
Deep within,
Wide without, sempiternally.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My meditation
Is compelled by God to be His Voice,
And only Him
To fulfil all-where His express Choice.

#+END_VERSE


* 24. My aspiration

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My aspiration
Tells me I can reach God's Eye,
And also God's Feet
In prayer-tears I can tie.

#+END_VERSE


* 25. My dedication

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My dedication,
My God-manifestation-bloom
To reserve a seat
In God's hallowed Heart-Room.

#+END_VERSE


* 26. My dear, sweet Lord

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My dear Lord,
How can I live
Without Your Life's Compassion-Eye?
My sweet Lord,
How can I breathe
Without Your Heart's Forgiveness-Sky?

#+END_VERSE


* 27. Forward and backward[fn:: SMHS 27-70. 16 June 1998]

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I look forward
To see my Lord's Face.
I look backward
To garner my disgrace.

#+END_VERSE


* 28. Soothsayers and truth-prayers

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Indeed, this mystery-world of ours
Is full of soothsayers;
Empty within, empty without --
Nowhere truth-prayers.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:30
  :END:

Each time
My happiness-heart smiles,
My life
Enjoys expanding miles.

#+END_VERSE

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:32

Desire from God His Peace-Award.

#+END_VERSE


* 32. Ignorance-life-tree

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Certainly we know
God loves the world through you and me.
We needs must fell
Our venomed ignorance-life-tree.

#+END_VERSE


* 33. Detachment-songs

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the morning,
My life and my heart together sing
Detachment-songs
From world-castigation.
In the evening,
My life and my heart together sing
Detachment-songs
From world-ovation.

#+END_VERSE


* 34. Soul divine and life human

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
My soul divine
Endlessly flies and flies and flies.
Every day
My life human
Pitifully cries and cries and cries.

#+END_VERSE


* 35. Complains and contains

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind
Complains, complains, complains.
My heart
My Lord's Fondness contains.

#+END_VERSE


* 36. What else?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What else is my desire-life,
If not exasperating?
What else is my aspiration-heart,
If not exhilarating?

#+END_VERSE


* 37. Two thirsty eyes

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Away
My Beloved flies.
Mine are
Two thirsty eyes.

#+END_VERSE


* 38. His Nectar-Rays

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In scanning the longest road,
My orphan eyes pass their futile days.
Where is my Beloved sweet?
Oh, where are His Nectar-Rays?

#+END_VERSE


* 39. The all-devouring night

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved sweet, let my sight
Dissolve in Your
Self-Effulgence-Light
Before You depart.
Helpless shall cry
The all-devouring Night.

#+END_VERSE


* 40. Mind-mart

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved sweet,
Your Love-Dagger has pierced my feeble heart
To raise my life
Far above mankind's wild confusion-mind-mart.

#+END_VERSE


* 41. Until You claim me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved sweet,
Until You claim me Your own,
My heart shall sob,
My breath and I shall moan.

#+END_VERSE


* 42. My heart-nest

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved sweet,
Your cruel Absence gives me no rest;
Empty of love
My sleepless, tear-flooded heart-nest.

#+END_VERSE


* 43. Indifference-absence

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved sweet,
In the indifference-absence
Of Your Compassion-Heart-Sky,
Do You desire
To know what I
And my heart do?
Ceaseless we pant, we sigh.

#+END_VERSE


* 44. I have exiled

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved sweet,
Drunk in devotion-smiles-tears,
I have exiled
My brooding self-doubts and fears.

#+END_VERSE


* 45. Your Dream-Boat

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved sweet,
Your Dream-Boat You want me to sail.
My promise august:
Succeed I must -- and never fail!

#+END_VERSE


* 46. Your abandoned child

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved sweet,
Why are You duty-free,
Careless, senseless and wild?
Tell me, who else if not me,
And me alone --
Your bewildered, abandoned child?

#+END_VERSE


* 47. God's right-hand man

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every soul
Is a God's right-hand man.
Every heart
Is a God's masterpiece-plan.

#+END_VERSE


* 48. Torture me not

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday,
How do you dare
To torture me today?
Can you not see
I have joined
My Lord's Oneness-Heart-Play?

#+END_VERSE


* 49. Easy solution

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Focus not on the dire problem,
But on the easy solution.
Live not on an empty promise,
But be a true man of action.

#+END_VERSE


* 50. My Lord expects

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:51
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord expects
My heart's devotion
To be fully empty
Of commotion.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:52
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Supreme
Cares not for my life's supremacy.
Sleepless He desires
My surrender-heart-intimacy.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:53
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My suspicion-mind
Makes everything intricate.
My aspiration-heart
Makes everybody intimate.

#+END_VERSE


* 53. A new way

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:54
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Discover an utterly
New way
To love your Beloved
Every day.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:55
  :END:

Where the Absolute Lord Supreme is.
But who cares,
If He gives me not an iota of peace!

#+END_VERSE

  :END:

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:60
  :END:

  :cite-key: SMHS:61
  :END:

* 61. Cry and sigh

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:62
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Why I do not even try;

Even to cry and sigh?

#+END_VERSE


* 62. Compassion-Eye

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:63
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To Your Compassion-Eye-Light;

#+END_VERSE


* 63. An ageless child

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:64
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Happiness is an ageless child
In my pure heart-garden.
Nowhere I see and feel
The world's heavy burden.

#+END_VERSE


* 64. The Sunshine-Love

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:65
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To limit God's infinite Love,
An impossibility-task.
Therefore, in God's Sunshine-Love
My life and I ever bask.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:66
  :END:

My soul-Immortality
Flies and flies.
My life-Eternity
Cries and cries.
My heart-Infinity
Expands and expands.
My mind-stupidity
Everything disbands.

#+END_VERSE


* 66. Popularity

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:67
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Perfection-popularity
We needs must need
Our divinity's God-hunger
Perpetually to feed.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:68
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord's Fulfilment-Command.
May my life be
With no expectation-demand.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SMHS:69
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Closes my temptation-door
And shows me the sunlit path

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem

  :cite-key: SMHS:70
  :END:

My mind God tamed.
Before I knew,

#+END_VERSE

#+END_VERSE
