* 1.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I can teach my mind
Every day.

#+END_VERSE


* 2.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I can think
Infinitely more
Of my aspiring friend,
My heart,
Than of my unaspiring
And disappointing enemies:
My lethargic body,
My aggressive vital
And my doubting mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 3.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That my life's deaf days
And blind nights
Have ended.

#+END_VERSE


* 4.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I shall never question
My inner captain:
My soul.

#+END_VERSE


* 5.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I may receive
Only God's Love,
Which the entire world
Can neither give me
Nor take away from me.

#+END_VERSE


* 6.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I do not forget
What I have
In my aspiration-world:
Peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 7.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I do not forget
What I am
In my realisation-world:
Love.

#+END_VERSE


* 8.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I remember
Only one thing:
God's Forgiveness
Every day saves me.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That God's Compassion-Sun
Never hides inside
My mental clouds.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I do not overestimate
My love of God.

#+END_VERSE


* 11.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I do not underestimate
God's Love for me.

#+END_VERSE


* 12.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That my pride
Will never exploit
God's Compassion-Sea.

#+END_VERSE


* 13.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That my impurity
Will never exploit
God's Forgiveness-Sky.

#+END_VERSE


* 14.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I may study
The art of unlearning
Every day
Consciously
And devotedly.

#+END_VERSE


* 15.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I can take off
Every day
Thirty-one minutes
From my mind-factory
To play with
My Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 16.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I do not disappoint
My Beloved Supreme
At the end
Of my journey's close.

#+END_VERSE


* 17.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
That I make a complete
And unconditional surrender
To my Beloved Supreme
At the very start
Of my Heavenly journey's dawn.

#+END_VERSE

* 29.

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:29

#+BEGIN_VERSE

My meditation
Is my life's sleepless
Willingness-surrender-gratitude
To my Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 30.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I meditate and meditate
And meditate
So that I can swim fast,
Very fast,
Against my mind's
Desire-current.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:31

I meditate to fulfil
My blue-gold soul-bird's
God-manifestation-dream
Here on earth.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I meditate to soulfully offer
My purity-heart's
Devotion-tears
And my spontaneity-life's
Dedication-smiles
To my Pilot Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 33.

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I meditate so that
I can directly learn
Hundreds of soulful
Heart-offering-songs
From God the Supreme Singer.

#+END_VERSE


* 34.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I meditate to be
In the loving company
Of my heart's mounting
Aspiration-flames
And my life's spreading
Dedication-fragrance.

#+END_VERSE


* 35.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

During my meditation,
I watch the dangerous fight
Between my mind's
Teeming doubts
And my heart's
Blossoming faith.
To my extreme joy,
My heart always wins.

#+END_VERSE


* 36.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

During my deep meditation,
My soul cuts off
The worthless insecurity-branches
Of my aspiration-life-tree.

#+END_VERSE


* 37.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Supreme
Has repeatedly told me
That He will not be able
To treasure my meditation
As long as I have
A complexity-mind,
An insecurity-heart,
A hostility-vital
And
An impurity-body.

#+END_VERSE


* 38.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Supreme
Has time and again warned me
That my meditations will be
Complete failures
If I am vehemently strict
With others' imperfections
And extremely indulgent
With my own imperfections.

#+END_VERSE


* 39.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My soul is telling me
That only during
My soulful meditation
Can it forcefully stop
My wild vital from travelling
On dangerous desire-highways.

#+END_VERSE


* 40.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My meditation reaches
Its acme of perfection
Only when it sees
God's Compassion-Eye,
God's Forgiveness-Heart
And
God's Protection-Feet.

#+END_VERSE


* 41.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the small hours of the morning,
With my heart-purity's
Soulfulness-steps,
I embark on my meditation-journey.

#+END_VERSE


* 42.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I unconditionally meditate,
God proudly drives me
In His Concern, Affection, Love
And Fondness-Limousine.

#+END_VERSE


* 43.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I unconditionally meditate,
God makes me
His ever-transcending
Dream-Reality's perfection-partner.

#+END_VERSE


* 44.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I unconditionally meditate,
God asks me to stand beside Him
While He is watching
His universal creation
From His transcendental
Vision-Balcony.

#+END_VERSE


* 45.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I extol myself to the skies,
Because of my stupendous
Success-life,
My meditation-friend-commander
Commands my volcano-pride
To fall dead
At my sweet Lord's
Forgiveness-Feet.

#+END_VERSE


* 46.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My high meditation says
To my heart,
"Who tells you, my dear friend,
That you are always
Pitifully encaged?
Not true, not true."

#+END_VERSE


* 47.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My good meditation says
To my mind,
"Who tells you, my dear friend,
That you are always at the mercy
Of venomous doubts?
Not true, not true."

#+END_VERSE


* 48.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My deep meditation says
To my vital,
"Who tells you, my dear friend,
That you are always wildly enraged?
Not true, not true."

#+END_VERSE


* 49.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My silent meditation says
To my body,
"Who tells you, my dear friend,
That you are always a victim
To worthless and useless lethargy?
Not true, not true."

#+END_VERSE


* 50.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I have
A most illumining meditation,
My Lord Supreme smilingly,
Compassionately and blessingfully
Tells me,
"My child, take anything you want
Free of charge
From My Infinity's Treasure-Shop."

#+END_VERSE

* 52.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:52
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer and God
Cry together.
My meditation and God
Smile at each other.

#+END_VERSE


* 53.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:53
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer is my faith
In God.
My meditation is God's faith
In me.

#+END_VERSE


* 54.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:54
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer says,
"God, where are You?"
My meditation says,
"God, where are You not?"

#+END_VERSE


* 55.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:55
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer tells me
That the road to God
Is sincerity.
My meditation tells me
That the road to God
Is God Himself.

#+END_VERSE


* 56.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:56
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer is God's Concern
For me.
My meditation is my concern
For God.

#+END_VERSE


* 57.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:57
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer is my mind's
Future smile.
My meditation is God's
Present Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 58.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:58
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I pray, God asks me
To keep my mouth shut
And open my heart.
When I meditate, God sees
That my heart's door
Is wide open.

#+END_VERSE


* 59.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PLMH:59
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

#+END_VERSE
