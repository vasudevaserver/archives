* 1. To Be

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SCFS:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To be, indeed, a cosmic god,
I shall try only to succeed.
To be, indeed, a real man,
I shall try only to proceed.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. Sing Me A Song

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SCFS:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sing me a song
My Lord Supreme,
I shall give You
My silver dream,
I shall give You
My beauty's sky,
I shall give You
My oneness-tie.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SCFS:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Beautiful emptiness
Soulful readiness
Realisation newness
Perfection oneness

I need, I need, I need
My God and my God-need to feed.

#+END_VERSE

You have won my heart.
You have conquered death-dart
In life's confusion-mart.

#+END_VERSE

My Lord Supreme."

In my moment nothing lives.

To reach His Vision-sky.

Just before tomorrow's sunrise.

God's Promise, I execute.

Yet
Its pride vaster than the sky.

It knows not how to die.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SCFS:19

My life, done with the material-life-night,
Has now embraced
The life of renunciation-light.
Meaningless and fruitless
Are the material needs.
Nowhere to be found
God-satisfaction-seeds.

#+END_VERSE

To prove my worth.

Of Titan-dart.

#+END_VERSE

I have totally lost.

And live therein to last.

#+END_VERSE

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SCFS:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Defects of my desires:
They do not want to die,
But they die.
They want to fly,
But they cannot fly.
They want to bind my heart,
Behold, they fail.
To the unhorizoned shore
My boat I sail.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: SCFS:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Any day, any hour, my supreme Guest
Will surely come with His
All loving Compassion-Nest.

#+END_VERSE

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God is hospitable,
Therefore
No anxiety, no worry
Have I.
I shall give Him
What I have preserved:
Gratitude-sky.

#+END_VERSE

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Possibility,
What is it after all?
Uncertainty --
Ant-weak, giant-stout,
Within, without.
That's all; that's all.

#+END_VERSE

  :cite-key: SCFS:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A sea of plenty
I conceive,
Believe it or not.
But
Alas
Nothing I receive,
Nothing I achieve.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Farewell, farewell,
O ungrateful world.
So much I have done for you,
So highly I have thought of you.
To please you, your all,
I totally forgot
To respond to God's God-Call.

#+END_VERSE

For the poor and feeble souls.

For the lovers of myriad goals.

#+END_VERSE

Alas, alas!

Ignorance deep.

The wild music
Of ignorance-life.

Transformation-flame.

To help me reach my goal.

Of clamour-din.

Supremely fruitful.

I fight;

I fight;
Desire-worlds I permit not
To throng.

#+END_VERSE

I badly need.
The life of power
Who wants?
Not me, not me.
Although
Power wants to bind me,
I shall remain
At easy ease,
Eternally free.

#+END_VERSE

I need,

And reach.
