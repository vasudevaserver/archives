But even that one prayer
You never sanction!

#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
How can you be
so heartlessly cruel to me?

Why do You not give me any chance
To smile at You,
To make my heart
Infinitely sweeter
And my life
Infinitely richer?

#+END_VERSE
