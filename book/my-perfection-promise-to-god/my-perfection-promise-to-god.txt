#+BEGIN_VERSE

My prayer
Is my perfection-promise
To God.
My meditation
Is my God-satisfaction
In God's own Way.

#+END_VERSE

To remember
God's powerful Eye.

To remember
God's blissful Heart.

Universally happy.

God has revoked
My desire-car-license.

A free aspiration-plane-ticket
To fly all over the world.

#+END_VERSE

#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind pointedly reminds me
Of all the things
That God has not done for me.
My heart lovingly reminds me
Of all the things
That God has unconditionally
Done for me.

#+END_VERSE

#+BEGIN_VERSE

I see a new world
Of peace and bliss
Emerging from my age-old
Mind-hallucinations.
This is what
My compassion-heart
Has done for me.

#+END_VERSE

Inside your boasting mouth.

To arriving at
Your God-Destination.

I see my Lord's

I see my Lord's
Fulfilled Dreams.

When I live inside
My Lord's Heart-Garden,
I feel that each time
I breathe in and breathe out
A new
God-realisation-hope
And a new
God-manifestation-promise
Have become
My two real friends.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:

#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
Why do I not cheerfully,
Gratefully and sleeplessly
Allow God's Compassion-Light
To enter into my heart-room
Faster than the fastest
To turn me into
His peace-blossoming tree?

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MPPG:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I sleeplessly
Offer myself to God,
He tells me
I have now become
A true member of
His intimate and inner Circle.

#+END_VERSE


* 17.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MPPG:17

#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mine is the sound-life
That God uses to produce
His Victory here on earth.
Mine is the silence-heart
That God uses to transcend
His own Infinity's
Self-Transcendence-Dreams.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MPPG:18

#+BEGIN_VERSE

My searching mind
Is sincerely looking
For the heart-garden
Of the God-seekers.
My aspiring heart
Is selflessly looking
For the full manifestation
Of the sleepless God-dreamers.

#+END_VERSE


* 19.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MPPG:19

#+BEGIN_VERSE

My earth-responsibilities
Are my Heaven-sent
Opportunities
To bring to the fore
My divinity's prosperity
And offer it
To the heart of humanity
For the manifestation
Of God's Peace,
God's Bliss
And God's Satisfaction
On earth.

#+END_VERSE
