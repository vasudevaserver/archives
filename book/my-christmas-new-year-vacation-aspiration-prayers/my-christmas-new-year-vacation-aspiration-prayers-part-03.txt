/Sri Chinmoy offered the following morning prayers during his visit to Brazil and Paraguay in December 1999 and January 2000. Ten different groups recited the prayers turn by turn three times, then twice, then once./

7 JANUARY 2000

FOZ DO IGUAÇU, BRAZIL



* 1 I Know, I Know, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:236
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I know, I know,
Definitely I know the way
To my Lord Beloved Supreme,
Even in the stark darkness
Of my ignorance-night.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 No More Towering Promises, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:237
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No more towering promises,
But flowering
God-manifestation-fulfilments-
More, ever more.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Every Day ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:238
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Devours the golden tears
Of my silver heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 To My Great Astonishment, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:239
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To my great astonishment,
Today delight is dancing
From my lotus-heart
To my rose-eyes.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Every Day Without Fail ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:240
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day without fail
I ply my life-boat
Between my resolute mind-river
And my aspiring heart-river.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 God's Hand, God's Eye ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:241
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's Hand, God's Eye
And God's Heart
Are unconditionally showing me the way
To Eternity's Peace and Infinity's Bliss.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 My Lord, Today I Have ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:242
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord, today I have
Two very special presents for You,
And I am placing them
Cheerfully and unconditionally
At Your Feet:
My tearful heart and my grateful life.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 God Does Not Like At All ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:243
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God does not like at all
My fearful and doubtful mind.
God immensely loves
My tearful and blissful heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 My Heart's Aspiration-Cries ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:244
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart's aspiration-cries
And
My life's dedication-smiles
Thrive only
In my God-surrender-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 I Keep My Ever-Blossoming ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:245
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I keep my ever-blossoming
Gratitude-heart
Safe in the homeland
Of my soul.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:248
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme
Allows me to feed Him sumptuously
Only when my aspiration-heart
Flies at top speed.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 God Has Been Telling Me All Along...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:249
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God has been telling me all along
That self-pity
Is the worst possible crime
In a seeker's life.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 My Mind Has Been Studying For Years...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:250
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind has been studying for years
To receive a doubt-diploma.
My heart has been studying
For years and years
To receive God-love, God-devotion
And God-surrender-diplomas
From God Himself.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 May The Smiles Of My Inner Sun...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:251
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

May the smiles of my inner sun
Illumine the dense clouds
Of my outer mind-sky.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 We Cry And Cry ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:252
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We cry and cry
For the Compassion-Moon
Of God's Eye.
God cries and cries
For the satisfaction-sun
Of our heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 When Our Aspiration Is On The Rise,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:253
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When our aspiration is on the rise,
Paradise lovingly, cheerfully and proudly
Beckons us.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 God Tells Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:254
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God tells me
That my expectations and frustrations
Do not have the last word.
He also tells me
That my love of God
Has ever the last word.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 Today I Have Made ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:255
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Today I have made
A solemn promise to myself
That I shall not compel my Lord Supreme
To go hungry for my smile.

#+END_VERSE

Is meant to be ever-transcending
In his God-search
And God-discovery.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:260
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must realise by this time
That to try to perfect others
Is, indeed, a blind
And hopeless quest.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:261

A long-forgotten
World-attachment-story.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 All Good Seekers Are Commanded ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:262
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

All good seekers are commanded
By God Himself
To answer His Heart-Calls
On the very first ring.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 To Run Away ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:263
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To run away
With the mind's freedom
Is to lose one's own
Heart-wisdom.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 My Heart, Keep Climbing, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:264
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart, keep climbing,
Keep climbing,
Even if on rare occasions
You slide down.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 Only A Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:265
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only a heart
Of sleepless God-surrender
Knows the way
To God's blissful Eye,
God's peaceful Heart
And God's powerful Feet.

#+END_VERSE

* 41 My Lord, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:276
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
Although my spiritual progress
Is most deplorable,
I beg of You a million times:
Please, please do not allow me
To go back to my old,
Desire-bound, ordinary life.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:277
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Everything that I have
And everything that I am.
Be pleased to start with
My abominable pride.

#+END_VERSE
