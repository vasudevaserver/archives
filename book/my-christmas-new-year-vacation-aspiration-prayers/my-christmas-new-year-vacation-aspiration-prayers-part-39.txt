  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3138

A disobedience-mind.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:

  :PROPERTIES:

God’s Satisfaction-Heart
