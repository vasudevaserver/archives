/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Christchurch, New Zealand and Cairns, Australia in December 2002 and January 2003./

/27 DECEMBER 2002/

/CHRISTCHURCH, NEW ZEALAND/



* 1 No Limit ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1564
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No limit
To what my love, devotion
And surrender can do,
Not only for me,
But also for the entire world.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 Keep Singing, Keep Singing! ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1565
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Keep singing, keep singing!
God will soon be arriving.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 If I Continue Calling ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1566
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If I continue calling,
My life will blossom
And my Lord Supreme
Will smile, sing and dance.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 The Exquisite Bliss ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1567
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The exquisite bliss
Of my God-surrender-life
Nobody on earth
Can hamper.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 My Master’s Tears And Smiles ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1568
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Master’s tears and smiles
Are the only members
Of my heart-family.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 The Desiring Mind ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1569
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The desiring mind
Is apt to brood.
The aspiring heart
Is apt to bleed.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 God’s Compassion-Eye Intervenes ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1570
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s Compassion-Eye intervenes
Between
Man’s Himalayan blunders
And his well-deserved punishment.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 God’s Compassion-Confidence In Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1571
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s Compassion-Confidence in me
Is my only
Competence-victory.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 My Lord ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1572
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
Your ceaseless Tears I see
Not only in my heart,
But also in every heart.
My Lord,
Your Nectar-Smiles I see
Nowhere, nowhere, nowhere.

#+END_VERSE

* 10 I Give My Lord Supreme ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1573
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I give my Lord Supreme
Unimaginable joy and pride
When I am able
To steal His Compassion-Eye
And His Forgiveness-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 To Protect Me...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1574
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To protect me
From the undivine forces,
My Beloved Supreme
Has kept His Eye and His Heart
As my sentinels at my heart-gate.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Every Moment Is A Golden Opportunity ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1575
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every moment is a golden opportunity
For me to love and serve
The tears and the smiles of the world.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 I Must Disregard And Discard ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1576
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must disregard and discard
What my mind thinks of me
And says about me.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 I Do Not Go To The Sky-Moon -- ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1577
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I do not go to the sky-moon --
I go to my heart-moon
For it to inspire me
And inundate me
With Infinity’s Peace and Bliss.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 I Have Many Desires ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1578
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I have many desires,
But my Lord has only one:
My constant happiness.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 Day And Night ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1579
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Day and night
My soul is helping me
To discount all my worries
And anxieties.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 God Loves ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1580
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God loves
My very, very short-distance prayers
And not
My ultramarathon-distance prayers.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 God’s Unseen Hand ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1581
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s unseen Hand
Most lovingly
And most compassionately
Takes my hands
To show me the way
To the Golden Shore.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 My Greatness Depends On ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1582
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My greatness depends on
The acceptance of the world.
My goodness depends on
The Love and Concern
Of my Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1587
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

With His ever-increasing Heart-Tears
And
With His ever-increasing Soul-Smiles.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 I Must Become A Perfect Stranger ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1588
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must become a perfect stranger
To ignorance-sleep
So that I can grow into
A God-lover
Of the highest magnitude.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1589
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Has only one task to perform:
Awake!

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1590
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The divine in me
Has three tasks to perform:
Bravely march forward,
Sleeplessly climb upward,
Breathlessly dive inward.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1591

Man-Enlightenment-Whisper.

  :genre: prayer

  :cite-key: APR:1592

A fragile faith in God.

  :keywords:

#+END_VERSE


* 38 During My Deepest Meditation ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1601
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

During my deepest meditation,
I hear my Lord Beloved Supreme
Secretly whispering:
“My child, you were born to be
My Eternity’s Greatness,
My Infinity’s Goodness
And My Immortality’s Fulness.
My child, this message
Is not only for you,
But it is for each and every
Creation of Mine.”

#+END_VERSE

#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved Supreme,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
Tells me that my entire life
Is a most powerful song-bridge
Between humanity’s tears
And Divinity’s Smiles.

#+END_VERSE
