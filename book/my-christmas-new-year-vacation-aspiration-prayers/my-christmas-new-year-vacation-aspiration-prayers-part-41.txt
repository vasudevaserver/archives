/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Pangkor Island, Malaysia in November and December 2005./

/30 November 2005/

/Pangkor Island, Malaysia/



* 1 I Can And I Must Learn ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3304
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I can and I must learn
How to live a perfect life
Inside my Lord’s Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 The Fresh Morning Dew ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3305
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The fresh morning dew
Awakens
My sleeping aspiration-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 God Wants Only One Thing ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3306
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God wants only one thing
From me:
A ceaseless and breathless
God-aspiration-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 At A Distance ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3307
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

At a distance
I imagine God’s Feet
Until the day arrives
When I can place myself
At God’s Feet.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 God Wants Me To Please Him ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3308
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God wants me to please Him
In a prayerful and soulful way,
And not in an astounding way.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 God’s Compassion-Rain ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3309
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s Compassion-Rain
Falls
To enthral my heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 No Mind-Ignorance ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3310
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No mind-ignorance
Can stop
My Heaven-climbing
Journey’s start.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 A Happy Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3311
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A happy heart
Is
A God-given capacity
To spread God’s Light
Freely and ceaselessly.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 This Time When God Sends Away ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3312
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

This time when God sends away
My ignorance-pride,
I shall definitely not
Bring it back.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 I Pray And Meditate ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:3313
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray and meditate
Inside my heart’s
Sweetness-devotion-moon.

#+END_VERSE
