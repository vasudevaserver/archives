Of a new
Consciousness-delight-dream.

Has to be made here and now.

Shall be granted.

But my inner faith
That decides who God is,
Where God is
And what God actually expects
From my life.

Pride-crown-destination.

* 10 I Must Examine ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1243
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must examine
The beauty and fragrance
Of my own happiness-heart
Before I offer them
To humanity’s heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 My Knowledge-Light ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1244
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My knowledge-light
Is the quantity-beggar.
My wisdom-delight
Is the quality-chooser.
My Lord Supreme tells me
Not to worry.
He will gladly take care of
The beggar in me
And the chooser in me.
I just have to love Him only
And need Him only.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 To Me, The Highest Height ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1245
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To me, the highest height
Is not the happiest privilege,
But the greatest and heaviest
Responsibility.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 My Lord Has Chosen ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1246
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord has chosen
My utterly insignificant life
To accomplish the impossible:
The full manifestation of God
Here on earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 If My Life Really Desires ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1247
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If my life really desires
To be a God-server,
Then God’s Heart will be
My life’s untiring supplier.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 I Measure My Life-Boundaries ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1248
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I measure my life-boundaries.
My Lord counts only
My heart-garden-flowers.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 There Are God-Doubters ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1249
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There are God-doubters.
There are God-believers as well.
Who can prevent me
From being a God-believer,
And who can prevent me
From being a God-lover,
A God-server
And, finally, a God-manifester?

#+END_VERSE


* 17 In God’s Eye ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1250
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In God’s Eye
I am never insignificant.
In God’s Heart
I am eternally and infinitely
More significant
Than I can ever imagine.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 My Life-Book Begins With ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1251
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My life-book begins with
The God-aspiration-chapter
And ends with
The God-realisation-chapter.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 The Heart Has Another Name ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1252
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The heart has another name:
Aspiration.
The mind has another name:
Suspicion.
The vital has another name:
Restlessness.
The body has another name:
Idleness.
Life has another name:
Uncertainty.
Man has another name:
Complexity.
The soul has another name:
Observer.
God has another Name:
Forgiver.

#+END_VERSE

* 21 My Mind Speaks To Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1254
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind speaks to me.
I immediately obey.
My vital speaks to me.
I proudly obey.
My body speaks to me.
I lovingly obey.
Alas, when shall I stop
Obeying them, when?
Alas, when shall I be
The choice instrument
Of my Lord Supreme, when?

#+END_VERSE

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1256

I shall never, never, never desire:
God-disobedience.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1257
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1258

All the time.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1259
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My God-defiance
Bravely marches along the road
That has no goal.
My God-disobedience
Proudly follows my God-defiance.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 We Cannot Merit ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1260
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We cannot merit
God’s Transcendental Consciousness
And His Universal Consciousness,
But we can inherit and do inherit
His Transcendental Consciousness
And His Universal Consciousness.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 My Outer Beauty ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1261
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My outer beauty
Attracts and welcomes
All the hostile forces of the world.
My inner beauty
Frightens and transforms
All the hostile forces of the world.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1262
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If I am a real God-lover,
Then I must always remember
That God the Power infinite
Is my constant helper.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 I love God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer

Indeed, this is the bloom of truth,
And this is the blossom of truth.

#+END_VERSE
