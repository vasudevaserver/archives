/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Sanya, China in November and December 2004./


/29 November 2004/

/Sanya, China/



* 1 My Own Inner Capacities ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2632
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My own inner capacities
Frighten me.
My own outer capacities
Sadden me.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 My Lord Is Telling Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2633
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord is telling me
That He feeds on my aspiring,
Climbing and flaming heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 To Transform The World ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2634
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To transform the world,
The God-messengers do bring down
The nectar-flooded Messages
From God
Most perfectly.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Alas ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2635
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
I do not know when and where
I shall start my gratitude-heart
To my Lord Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 My Lord Has Promised Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2636
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord has promised me
His Golden Shore.
Therefore, I do not mind in the least
Travelling through endless
Darkness-nights.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 My World-Attachment-Addiction-Licence ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2637
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My world-attachment-addiction-licence
Has just expired.
I shall never, never, never renew it.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 No Seeker ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2638
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No seeker
Will ever be able to escape
From his giant ego-enemy.
The seeker must conquer
This enemy.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Each Seeker ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2639
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each seeker
Has to be endowed
With an indomitable faith
In God.

#+END_VERSE

* 9 Each Prayer ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2640
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each prayer
Is a flame of my heart
Climbing towards
The highest Heaven.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Each Meditation ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2641
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each meditation
Is a unique opportunity
For me to establish
My conscious oneness
With my Absolute Lord Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 If I Am Not Absolutely Sincere ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2642
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If I am not absolutely sincere
In my prayers
And in my meditations,
How can my soul set fire
To my desire-life-house?

#+END_VERSE


* 12 If I Really Want To Make ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2643
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If I really want to make
The fastest progress
In my spiritual life,
My Lord’s Compassion-Eye
Is bound to help me
To attempt the impossible task
And be victorious.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Before I Pray And Meditate ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2644
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Before I pray and meditate,
My soul feeds my heart, my mind,
My vital and my body
With a dynamic enthusiasm
And intense eagerness.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 Each Self-Giving Action Of Mine ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2645
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each self-giving action of mine
Immediately becomes
A most beautiful flower
In my Lord’s Victory-Garland.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 Each Time I Obey My Lord Supreme ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2646
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each time I obey my Lord Supreme
Happily and unconditionally,
He comes nearer and nearer,
Proudly and triumphantly,
Towards me.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 First I Must Become ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2647
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

First I must become
A sleepless and breathless God-lover.
Only then can I become
An unmistakable God-winner.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2648

#+BEGIN_VERSE

* 18 Each Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2649
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each heart

Of God’s Delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 Nothing Puzzles My Lord ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2650
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing puzzles my Lord --
No, not even my ingratitude-mind.
But it shocks my heart
Most powerfully.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 My Beloved Lord Says To Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2651
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved Lord says to me,
“Do not come to Me, My child,
For your perfection.
Just come to Me only
For My own Satisfaction.”

#+END_VERSE


* 21 When I Claim God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2652
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I claim God
As my own, very own,
I give myself
Ten out of one hundred.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 When God Claims Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2653
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When God claims me
As His own, very own,
I give myself
One hundred out of one hundred.
My Compassion-flooded
Lord Beloved Supreme
Does not agree with me.

#+END_VERSE
