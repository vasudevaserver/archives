/Sri Chinmoy offered the following morning prayers during his visit to Brazil and Paraguay in December 1999 and January 2000. Ten different groups recited the prayers turn by turn three times, then twice, then once./

/22 DECEMBER 1999/

/RIO DE JANEIRO, BRAZIL/



* 1 Run, Speedily Run! ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:117
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Run, speedily run!
Covet, greedily covet
God-service-opportunities.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 My Lord, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:118
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
Out of His infinite Bounty,
Pilots my life-boat
To His own Golden Shore.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 God Begs Us Every Day To Study...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:119
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God begs us every day to study
The life-transformation-course
Given by Him.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Devotion Is Not ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:120
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Devotion is not
An emotional exuberance.
Devotion is
Our God-love-perfection
And our God-service-satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 I Must Never Allow ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:121
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must never allow
My aspiration-heart
To be blighted
By God-doubts and self-doubts-
Even for a fleeting second.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 May My Present God-Love, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:122
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

May my present God-love,
God-devotion and God-surrender
Be dwarfed by my future giant
God-love, God-devotion
And God-surrender.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 When My Mind Fails, My Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:123
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When my mind fails, my heart cries.
When my heart fails, my soul cries.
When my soul fails, my God cries.
When my God fails,
He tries again and again and again
Until He ultimately
And unmistakably succeeds.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 As God's Forgiveness ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:124
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

As God's Forgiveness
Is not far away,
Even so, my life's absolutely new start
Must not take a long time.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 Day In And Day Out ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:125
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Day in and day out
God has made Himself available
So that He will not be assailable.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 God Loves And Treasures ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:126
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God loves and treasures
My sleepless selflessness
Infinitely more than
My uniqueness.

#+END_VERSE
