/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Cambodia in December 2001 and January 2002./


/25 December 2001/

/Phnom Penh, Cambodia/



* 1 My Lord ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:995
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
Why am I so ungrateful to You?
I clearly see that
Your Heart is drawn out towards me
To shelter me
And be my strongest support.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 I Must Be The Winner ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:996
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must be the winner
In the race of life --
I must!

#+END_VERSE


* 3 My Lord Tells Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:997
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord tells me,
“My child,
When your enemies hinder you
In every way imaginable,
Cultivate faith in Me.
I shall be with you,
In you and totally for you.”

#+END_VERSE


* 4 If I Do Not Care ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:998
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If I do not care
To manifest my Lord’s Light
On earth,
Why should He liberate me
From the bondages of time?

#+END_VERSE


* 5 I Have Come To Learn ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:999
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I have come to learn
That my own sincerity-heart
Is a source of tremendous energy
And vitality
In my spiritual life.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 O My Inner Faith ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1000
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my inner faith,
You are the only one
To enable me to withstand
All the attacks
Of my stark adversaries.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 My Lord Says To Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1001
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord says to me,
“My child, do not give up,
Do not give up!
I am coming, I am coming!
I am coming to you as a Beacon
In your ignorance-night.”

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Do Not Doubt ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1002
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do not doubt,
Do not hesitate!
Your God-fulfilment-promise
You will be able to carry through
To completion.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 Weakness-Multitudes ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1003
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Weakness-multitudes
Soulfully confessed
Are immediately forgiven.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 God’s Love Is Not Only ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1004
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s Love is not only
For a few selected seekers.
It is definitely available
To all.
Needless to say,
You and I are included.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 God’s Special Blessings ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1005
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s special Blessings
Are not beyond our reach.
It is His genuine desire
That we all receive
His special Blessings
Directly from His own Hand.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:

#+END_VERSE

  :cite-key: APR:1008

#+END_VERSE


* 15 God Says To Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1009

#+BEGIN_VERSE

“My child, true,
You have gone astray.
But do I not have Love-Power
To draw you back?
Lo, you are back
And once more safe
Inside My Heart-Home.”

#+END_VERSE


* 16 When God’s Voice ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1010
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When God’s Voice
Is my only choice,
He in no time bridges
The longest gap
Between my life and His Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 I Have Completely Forgotten God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1011
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I have completely forgotten God,
But God tells me that His Heart
Is still sleeplessly occupied
With me and my life.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 My Lord Beloved Supreme Tells Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1012
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme tells me,
“My child,
You can doubt Me if you want to,
But you will never be able to doubt
My Love.”

#+END_VERSE


* 19 My Lord Of Infinite Compassion ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1013
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord of infinite Compassion
Says to me,
“My child,
You express your wild hostility
Towards Me
And I accept it gladly.
I know that is your way of life.
My child,
Since I gladly accept
What you give Me,
Can you not accept
What I have to offer:
My Love, My Concern,
My Compassion and My Forgiveness?”

#+END_VERSE


* 20 When I Meditate ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1014
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I meditate
Solely on God’s Victory,
I go far beyond
The confines of time.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 Ignorance-Night I Have ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1015
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ignorance-night I have.
Wisdom-light I need
To enrich my life
Divinely and ceaselessly,
And God is all ready
To fulfil me.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:

#+END_VERSE


* 24 God Says That He Has Seen Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1018
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God says that He has seen me
Countless times,
And I am still the same.
Next time He wants to see me
With a new mind, a new heart
And a new face.
If I fail Him,
He may never give me
Another chance.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 When My Mind ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1019
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When my mind
Wants to speak to God,
God says to my mind,
“You are too close!
Go back at least
One hundred metres.
Do not worry --
I have two very big Ears.
I shall be able to hear you
Very easily.”

#+END_VERSE


* 26 When My Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1020
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When my heart
Wishes to speak to God,
God says to my heart,
“My child, why do you have to stand
So far away?
Am I a stranger?
Come, come very, very close to Me.
The nearer you come,
The better for both of us
To hear each other.”

#+END_VERSE
