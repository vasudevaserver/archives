/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Malaysia and Cambodia in December 2001./

/14 December 2001/

/Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia/



* 1 My Lord Asks Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:918
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord asks me
How I can be so low
That I want to give Him
A solo performance.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 God The Eye ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:919
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God the Eye
Feeds my God-aspiration-hunger.
God the Heart
Feeds my God-manifestation-hunger.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Nothing Else Is Our Birthright ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:920
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing else is our birthright
Save and except
Our inner delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 The More Possessions ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:921
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The more possessions,
The more processions.
The less outer prosperity,
The more inner divinity.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 God Comes Down ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:922
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God comes down
From His transcendental Height
To measure my heart,
And not
To measure my house.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 God’s Visible Love For Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:923
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God’s visible Love for me
Is my sleepless service-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 I Must Transform ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:924
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must transform
All my mind-stories
Into my God-pleasing
Heart-songs.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 The Soil Of Faith ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:925
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The soil of faith
And the seed of love
Receive from God
His transcendental Blessings
And Pride.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 God Cries And Cries ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:926
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God cries and cries
When the fulfilment of our desires
Gives us endless pleasures.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 We Must Not Ask God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:927
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We must not ask God
Why, how, when and where.
We must tell God
That we are sleeplessly ready
With our eagerness-heart
To please Him in His own Way.

#+END_VERSE

On the lap of Mother Earth.

  :PROPERTIES:

  :keywords:

Here on earth.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:933

The soul is the dreamer.
God is the Reality-Bridge
Between the heart-dream
And the soul-dreamer.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:934

While sitting and while standing.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:936
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God loves our small jokes,
But He never likes our big jokes.
Our biggest joke is:
God does not need us.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:937
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A slave to my every desire.

A master-fulfiller of every promise.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 Every Morning ...

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:938

To far horizons of self-transcendence.

#+END_VERSE

* 27 My Lord ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:944
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
I would like to remain
Eternally a server of Yours,
And I do not want to become
Your Eternity’s partner.
“My child,
Then your heart will be empty
Of My Love,
Your life will be empty
Of My Concern
And your breath will be empty
Of My Satisfaction.
My child,
If you become My partner,
Then you will take so much
Of My world-transforming responsibility.”
My Lord, forgive me.
I shall abide by Your Request.

#+END_VERSE

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas, it is too late
For me to ask God
Where I can find Him,
When it will be the right time
And how I can please Him.
Now my desire-bound life and I
Can tell my Lord only this:
“O Lord, we love You.”

#+END_VERSE


* 76 My Mind Must Live ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:993
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind must live
In prayers.
My heart must live
In meditations.
Only then shall I be able to have
A full life of God-Beauty
And God-Fragrance.

#+END_VERSE


* 77 My Lord Tells Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:994
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord tells me,
“My child,
Do not reverse the truth.
Dive deep within.
My Dictates are not binding you.
It is you who are binding My Dictates
And delaying My manifestation
Here on earth.”

#+END_VERSE
