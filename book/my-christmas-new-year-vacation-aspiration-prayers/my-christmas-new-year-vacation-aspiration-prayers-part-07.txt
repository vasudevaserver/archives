* 1 Secretly ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:595

#+BEGIN_VERSE

Secretly
God travels everywhere
Only to find
A gratitude-heart
And a surrender-life.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:596
  :END:

To the same extent
As I need breath
In my life.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:597
  :END:

We must realise
That God is always ready
With a helping Hand
To take us
To the Promised Land.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:598
  :END:

Religion is the body.
Spirituality is the heart.
Yoga is the breath.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:599
  :END:

I see the height of greatness.

I feel and become
The depth of goodness.

  :keywords:

Helpless tears

Your heart's inner cry!"

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:

  :PROPERTIES:

#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
I most sincerely thank You,
For You love my tiny world.
My Lord,
I am sleeplessly grateful to You,
For You love the entire world
In and through my tiny heart.

In His own Way.

#+END_VERSE
