/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to New Zealand in November and December 2002./





27 November 2002

Hamilton, New Zealand



* 1 If Everybody Knew ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1322
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If everybody knew
How unconditional at every moment
God truly is,
This world would have become
The Garden of Eden.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 O Seeker ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1323
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O seeker,
Do not forget
That curiosity-attacks
Can be extremely dangerous.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 My Fame ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1324
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My fame
Is fleeting.
My God-surrender-joy
Is everlasting.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Nothing Is Incurable ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1325
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing is incurable --
No, not even the wild suspicions
Of the mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 I Must Wake Up ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1326
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I must wake up
And join my heart’s
Sleepless cries for God.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 No Matter What Others Think Of Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1327
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No matter what others think of me
Or say about me,
I shall always take the side
Of my aspiration-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 A True God-Lover ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1328
  :END:

A true God-manifestation-light-
Hero-warrior.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 An Aspiration-Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1329
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

An aspiration-heart
Is always in search of
A worry-free life.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 God Has A Very Special Plan ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1330
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God has a very special plan
For me.
I must not allow my mind
To indulge in useless expectations,
Worries and anxieties.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Every Day I Must Practise ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1331
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day I must practise
My God-surrender-songs
At least for ten minutes.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 As I Treasure ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1332
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

As I treasure
My God’s Forgiveness-Smiles,
Even so, He treasures
My gratitude-tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Some Seekers Do Not Pray And Meditate ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1333
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Some seekers do not pray and meditate
Even once a week.
Therefore the sages and the seers
Of the hoary past
Are horrified
At their aspiration-forgetfulness
And aspiration-negligence.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Finally My Prayerful Life ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1334
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Finally my prayerful life
Is becoming a unique
God-gratitude-heart-beauty-fragrance.

#+END_VERSE

* 23 When God Came To Him ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:1344
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When God came to him
For the fifth time,
He asked God
Why He continued coming.
He asked God if God was a beggar
Or a robber.



(23. continued)

God said to him,
“My child,
I am neither a beggar
Nor a robber.
I just cannot help seeing you,
For you are My Divinity’s
Self-Transcendence-Dream.”

#+END_VERSE

Of His Immortality’s Smiles.

  :cite-key: APR:1359

“My child, I have a beautiful story

When you were My fondness-lamb.”

Is all about my immediate future
When You will make me
Your inseparable oneness-heart-hero.”
