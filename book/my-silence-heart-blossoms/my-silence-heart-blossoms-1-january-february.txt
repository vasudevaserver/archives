* January 1st

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Silence-blossoms have awakened my mind.
My tears and God's Smiles clasped I find.

#+END_VERSE


* January 2nd

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My orphan tears begin my day.
My princely smiles blaze the Way.

#+END_VERSE


* January 3rd

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I keep my heart's streaming tears
Sleeplessly alive,
So that my Lord's beaming Smiles
In me can thrive.

#+END_VERSE


* January 4th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Energised by my self-improvement task,
In my Lord's Transcendent Sun-Flames I bask.

#+END_VERSE


* January 5th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Obedience longs to feed the Lord
Infinitely more.
Self-deleted, fastest it runs, arrives
And touches the Shore.

#+END_VERSE


* January 6th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I shall climb up
My soul's dream-flooded staircase.
Endless obstacles
Dauntless my heart shall face.

#+END_VERSE


* January 7th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Fastest runs to the Goal Supreme
My Lord's Love-Train.
Singing and dancing, therein my breath-
No bondage-chain.

#+END_VERSE


* January 8th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be not empty of light and joy,
My foolproof mind.
Heavenward race you never win,
Always behind.

#+END_VERSE


* January 9th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

His Face, my face, my Lord together saw,
And said, "Yours is perfect like Mine, no flaw."

#+END_VERSE


* January 10th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ecstasy-flooded is the road of Love divine,
Divinity's oneness-fulness-sun-shrine.

#+END_VERSE


* January 11th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God is weeping for you;
Oh, do not cry.
You are bound to succeed:
Just once more try.

#+END_VERSE


* January 12th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each time I shake hands with hostile desires,
My God-oneness-visa-delight expires.

#+END_VERSE


* January 13th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Battered by teeming doubts
And blinding fears,
I compel my Lord Supreme
To swim in tears.

#+END_VERSE


* January 14th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Break not your God-gratitude-promise-height;
Yours will be Infinity's Silence-Light.

#+END_VERSE


* January 15th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The starved desires that stormed my mind
Are now deceased, left far behind.

#+END_VERSE


* January 16th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only in God's Orbit I choose to live,
And my mind-thorns, heart-fragrance I shall give.

#+END_VERSE


* January 17th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When God's God-deep
Love-Light-Fragrance appears,
To the Golden Shore
His dream-life-boat man steers.

#+END_VERSE


* January 18th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Oh, if I never fail my Lord,
Oh, if I never fail,
His Heart will be eager to know
My life's every detail.

#+END_VERSE


* January 19th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas, like me, God also has bad days,
And more so when I doubt
His all Compassion-Eye,
Forgiveness-Heart.
Totally insane I shout.

#+END_VERSE


* January 20th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God comes to me
Precisely in the form I need.
Him I must feed
The way His Heart wants me to feed.

#+END_VERSE


* January 21st

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God pressures my mind
Because of its stark unwillingness-flow.
God treasures my heart
Because of its sleepless eagerness-glow.

#+END_VERSE


* January 22nd

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Silence I choose to play with my heart-flames.
Every single day my monkey-mind God tames.

#+END_VERSE


* January 23rd

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

An immediate 'Yes!' to God's every Command,
O my hesitant mind, from you I demand.

#+END_VERSE


* January 24th

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Heaven is so far from the human mind,
But the heart is extremely close.
Heaven commands the mind to halt its journey,
And calls the heart its rose.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSHB:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

With my gratitude heart-tears and smiles
My Lord I greet.
My Lord gives me His
All, the moment I clasp His Feet.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre: poem
  :keywords:

  :END:

Delight is born of obedience-willingness;
