#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sincerity tells you
What to do.
Purity shows you
How to do.
Divinity does not tell or show;
It just does everything
For you.

#+END_VERSE

My Lord,
This time I can give You
Only my frustration.
When You come next time,
I shall give You
My aspiration.
