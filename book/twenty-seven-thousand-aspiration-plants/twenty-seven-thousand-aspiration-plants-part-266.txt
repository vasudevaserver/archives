When you postpone
The Hour of God,
God, like a human being,
Loses heart.

#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Master,
No matter how many times
I fail you,
Please keep me again and again
In your obedience-hope-orbit.

If your life is progress --
Continuous progress,

And I am all for you.
