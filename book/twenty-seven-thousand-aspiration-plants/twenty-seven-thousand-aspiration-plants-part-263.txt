#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord has blessed me
With a self-giving purity-heart
That will never deny Him.

#+END_VERSE

Blessing His creation
With infinite Ecstasy.

O my mind,
Come out, come out
Of your ignorance-fog.
Look! A rainbow is dancing
Over your head.

#+END_VERSE

  :genre:    poem
  :keywords:

  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you live in the heart,
Then you can easily see
How it radiates God's Compassion,
God's Affection, God's Love
And God's Grace.

* 26300

  :genre:    poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:26300

#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I prayerfully look at my Lord's Face,
He embraces my eyes.
When I prayerfully look at my Lord's Feet,
He blessingfully and proudly
Blesses my head with His Feet.
