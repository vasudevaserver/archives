#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I use my mind-telephone
To speak to God,
I find God's Line
Is always busy.
When I use my heart-telephone
To speak to God,
Although the line is never busy,
I get no response.
When I use my soul-telephone
To speak to God,
God immediately answers
And both of us enjoy
Our marathon conversation.

#+END_VERSE
