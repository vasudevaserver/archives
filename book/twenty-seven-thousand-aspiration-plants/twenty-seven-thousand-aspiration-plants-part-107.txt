"God, if You really love me,
Then liberate me
At this very moment."
My heart said to God:

And keep me forever

#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my clamour-mind,
Do you really love me?
Tell me, 'yes' or 'no'.
"For God's sake,
I do not love you!"
Says the mind to me.

O my glamour-vital,
Do I really need you?
Tell me, 'yes' or 'no'.
"I tell you, no, no and no!"
Says the vital to me.

#+END_VERSE
