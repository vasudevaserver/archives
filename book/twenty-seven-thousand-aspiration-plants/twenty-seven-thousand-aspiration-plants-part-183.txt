#+BEGIN_VERSE

God is proud of you
Not because
You have done quite a few
Extraordinary things,
But because in you He sees
The loving acceptance
Of your heavy life-burden.

#+END_VERSE

* 18286

  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:18286

#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing meaningful
And fruitful
Can be replaced.
Therefore, how can
My immortality-heart
Be replaced
By my perplexity-mind?

#+END_VERSE

* 18288

  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:18288
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

On the one hand,
I do not worship my Master
Because, like me,
He has a human form.
On the other hand,
I expect everything from him,
Thinking that for me
He is the God-representative
On earth.

#+END_VERSE
