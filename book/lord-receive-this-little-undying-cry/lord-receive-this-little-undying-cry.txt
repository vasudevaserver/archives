* 1. Lord, receive this little undying cry

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Lord, receive this little life.
It is all Yours.

Lord, receive this little world.
It is all Yours.

Lord, receive this little undying cry.
It is all Yours.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. I assure you

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Love earth.
    I assure you,
    The world will not
    Disappoint you.

Love Heaven.
    I assure you,
    Heaven will not
    Desert you.

Love the inner man in yourself.
    I assure you,
    He alone can and will
    Fulfil you.

#+END_VERSE


* 3. Do you want to be

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do you want to be a ruler?
Then be just.

Do you want to be a lover?
Then be pure.

Do you want to be a teacher?
Then be sure.

Do you want to be another God?
Then be perfect.

#+END_VERSE


* 4. He is wrong

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Who told you
That Heaven is indifferent?
He is totally wrong.

Who told you
That earth is weak?
He is completely wrong.

Who told you
That God does not have
The same concern
    For earth-reality
    And Heaven-dream?
He is absolutely wrong.

Who told you
That I do not love you most?
He is shamelessly wrong.

#+END_VERSE


* 5. They please me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Earth pleases me
With its stark hunger.

Heaven pleases me
With its giant feast.

God pleases me
With His stupendous Satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 6. Power

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You had occult powers;
    Therefore
I adored you.

You have spiritual powers;
    Therefore
I love you.

You will have love-power;
    Therefore
I shall need you,
I shall need you only.

#+END_VERSE


* 7. Naturally, I was flabbergasted

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My occultist friend
Told me that he could run
Faster than a deer.
Naturally, I was flabbergasted.

My yogi friend
Told me that he could
Make himself stronger
Than an elephant.
Naturally, I was fascinated.

My avatar friend
Told me that he became
A faithful dog
    To carry my message to God
    And God's Message to me.
Naturally, I was happy and grateful.

#+END_VERSE


* 8. Show me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Show me one beautiful thing
In the physical world.
I shall love you.

Show me one useful thing
In the vital world.
I shall love you.

Show me one fruitful thing
In the mental world.
I shall love you.

Show me one soulful thing
In the psychic world.
I shall love you.

#+END_VERSE


* 9. Dreamers and lovers

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Dreamers are lovers,
Lovers are dreamers.

Dreamers are lovers
    In the world of light.

Lovers are dreamers
    In the world of night.

Dreamers are lovers
    In the world of perfection.

Lovers are dreamers
    In the world of preparation.

#+END_VERSE


* 10. A bird of the East

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A bird of the East
Told me that God is beautiful
    And that God loves singing.
I believed the bird.
I am now trying to be beautiful myself
In order to sing with God.

A bird of the West
Told me that God is powerful
    And that God loves dancing.
I believed the bird.
I am now trying to be powerful myself
In order to dance with God.

#+END_VERSE


* 11. There we are free

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the heart of gratitude-sky
My body, my soul and I are free.
    My body freely serves God.
        My soul freely manifests God.
            I freely enjoy God.

#+END_VERSE


* 12. We are unkind

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

    The clouds are simply unkind
When they cover the face
    Of the beautiful moon.

    I am mercilessly unkind
When I ridicule the suffering world's
    Bleeding swoon.

#+END_VERSE


* 13. Complainers

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

    Who complained?
The hungry animal in me.
    Who complains?
The ugly human in me.
    Who will complain?
The desperate divine in me.

#+END_VERSE


* 14. Inner progress

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord is satisfied
    With my inner progress,
But I am not.
    My Lord is satisfied
With my outer success,
But I am not.

    Why? Why?

I am not satisfied
    Because
I am still unkind to my Lord.
I have not given Him
    An opportunity to rest,
Which he so rightly deserves.

#+END_VERSE


* 15. Renunciation

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Earth, I have renounced Heaven
    Only to love you.
Heaven, I have renounced earth
    Only to satisfy you.
I have renounced myself
    Only to serve God.
        Alas,
It seems I have not pleased
    Any of them.
        Alas,
Is it my poor fate
    Or
Is it God's wise Choice?

#+END_VERSE


* 16. Gratitude

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He covered the length and breadth
    Of the world
To see the face of gratitude.
What he saw
    Everywhere
        Was ingratitude-trees.

#+END_VERSE


* 17. Our needs

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

    I need patience-light.
You need tranquility-height.
    I need forgiveness-might.
You need perfection-white.

#+END_VERSE


* 18. Inner vision

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My inner vision tells me
    That I am nearer than the nearest
        To my Lord Supreme.

My outer sight tells me
    That I am farther than the farthest
        From my Lord Supreme.

Whom to believe?

If I believe my inner vision,
    Pride tortures my heart.
If I believe my outer sight,
    Frustration tortures my mind.

    Therefore
I am not going to believe either.

#+END_VERSE


* 19. Hope

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sweet is my hope.
Pure is the life of my hope.

With my sweet hope
    I try to reach the higher worlds.
With my pure hope
    I try to fathom my inner worlds.

    But alas,
In neither way do I succeed.
    I fail,
        I miserably fail.

#+END_VERSE


* 20. Purity

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In purity's heart
Earth's saint-life grows.

In purity's soul
God-vision expands.

In purity's goal
Heaven and earth together dance.

#+END_VERSE


* 21. Humility

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

    Humility
Is divinity
On the earth-plane.

    Humility
Is perfection
In earth-achievements.

    Humility
Is God's Satisfaction
In His cosmic Game, His universal Game,
His human and divine Self-transcendence Game.

#+END_VERSE


* 22. Joy

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Give joy;
    Earth will be yours.

Take joy;
    Heaven will be yours.

Become joy;
    God will claim you,
    Earth will need you,
    Heaven will treasure you.

#+END_VERSE


* 23. Selflessness

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I had a dream.
In my dream
I became selflessness.
My dream disappeared
    And
I became reality-story.
In my reality-story
I discovered myself
As the perfect perfection of
    Earth-possessiveness,
    Heaven-possessiveness,
    God-possessiveness,
        And
    Self-possessiveness.

#+END_VERSE


* 24. Surrender

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To earth
I have surrendered my wisdom
So that earth-heart can be flooded
With my wisdom-light.

To Heaven
I have surrendered my ignorance-night
So that Heaven can claim me
And Heaven can utilise me
As its very own.

To God
I have surrendered my 'I-ness;'
    Therefore
God declares
Not only with His infinite Bounty,
But with His utmost Sincerity,
That I am another God.

#+END_VERSE


* 25. Perfection

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

On earth
Perfection-plant grows.

In Heaven
Perfection-river flows.

In God
Perfection-sky glows.

#+END_VERSE


* 26. Aspiration

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There was a time
When I and my aspiration
Tried and cried
To see the Face of God.

But now my aspiration-God and I
Try and cry and cry and try
To see the face of one single real man.

#+END_VERSE


* 27. Illumination

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Earth-illumination is another name
For earth-transformation.

Heaven-illumination is another name
For Heaven-transcendence.

God-illumination is another name
For the liberation of earth-bound consciousness
    And
The perfection of Heaven-free concern.

#+END_VERSE


* 28. Compassion

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He answered
Millions and millions of questions,
Earthly questions
    And
Heavenly questions.
But one day
He was asked
What compassion was.
He remained silent,
For compassion is
God-property
    And
God-reality.

#+END_VERSE


* 29. Inner beauty

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Your inner beauty
Lies in your surrendering to God,
Your Beloved Supreme,
All that you are:
    Gratitude-sea.

Your outer beauty
Lies in your unconditional smile
For suffering earth-life
    And
Bleeding world-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 30. Concern

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

With my unlit and unconscious
    Human concern
I break God's cosmic Rhythm
And ruin His cosmic Game.

With my illumined and conscious
    Divine Concern
I not only serve God in His cosmic Game
But also add beauty and satisfaction
To His cosmic Game.

#+END_VERSE


* 31. Simplicity

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

    Simplicity
Is God-Quality
In our inner life.

    Simplicity
Is God-Capacity
In our outer life.

    Simplicity
Is God-Beauty
In God's own life.

#+END_VERSE


* 32. Delight

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My body's delight
Is my ignorance-sleep.

My vital's delight
Is my aggression-sword.

My mind's delight
Is my suspicion-eye.

My heart's delight
Is my oneness-embrace.

My God's delight
Is the message of
    My Self-transcending Beyond.

#+END_VERSE


* 33. Heaven-reluctance

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Earth is the mother
Of four billion things.
She gladly gives them away
To Heaven.

Heaven is the father
Of only one thing: smile.
He is reluctant to give it away
To earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 34. Let me love

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Let me love.
God will give me
His eternal Name.

Let me serve.
God will make me
A member of His Eternity's Game.

#+END_VERSE


* 35. What never dies?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What never dies?
The dissatisfied animal in me.

What never lives?
The satisfied human in me.

#+END_VERSE


* 36. I call them something else

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What you call
An emancipated life,
I call that very thing
God's Compassion-Smile.

Whom you call
A realised man,
I call that very man
God's Concern-Experience.

#+END_VERSE


* 37. We are great

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Heaven has received
My aspiration;
Therefore
Heaven is great.

Earth has received
My dedication;
Therefore
Earth is great.

My life has received
My liberation;
Therefore
My life is great.

#+END_VERSE


* 38. Followers

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Earth follows Heaven;
    Therefore
Earth is entitled to be happy.

Heaven follows earth;
    Therefore
Heaven is entitled to be brave.

#+END_VERSE


* 39. Heart-conclusion

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mind-seclusion
Is not heart-conclusion.
Heart-conclusion
Is life's realisation
    And
God's Victory-Satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 40. Unpleasant truth

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Unpleasant truth exposed:
You are reviled.
Unpleasant truth concealed:
You are revered.
    Therefore
Be wise.
Act not like a first-class fool.

#+END_VERSE


* 41. If you have to fly

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you have to fly,
Then fly for the highest truth.

If you have to run,
Then run for the brightest light.

If you have to dive,
Then dive for the deepest delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 42. Ages in his face

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I saw
Age in his face
I cried and cried
    Because
It was I who was responsible
For the age in his face.

When I saw
Ages in his face
I danced and danced
    Because
It was I who had helped him
And made him the possessor of ages.

#+END_VERSE


* 43. Do you have anything else?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Oh earth,
Do you have anything else
Save and except
Your blind God?

Oh Heaven,
Do you have anything else
Save and except
Your callous indifference?

Oh God,
Do You have anything else
Save and except
Your unknowable Sacrifice-Life?

#+END_VERSE


* 44. Yours is the God

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yours is the body
    That sleeps.

Yours is the vital
    That strikes.

Yours is the mind
    That envies.

Yours is the heart
    That bears.

Yours is the soul
    That dreams.

Yours is the God
    Who has a quenchless thirst.

#+END_VERSE


* 45. Tears

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Tears that reveal your heart
Thrill God's Heart,
For that is the only time
When God can make you feel
That you are His own,
    Very own.

#+END_VERSE


* 46. How far can you take me?

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O hope,
How far can you take me?

O faith,
How deep can you take me?

O cry,
How high can you take me?

O God,
How near can You make me?

#+END_VERSE


* 47. No difference

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Where is the difference
    Between
My earth and a warrior?
No difference,
Absolutely none.

Where is the difference
    Between My Heaven and a dreamer?
No difference,
Absolutely none.

Where is the difference
    Between
My God and a lover?
No difference,
Absolutely none.

#+END_VERSE


* 48. Choices

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Here on earth
I had to choose either
The soul or material wealth.
I chose the soul.

There in Heaven
I had to choose either
God-manifestation on earth
    Or
God-enjoyment in Heaven.
I chose God-manifestation on earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 49. Always be busy

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Always be busy.
    Earth's indolence
Will surrender to you;
    Heaven's superiority
Will surrender to you.

#+END_VERSE


* 50. Be pure

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: LRTC:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Are you not sure
That God is everywhere?
Then just do one thing:
Try to be pure.
If you become pure,
Then you will see
That it is not just God
Who is everywhere
    But
You, too, are everywhere.
Be pure, like God.
Your existence-light
Shall shine everywhere.

#+END_VERSE
