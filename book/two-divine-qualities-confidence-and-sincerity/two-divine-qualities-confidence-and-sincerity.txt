* Part I -- confidence[fn:: TQCS 1-20. These twenty poems on confidence were written on 14 December 1981.]

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: section
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:1e1
  :END:



* 1.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only a self-giving devotee
Can have ecstatic confidence
In the depths of his heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 2.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If we can earnestly
And soulfully meditate,
Then we can easily have
Cheerful confidence.

#+END_VERSE


* 3.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Perfect confidence is not
In earth-possession
But in self-illumination.

#+END_VERSE


* 4.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To acquire victory's crown,
One must cultivate
Resolute confidence
In one's spiritual life.

#+END_VERSE


* 5.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If a seeker is not
Continuously blessed
With purity-breath,
Then his life
Will unmistakably betray
Arrogant confidence.

#+END_VERSE


* 6.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Success on the outer plane
Does not remain a far cry
After the mind has achieved
Solid confidence.

#+END_VERSE


* 7.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Before God-realisation
It is almost an impossible task
To have supreme confidence
In one's aspiration-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 8.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Devilish confidence
And destruction
Are immediate neighbours.

#+END_VERSE


* 9.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To have complacent confidence
Is the beginning
Of one's fast-approaching perdition
In one's inner life.

#+END_VERSE


* 10.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are a good seeker,
Then you will try
To earn confidence
In the depths
Of your aspiring heart
And not enjoy confidence
In your obscure and impure vital.

#+END_VERSE


* 11.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A disciplined life
Can unmistakably
Register confidence
In the searching mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 12.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When the clever mind undermines
Your spiritual confidence
In your life,
Do not be doomed to disappointment,
Even for a fleeting second.

#+END_VERSE

* 37.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I become my own
Sincerity-heart,
God becomes
His fastest and strongest
Magnet-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 38.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The more I value
My sincerity-breath,
The stronger becomes
God's Manifestation-Promise
In and through me on earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 39.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In the battlefield of life,
We become the victors
Over ignorance-night
Only when
The sincerity of the mind,
The spontaneity of the heart,
The rapidity of the vital
And
The purity of the body
Work in perfect oneness.

#+END_VERSE


* 40.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sincerity,
I do love you and need you.
But I am telling you
In all sincerity
That I love and need
God's Will-Manifestation-Delight
On earth
Infinitely more.
Nay, God's Fulfilment-Smile
Is the only thing
That I sleeplessly need.

#+END_VERSE


* 41.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sincerity,
You are morality's
Loftiest height.

#+END_VERSE


* 42.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Morality is the excellence
Of the mind, heart and life.
Although spirituality
Is in morality and for morality,
It goes far beyond
Morality's territory-confines.

#+END_VERSE


* 43.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The human in us needs
Sincerity's perfect perfection
And
Morality's complete satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 44.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The divine in us
Also needs the same.
But the divine in us,
Being consciously and constantly
One with God's Will,
At times does not abide
By morality's strict injunctions.

#+END_VERSE


* 45.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Humanity's complete fulfilment
Can and shall dawn
Only when humanity prayerfully,
Bravely and self-givingly
Accepts Divinity
For the sake of Divinity.

#+END_VERSE


* 46.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: TQCS:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

"Let Thy Will be done,"
Him to fulfil in His own Way:
This is sincerity's sleepless dream
And breathless reality.

#+END_VERSE
