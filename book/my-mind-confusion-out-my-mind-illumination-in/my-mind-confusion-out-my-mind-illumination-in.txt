#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind
Was a slow
God-Wisdom-learner.
Now the story
Is completely changed.
My mind has become
A fast God-Wisdom-lover.

#+END_VERSE

* 10 Either.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MCMI:10

#+BEGIN_VERSE

Either my mind
Did not know
Or did not care to know
That pleasure
Is perishable and fleeting.
Now my mind
Is fully aware
Of pleasure's perishability
And the real name
Of pleasure:
Futility.

#+END_VERSE
