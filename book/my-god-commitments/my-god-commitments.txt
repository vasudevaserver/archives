* 1 Every Day ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
My soul reminds me
Of my God-commitments.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 God Proudly Treasures ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God proudly treasures
The beauty
Of my God-aspiration,
The purity
Of my God-dedication
And the divinity
Of my God-manifestation.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 To Be A Better Aspirant, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To be a better aspirant,
I helplessly cry within
And
Powerfully smile without.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 If You Do Not Want ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you do not want
To listen to God
Even once a day,
Why should God allow you
To remain inside
His Heart-Garden?

#+END_VERSE


* 5 O My Lord Supreme, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my Lord Supreme,
Do ask my heart
Not only to forgive my mind,
But also to lead
And guide my mind
To reach the golden
Destination-Shore.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 From Now On ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

From now on
I am determined
To love and serve God
Sleeplessly, blindly
And unconditionally.
Who cares if my ex-friend:
Ignorance,
Suffers?

#+END_VERSE


* 7 Each Serious Mistake ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each serious mistake
Leads to either
An outer punishment
Or an inner enlightenment,
Or both.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 My Soul-Friend ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My soul-friend
Wants me to make progress
Only through
My inner and outer
Happiness.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 The Inner Experience-Height ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The inner experience-height
Is the beginning of
The outer freedom-delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Very Happily And Proudly ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Very happily and proudly
God watches
My humility and His Divinity
Playing together.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 Can There Be Anything ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Can there be anything
More fruitful than
My sweetness-fragrance
Progress-heart-home?

#+END_VERSE


* 12 Why Do I Love And Look At...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Why do I love and look at
The beauty and purity
Of the world?
Because
They take away instantly
My own ugliness
And impurity
For my much-needed
Transformation.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Ignorance-Torture ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ignorance-torture
Unconsciously
Has awakened me
To pray for God-rapture.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 The World ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The world
Is really beautiful
Because
So my heart
Has imagined it.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 God Will Never Allow ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God will never allow
A true God-seeker
To waste time,
For each moment
In God-aspiration,
In God-realisation
And
In God-manifestation
Is so important.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 Even From An Iota ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Even from an iota
Of my patience,
I have come to learn
How wise, illumining
And fulfilling
God eternally is.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 If You Are A True Seeker ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are a true seeker
And
Want God-manifestation,
How can you then enjoy
Your chronic
Self-absorption?

#+END_VERSE


* 18 No Matter ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No matter
What the world
Thinks of me,
I am, indeed,
A God-lover
In my own right.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 Gratitude ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Gratitude
And surrender-flights
Know no turbulence
In the sunlit sky.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 My Heart And I ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart and I
Are so happy
That insecurity
Has finally drawn
Its last breath.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 Surrender, Surrender, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Surrender, surrender,
Surrender unconditionally!
Yours will be an inner
Ecstasy-flooded life.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 Alas, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
How I wish
Somebody would tell me
What my heart's
Daily true needs are!

#+END_VERSE


* 23 Every Day ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
I meet God
In His Heart's
Silence-Illumination-Sea.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 Alas, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
It is my own jealous mind
That destroys
My heart's
Sweet hope-dreams.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 O My Unaspiring Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my unaspiring mind,
You are neither needed
Nor wanted.
You are just tolerated.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 What I Always Need ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What I always need
Is not a publicity-mind
But a purity-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 O My Silence-Rainbow, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my silence-rainbow,
May I fly
With your
Heaven-climbing wings?

#+END_VERSE


* 28 If You Are ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are
A chosen God-dreamer,
Then before long
Your life's
Ignorance-nightmare
Will come to an end.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 Only ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only
A tranquillity-flooded heart
Can be the possessor
Of beautiful, soulful
And fruitful tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 As Love Of God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

As love of God
Without prayer
Is impossible,
Even so,
Peace of mind
Without meditation
Is impossible.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 Harmony Shall Echo ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Harmony shall echo
And re-echo
Throughout the length
And breadth of the world
When each human being
Values the beauty
And divinity
Of self-offering.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 In My Universal ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In my universal
Oneness-heart-nest,
No earth-bound desire-bird
Will ever be allowed
To take rest.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Nothing Is Impossible....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing is impossible.
There shall come a time
When I shall definitely
Be able
To move my searching mind
From ignorance-pathways.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 The Unwillingness ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The unwillingness
Of the mind
Has to surrender eventually
To the streaming tears
Of the heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 O My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind,
I do not think anybody
Is willing to pay attention
To your
Ruthless and shameless
Indifference-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 God Neither Imposes ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God neither imposes
Nor exposes.
He just proposes
Out of His infinite
Compassion-Light.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 O My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind,
Do you not remember
That there was a time
When you loved God
Most dearly?
If you do not
Remember the time,
Let me tell you.
It was before the time
When you made friends
With fear, doubt,
Anxiety and despair.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 O My Life, O My Life,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my life, O my life,
My heart must do
What it has to do.
You cannot and must not
Stand in my heart's way.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 My Lord, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
Please tell me why
You have created thoughts:
To punish me
Or to illumine me?
"My child,
The thoughts
That your mind creates
For you
Are for your punishment.
The thoughts
That I create for you
Are for your
Enlightenment."

#+END_VERSE


* 40 When God Comes To You, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When God comes to you,
He brings with Him
His transcendental Dream.
When you go to God,
You should take with you
Your earth-liberated reality.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 As A Glimpse Of God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

As a glimpse of God
Can give you
A taste of trance,
Even so,
A glimpse of God
Can make you
His direct representative
On earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 I Have Two Teachers: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I have two teachers:
The soul and the mind
The soul teaches me
God-acceptance-songs.
The mind teaches me
Life-possession-dance.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 God And God's Peace-Hours ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God and God's Peace-Hours
Are fast approaching me
Because
I am now grown into
My heart's
Unconditional breath.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 Every Day ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
My Lord Supreme
Wants me to multiply
My heart's
Sincerity-purity-smiles.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 Alas, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
I do not know why and how
I have lost
My heart's
Aspiration-hunger
And my life's
Dedication-thirst.

#+END_VERSE


* 46 The Indomitable ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The indomitable
Power of faith
Will eventually conquer
All mountain-barriers
Of our desiring and
Unaspiring mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 God Does Not Want ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God does not want
To expose
Your mind's stupidity
And your heart's impurity.
He just wants to plant
His own Divinity
Inside your Heaven-longing
Necessity.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 O My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind,
You are helping
The wrong parties
If you are siding with
The haughtiness
Of your vital
And
The laziness
Of your body.

#+END_VERSE


* 49 May Invisible Inspiration ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

May invisible inspiration
Be found inside my mind.
May invisible aspiration
Be found inside my heart.
May invisible enthusiasm
Be found inside my vital.
May invisible awareness
Be found inside my body.
May invisible perfection
Be found inside
All that I do
And all that I become.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 The God-Hunger-Song ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The God-hunger-song
Of the ancient sages
Must be learned
By the modern world
To please
The Absolute Lord Supreme
Once more in His own Way.

#+END_VERSE


* 51 What I Need ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:51
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What I need
Is a quenchless thirst
For the perfection of
My mind
And the satisfaction of
My Lord Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 52 There Can Be ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:52
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There can be
No iota of confusion
In the shrine of my heart.
But confusion
Can be endless
Inside my mind-temple.

#+END_VERSE


* 53 My Lord, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MGC:53
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
Were You looking for me?
Alas, I was in
My darkness-mind-room.
My Lord,
Will You once more
Look for me?
This time
You will definitely find me
Inside
My aspiration-heart-room.

#+END_VERSE
