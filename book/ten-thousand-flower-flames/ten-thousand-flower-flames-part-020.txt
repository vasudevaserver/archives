* 1901 My Life Is Silent

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:1901
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind is silent
Because it has realised
God the Power.

My heart is silent
Because it has realised
God the Compassion.

My life is silent
Because it has realised
God the Forgiveness.
