  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:7279

In my soul's lustre-surrender
I give to God

* 7280 The Fragrance Of A Purity-Flower

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:7280
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You do not have to be
Completely versed
In any sacred book.
Just make your heart
The fragrance of a purity-flower.

Is fast approaching you.
