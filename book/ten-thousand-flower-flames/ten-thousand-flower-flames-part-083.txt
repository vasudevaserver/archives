* 8201 Do Teach Me How To Create

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:8201
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
Do teach me how to create
Waves of enthusiasm inside my mind.

My Lord,
Do teach me how to create
A sea of love inside my heart.

  :genre:  poem

  :END:

Of my heart-sun.
