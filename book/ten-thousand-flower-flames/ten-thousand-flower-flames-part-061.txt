* 6001 The Same Road

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:6001
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The Road that my Lord Himself
Walked once upon a time
Is now right before me.
Who else is so divinely fortunate?
Who else has this chance
To be supremely perfect?

* 6100 He Will Do Everything
