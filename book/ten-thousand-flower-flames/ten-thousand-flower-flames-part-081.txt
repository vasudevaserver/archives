* 8001 Yesterday I Prayed To God

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:8001
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday I prayed to God
To let me know who I really am.
Today I am praying to God
To make me feel that my heart
Really loves Him
And that my life
Really needs Him.
