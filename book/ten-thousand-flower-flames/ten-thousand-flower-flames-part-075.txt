* 7401 If I Deepen My Faith

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:7401
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
What will happen
If I deepen my faith
In You?

"My son,
My rich Heart
Will grow richer and richest
In you."

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:7402
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mine is the mind
That longs for
A simplicity-smile.

Mine is the heart
That longs for
A sincerity-cry.

Mine is the life
That longs for
A perfection-run.

Mine is the God
That cares for
My transcendence-flight.

* 7454 Integrally Perfect

  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:7454
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I desired to become a great man.
My Lord said,
"That is not enough."

Today
I desire to become a good man.
My Lord says,
"That is not enough."

Integrally perfect."
