* 5701 The Concept Of Impossibility

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:5701
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The concept of impossibility
My Lord does not accept,
So how can l?
My life will be changed totally.
I just have to be patient.
It is only a question of time.
