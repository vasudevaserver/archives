* 4906 Do Not Give Up

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:4906
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Even if your aspiration-heart
Needs constant repair,
Do not give up.
You will, without fail,
Someday realise God.

Even if your dedication-life
Needs constant repair,
Do not give up.
You will, without fail,
Someday manifest God.

#+END_VERSE


* 4907 Perhaps I Need Not Know

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:4907
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What have I changed?
My doubting mind.
How? I do not know.

What shall I change?
My insecure heart.
How? I do not know.

What will God change for me?
My birthless and deathless
Ingratitude-breath.
How? I do not know,
I shall not know,
I cannot know,
Perhaps
I need not know.

#+END_VERSE
