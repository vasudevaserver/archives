* 29 The Christ ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: GPGM:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The Christ
Prayed and prayed
And then declared:
"I and my Father
Are one."
Sri Krishna
Meditated and meditated
And then declared:
"I am
The Lord
Absolute Supreme."

#+END_VERSE

Of God
The universal Liberator.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 I Pray And Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:

Because
I wish to see
The beauty
Of the higher worlds.

Because
I wish to smell
The fragrance
Of the inner worlds.

#+END_VERSE
