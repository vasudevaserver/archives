* 1 We Must Never Give Up ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:4020
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We must never give up
Our God-realisation-hope
And our God-manifestation-hope.

#+END_VERSE

Of my inner world.

To share with you,
