/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Belek and Antalya, Turkey in December 2006./

/2 December 2006/

/Belek, Turkey/



* 1 We Must Never Give Up ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:4259
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We must never give up
Our God-realisation-hope
And our God-manifestation-hope.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:4260
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God likes the silence-blooms
Of my outer world.
God admires the silence-blossoms
Of my inner world.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:4261
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
Do You not have any Secret
To share with me?
“My child,
I have many, many Secrets
To share with you,
But the time has not come.”

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:4262
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Between a strong desire
And a wild, hungry fire.

#+END_VERSE

  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:4263

And will never fail.

#+END_VERSE
