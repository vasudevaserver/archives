/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Bali, Indonesia in January 2004./

/5 January 2004/

/Legian, Bali, Indonesia/



* 1 God-Realisation ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2396
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God-realisation
Is not an impossible task --
No, not even
The most difficult task.
So says my soul to my mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 To Be A Supremely Chosen Instrument ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2397
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To be a supremely chosen instrument
Of God
Has to be the aim
Of each and every God-seeker.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Nothing Forever Remains The Same ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2398
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing forever remains the same.
This darkest night of my mind
Shall pass --
I know, I know.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 I Do Not Pray To God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2399
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I do not pray to God
To weaken my enemies.
I pray to God
To illumine them.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 God Wants Me To Build ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2400
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God wants me to build
A special Heaven
For me and my oneness-heart-friends
Here on earth
And nowhere else.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 No Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2401
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No heart
Is too weak
To receive Love
From God.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 No Life ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2402
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

No life
Is too insignificant
To love
And serve God.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 The Mind’s Proudest Verdict: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2403
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The mind’s proudest verdict:
God does not know
What the mind does.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 The Heart’s Humblest Opinion: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2404
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The heart’s humblest opinion:
God not only knows
What the heart does
In every detail,
But also inspires the heart
To do everything
Lovingly, soulfully
And self-givingly.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 My Heart-Breath ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2405
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart-breath
Secretly and sacredly treasures
My sleepless love of God
And my breathless service to God.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 My Lord Supreme ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: prayer
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: APR:2406
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Supreme
Is not at all interested
In listening to my life’s
Worthless and useless stories,
But He is extremely eager
To hear my heart-songs
And also extremely eager
To watch my life’s
Surrender-dance.

#+END_VERSE
