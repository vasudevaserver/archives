/The following prayers were offered by Sri Chinmoy in New York during February and March 2000 and recited turn by turn by various girls' and boys' groups./

/19 FEBRUARY 2000/

/PS 117/



* 1 God's Eye Watches Me....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's Eye watches me.
God's Heart waits for me.
God's Life feeds my all.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind,
Stop making constant excuses.
My heart,
Stop making daily excuses.
Can you not see
That your older brother, soul,
Makes no mistake?

#+END_VERSE


* 3 From Today On, Like My Soul,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

From today on, like my soul,
My heart, my mind, my vital,
My body-
Everything that I have and am-
Shall love God only.
A lifelong dream
Has finally come true.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 My Heart-Wisdom Needs...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart-wisdom needs
God-blessed humility.
Humility needs
The feet of universality
For its complete illumination
And total satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Ignorance-Walls Can Imprison...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ignorance-walls can imprison
My God-doubting mind,
But never my God-loving heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 With Redoubled Enthusiasm, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

With redoubled enthusiasm,
I am feeding my eyes
With the Beauty
Of my Lord's Eye.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 With Redoubled Enthusiasm, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

With redoubled enthusiasm,
I am kissing each speck of dust
Of my Supreme Lord's Feet.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 There Must Be No Going Back...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There must be no going back
On my full God-manifestation
And God-satisfaction-promises.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 When We Give God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When we give God
Our world-complaint list,
God swims in the sea
Of His Heart-Tears.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Man Begs God To Increase...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Man begs God to increase
His Infinity's capacity
To forgive him.
God begs man to increase
His divinity's capacity
To receive God.

#+END_VERSE

* 11 We Must Never Dream...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We must never dream
Of enlightenment
Unless and until we have
Prayerfully and soulfully offered
Our absolute loyalty
To our God-satisfaction-commitment.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 The Mind And The Vital Every Day...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The mind and the vital every day
Must go to the iron discipline-school
To be ready for the acceptance
Of God-Commands.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Do You Want To Know Why...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do you want to know why
God has singled me out?
He has singled me out
Not because I love Him more
Than anybody else loves Him,
But because He has given me
More God-hunger
Than He has given to anybody else.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 Self-Mastery Pioneers...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Self-mastery pioneers
To the outer world
Of surprising success-life.
God-discovery pioneers
To the inner world
Of astonishing progress-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 One Powerful Doubt Of The Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

One powerful doubt of the mind
Can easily derail
The running, blissful train
Of the heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 My Desire-Achievement-Fame...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My desire-achievement-fame
Is
My aspiration-enlightenment-shame.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 To Love God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To love God
Unconditionally
Is to appoint God
Permanently.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God tells me that
I disappoint Him
Infinitely more
Than I can ever imagine.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 Enthusiasm At Once...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Enthusiasm at once
Uproots my mind-jungle
And
Illumines my heart-temple.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 God's Love And Fondness...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MSH:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's Love and Fondness
For me
Become measureless
When my heart-tears
Blind His Eye completely.

#+END_VERSE

On earth."
