* 1 Little Do I Know ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:1
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Little do I know
Or care to know
That my bondage-life
Is my self-invention.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 Prayer-Tears ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:2
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Prayer-tears
Are indeed pure.
Meditation-smiles
Are supremely sure.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 We Cannot Solve Life's Riddles ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:3
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We cannot solve life's riddles
On our own.
Each riddle of life
Needs a special
Compassion-Smile from God
For its liberation.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 We Celebrate ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:4
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We celebrate
The supremacy
Of the division-mind,
Which is absurdity itself.
We must celebrate
The satisfaction-ecstasy
Of the oneness-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Ego And Fear ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:5
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ego and fear
Chase each other.
Doubt and faith
Chase each other.
Confidence and insecurity
Chase each other.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Forgiveness ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:6
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Forgiveness
Is a matter
Of luminous choice.
Forgiveness
Can enter
And does enter
The heart
Of progress-delight.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 We Do Not Need A Mind ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:7
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We do not need a mind
Of constant contradiction.
What we need is a heart
Of constant
Heaven-climbing aspiration.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 Aspiration-Shortage ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:8
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Aspiration-shortage
Is a very old story.
Let us write
A new story:
Aspiration-plenitude.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 May Each Seeker's Love Of God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:9
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

May each seeker's love of God
And surrender to God's Will
Be a fragrance-flooded
One-way street
With no U-turn.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Our World-Attachments ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:10
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Our world-attachments
Will vanish
Only when our minds
Sincerely care for
The world-transforming
Illumination.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 Alas, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:11
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
Long lost are
My God-loving days
And
God-worshipping nights.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 At Last Wisdom-Light ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:12
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

At last wisdom-light
Has dawned in my heart.
No more
Shall I be trapped
In the net
Of unhealthy,
Earth-bound thoughts.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 The Complexity-Challenges ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:13
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The complexity-challenges
Of life
Are outwardly hurtful
But inwardly fruitful.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 The Triumphant ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:14
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The triumphant
God-loving hearts
Are happily treasured
By God's Compassion-Eye
And Satisfaction-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 Each Seeker-Heart Realises ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:15
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each seeker-heart realises
That his Master-Saviour
Is an extraordinary member
Of God's immediate Family.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 Mortal ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:16
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mortal
Is the life of hope.
Let us try to place
Immortality
Inside the heart-breath
Of hope.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 Aspiration And Dedication ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:17
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Aspiration and dedication
Are by far
The best instruments
Of the God-manifesting souls.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 Let Us Abandon ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:18
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Let us abandon
Our yesterday's minds.
Let us deal with
Our today's
God-manifesting hearts.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 When I Treat Others ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:19
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I treat others
As God-manifesting
Divinity-souls,
I feel that I am
Safely on my way
To God-manifestation-lands.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 Try To Live ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:20
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Try to live
According to your heart's
Aspiration-wisdom-dictates.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 The Man-Made Life ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:21
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The man-made life
Is too weak to climb up
To the highest.
The God-made life
Remains always
At the summit-heights.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 Be Not Infatuated ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:22
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be not infatuated
With money-power
Or possession-power,
For their lives
Are extremely short.
Frustration
And
Destruction
Quickly follow them.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 The Failure ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:23
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The failure
Of desire-life
Is the satisfaction
Of earth's
Aspiration-victory.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 The Capacities ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:24
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The capacities
Of the inner man
Far transcend
The impossibility-barriers
Of the outer life.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 Continued Effort ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:25
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Continued effort
Is the open secret
Of success-life.
Continued surrender
Is the open secret
Of progress-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 My Success-Choice ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:26
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My success-choice
Has made my life
Miserable.
My progress-choice
Will make my heart
Always laudable.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 O Man, Hide Not ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:27
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O man, hide not
Your earth-bound desires.
Bring them to the fore,
Illumine them
And help them reach
Their destined goal:
Satisfaction divine.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 Since The Mind-Prison ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:28
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Since the mind-prison
Is never locked,
Insincerity and impurity
Frequently visit there.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 Blameless People ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:29
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Blameless people
Never dream of blaming
The blighted
Imperfection-world.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 When I Invite ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:30
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I invite
God the Voice
To fulfil me,
I reach the heart
Of my own
Perfection-choice.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 He Who Has ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:31
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He who has
The abundant capacity
To forgive others
Sees that he has
Nobody to forgive.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 To Forgive ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:32
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To forgive
Is to identify oneself
With
God's God-Satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Nobody Else ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:33
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nobody else
Causes my suffering.
My own choices
Are the only cause
Of my suffering.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 My Mind ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:34
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind
Wants to change
The minds of others.
My heart
Does not want to change
Any other heart.
It only wants to become
Part and parcel
Of all hearts.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 Listen To The Soundless Voice ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:35
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Listen to the soundless voice
To give you the capacity
To make the perfect choice
In your outer life.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 Mine Is The Choice ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:36
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Mine is the choice
Either to blindly bind
Or to smilingly liberate
The world.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 Spirituality Is Not Like ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:37
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Spirituality is not like
A scientific experiment.
Spirituality is divinity's
God-fulfilment-experience.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 He Who Has Faith ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:38
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He who has faith
In abundance
Can never lose
The reality,
For he has already
Become the reality.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 The Clever Man Does Not Know ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:39
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The clever man does not know
That God is great.
The wise man knows
That God is not only
Divinely great
But also supremely good.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 The Congregation ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:40
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The congregation
Of aspiring hearts
Is the liberation
Of binding lives.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 Each Man Must Try ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:41
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each man must try
Not only to curtail
But actually to end
His self-indulgence
Pleasure-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 To See ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:42
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To see
The perfection-divinity
In a human being
Is the most precious
Opportunity
Of a lifetime.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 The Mind Is Always Eager ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:43
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The mind is always eager
To export stupidity.
The heart is always eager
To import wisdom.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 My Life's ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: BLSI:44
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My life's
Heavenward journey
Is the liberation
Of my mind's
Ignorance-night.

#+END_VERSE
