* 17087

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:17087
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If your mind is hungry
To run farther,
And if your heart is hungry
To run faster,
Then God cannot and will not
Remain invisible.

#+END_VERSE


* 17088

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:17088
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O suspicion-mind,
I do not need you.
O inspection-mind,
I do not need you.
O interrogation-mind,
I do not need you.
O illumination-mind,
I need you,
I have always needed you
And I shall always need you.

#+END_VERSE

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:17089

#+BEGIN_VERSE

What makes my heart so special?
My heart loves God
In God's own Way.
My heart loves
Even my constantly doubtful mind.

#+END_VERSE
