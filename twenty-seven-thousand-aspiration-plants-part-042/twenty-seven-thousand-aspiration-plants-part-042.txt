* 4185

  :genre:    poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:4185
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The soul is all compassion,
But when it sees
That the mind has become
Total unwillingness
And the heart has become
Total cheerlessness,
Then the soul complains to God.

#+END_VERSE

* 4188

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:    poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:4188
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You have an outstanding mind.
But from your outstanding mind
I am not going to get
Anything soulful.

You have an astounding heart.
From your astounding heart
I shall not only get
Everything soulful
But also everything fruitful.

#+END_VERSE
