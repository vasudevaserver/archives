* 1 I Feel Immense Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I feel immense peace
The moment my heart
Opens its forgiveness-door.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 The Sunlit Smile ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The sunlit smile
Of your eyes
Comes directly from
The peace of your heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 I Lose My Peace Of Mind ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I lose my peace of mind
Even when I unconsciously
Turn away from
God's blessingfully extended Hands.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 If You Really Desire Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you really desire peace
In your life,
Then dissolve your stupid mind's
Resentment-stone.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 My Soul Most Compassionately ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My soul most compassionately
Teaches my heart
How to play peace-melody
On my life-flute.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Every Morning My Lord Supreme ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every morning my Lord Supreme
Happily comes and hears
My heart's silence-peace-songs.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 If Your Mind's Name ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If your mind's name
Is synonymous with
Unaspiring thoughts,
How can you ever have
An iota of peace?

#+END_VERSE


* 8 My Lord, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
I do not want Your Love,
I do not want Your Joy,
I do not want even Your Peace.
But I do want from You
The capacity to obey You
Sleeplessly.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 The Peace-Bird Flew ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The peace-bird flew
Into my heart-nest
The moment I showed
My life's gratitude-passport
To my Lord Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Peace Is Not ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace is not
A man-written story.
Peace is
A God-composed Song.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 Today I Am So Happy ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Today I am so happy
And proud of my mind
Because it has fully accepted
My Lord's blessingful Peace-Advice.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 In My Heart-Garden ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In my heart-garden
Each peace-flower-plant
Has a unique inspiration-fragrance.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 Of All God's ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Of all God's
Blessing-Gifts,
Peace
Is my most favourite.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 If Your Mind Is Surrounded ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If your mind is surrounded
By a cloud of insecurity,
And your heart
By a cloud of impurity,
Then how can you have peace?

#+END_VERSE


* 15 To Swim In The Peace-River, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To swim in the peace-river,
I prayed to God
To make me a choice instrument.
But God made me
His loving partner instead.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 When I Soulfully ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I soulfully
Think of peace
And prayerfully
Talk about peace,
I clearly see that
The Prince of Peace
Is coming closer to my life.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 O Compassion-Lord Shiva, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O Compassion-Lord Shiva,
Do bless me today
From Your Himalayan
Peace-Peak-Trance.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 I Acquire Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I acquire peace
Not from solitude
But from my world-servitude.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 Peace In Abundant Measure ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace in abundant measure
I feel
Inside my heart's cry,
And not in my life's sigh.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 Sweetness-Sorrows ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sweetness-sorrows
Give me
A very special kind
Of peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 My Inner Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My inner peace
Does not select anybody,
Does not reject anybody.
My inner peace
Always self-givingly projects itself.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 Peace Is ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace is
Divinity's strength
That covers
Infinity's length.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 My Mind's Success-Dance ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind's success-dance
Does not give me peace.
My heart's progress-song
Gives me peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 Inside My Mind-Ailments ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Inside my mind-ailments
Peace is my medicine.
Inside my heart-garden
Peace is my fragrance.

#+END_VERSE


* 25 Peace Is Not ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace is not
In my death.
Peace is in
My self-giving breath.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 The Love Of Your Mind ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The love of your mind
May not be for all,
But the peace of your heart
Is definitely for all.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 Peace Is ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace is
My soul's property
And my life's prosperity.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 We May Not See God, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We may not see God,
But we do feel
The Breath of God,
Which is peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 O World Of Unrest! ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O world of unrest!
We peace-lovers
Are trembling for you
Inside our aspiration-heart-garden.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 My Heart Of Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart of peace
Is my life's
Self-giving immortality.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 I Love ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I love
The human beings
Who embody peace.
I even love
The human beings
Who sincerely and soulfully
Long for peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 A Life Of Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A life of peace
Is the beginning
Of Heaven's
Cheerful and powerful descent.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Peace Is The Beauty ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace is the beauty
Of my heart.
Like the beauty of nature,
I cannot hide it.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 Unlike Other Friends Of Mine, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Unlike other friends of mine,
Peace is the only friend
That begs me not to enter
Into the desire-land.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 My Lord Asks Me To Develop ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord asks me to develop
A special kind of fondness
For the sweetness
Of my purity-heart-peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 You Must Stop Postponing ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You must stop postponing
Your heart's surrender to God's Will
If you want to have
Divinity's Love and Immortality's Peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 Bid Your Fear, Doubt And Anxiety...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Bid your fear, doubt and anxiety
A hasty farewell.
You will be able to enjoy
Immediate peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 Peace Always Chooses ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace always chooses
Our hearts
To be its perfect home.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 How Can You Have Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

How can you have peace
If you cannot come out of
Your mind's
Insecurity-imprisonment-cave?

#+END_VERSE


* 40 When God Saw In Me....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When God saw in me.
Sincere and perfect helplessness,
He gave me His Heart of Love
And His Breath of Peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 A Man Of Peace Is ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A man of peace is
The fragrance of
God's beautiful Garden-Heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 Do You Want To Know ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do you want to know
Where the residence of peace is?
It is inside
Your expectation-free existence.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 If You Are A Man Of Peace,...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are a man of peace,
Then you are divinely great.
But if you share your peace
With the world unreservedly,
Then you are unquestionably
Supremely and unsurpassably good.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 Peace Is My Soul's ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace is my soul's
Brightest readiness-smile
In all kinds of life-weather.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 Your Heart's Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Your heart's peace
Is bound to be suffocated
Inside your mind's sealed and unlit
Expectation-caves.

#+END_VERSE


* 46 Your Volleyball-Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Your volleyball-heart
With its restlessness-game
Will not be able to see, feel
Or receive even an iota of peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 My Peace ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My peace
Is my soulful glimpse
Of my Lord's
Infinite Liberation-Light.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 How Can You Have ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

How can you have
A mind of joy and a heart of peace
If you have chronic
Superiority-inferiority complex relapses?

#+END_VERSE


* 49 The Mind's Imperfection-Philosophy ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The mind's imperfection-philosophy
Is empty of peace
And the heart's perfection-song
Is full of peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 Peace Is Not To Be Found ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace is not to be found
In my prayer-success-mind.
Peace is to be found only
In my meditation-progress-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 51 While Walking ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:51
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

While walking
Along Eternity's Peace-Road,
I need only one thing
From my Beloved Supreme:
A powerful Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 52 His Mind Has Boundless Peace....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:52
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

His mind has boundless peace.
Do you know why?
Because his mind is sheltered
Twenty-four hours a day
By a purity-sky.

#+END_VERSE


* 53 At Long Last ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:53
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

At long last
Today I am able to say hello
To my long lost, long forgotten
Very old dear friend,
Peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 54 My Heart's Gratitude-Tears ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:54
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart's gratitude-tears
And my life's surrender-smiles
Are the products of my life's
Immortalising peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 55 Sing, My Heart, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:55
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sing, my heart,
Sing Eternity's oneness-song.
You will before long feel
Infinity's fulness-peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 56 My Aspiration Is My Bridge ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:56
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My aspiration is my bridge
Between
My Lord's Compassion-Satisfaction-Eye
And my heart's silence-peace-thrill.

#+END_VERSE


* 57 God's Peace- Fragrance-Heart ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:57
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God's Peace- Fragrance-Heart
Is not meant for those
Who live in self-indulgence-world.

#+END_VERSE


* 58 You Are Telling Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:58
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are telling me
That you have no peace of mind.
Such being the case, I am asking you
To turn your self-doubt
Into earth-trust.
Try, you certainly can!

#+END_VERSE


* 59 You Do Not Have To Climb Up...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:59
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You do not have to climb up
To the highest height
Of the aspiration-mountain.
Just start climbing up the mountain
Soulfully and unconditionally.
God will bless you
With his blessingful Love,
Blessingful Joy and blessingful Peace.

#+END_VERSE


* 60 When We Give God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PGFH:60
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When we give God
What we have,
He gives us
His Readiness-Smile-Eye.
When we give God
What we are,
He gives us
His Fulness-Peace-Heart.

#+END_VERSE
