* 1 My Master’s Acceptance ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2230
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Master’s acceptance
Of my life
Is God-realisation guaranteed
At God’s choicest Hour.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2231
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

Except one thing:
His Heart has yet to learn
How to exclude
Any human being.

#+END_VERSE
