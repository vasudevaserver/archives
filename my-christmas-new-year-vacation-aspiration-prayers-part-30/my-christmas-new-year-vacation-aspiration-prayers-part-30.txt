/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Bali, Indonesia in January and February 2004./


/13 January 2004/

/Legian, Bali, Indonesia/



* 1 My Master’s Acceptance ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2469
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Master’s acceptance
Of my life
Is God-realisation guaranteed
At God’s choicest Hour.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 God Knows Everything ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2470
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God knows everything
Except one thing:
His Heart has yet to learn
How to exclude
Any human being.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2471
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Or suffering from
God-faith-eclipse.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2472
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The Transcendental God-Height
Is only for the one-pointed
God-seekers, God-lovers
And God-servers.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2473
