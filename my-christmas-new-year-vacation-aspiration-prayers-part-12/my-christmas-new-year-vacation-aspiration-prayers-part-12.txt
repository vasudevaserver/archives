* 1 I Know And I Feel ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:836
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I know and I feel
That my Lord Beloved Supreme
Has an everlasting Love
For me.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 God Does Not Hide Anything ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:837
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God does not hide anything
From me.
Because of my blind eyes,
I do not see
What He does for me
Self-givingly and sleeplessly.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 God Blesses Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:838
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God blesses me
Out of the Bounties of His Heart.
Alas, where is my receptivity-heart,
Where?

#+END_VERSE


* 4 If We Want To Walk ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:839
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If we want to walk
Every day
In the newness of life,
Then we must have enthusiasm
In our mind
In abundant measure.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 Every Morning And Evening ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:840
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every morning and evening
My Lord tells me
Only one thing:
Not to do,
But to be.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 God Expects ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:841
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God expects
The deepest affection-portion
From my aspiration-heart,
And no,
Never some random portion.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 The Days Are Short ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:842
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The days are short.
My desires are endless.
O climbing aspiration-flames,
Where are you?
In your absence
I am helpless,
I am hopeless,
I am useless.

#+END_VERSE
