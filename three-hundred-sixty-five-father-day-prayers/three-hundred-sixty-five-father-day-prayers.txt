* 1. Nowhere

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nowhere to live?

Try my heart-home.

I think you will like it.

Nowhere to sleep?

Try my soul-room.

I am sure you will love it.

Just love it.

#+END_VERSE


* 2. A Beggar

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday

I acted like a beggar

By asking God

To give me a smile.

Today

I am acting like a beggar

By asking God

To give me His Power.

Tomorrow

I shall be acting like a beggar

By asking God

To make me His only perfect instrument.

#+END_VERSE


* 3. Father, Do I Have A Mission

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Father, do I have a mission?

Daughter, you have not one

But two missions,

secret and sacred.

Your heart of life is your secret mission.

Your life of heart is your sacred mission.

#+END_VERSE


* 4. Do You Know, Daughter

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do you know, daughter,

Why God loves you?

No, Father, I do not know.

He loves you

Because

You are a good player of love-game.

Do you know, daughter,

Why God is devoted to you?

No, Father, I do not know.

God is devoted to you

Because

You are a superb player of devotion-game.

Do you know, daughter,

Why God has surrendered to you?

No, Father, I do not know.

God has surrendered to you

Because

You are an excellent player of surrender-light.

#+END_VERSE


* 5. What Are They

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What is earth,

If not the splendour of sacrifice-might?

What is Heaven,

If not the splendour of vision-light?

What is humanity,

If not the splendour of expectation-soul?

What is divinity,

If not the splendour of ecstasy-sea?

#+END_VERSE


* 6. You Are Thinking

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You are thinking of yourself.

Lo, you are descending

From

The human life to the animal life.

You are thinking of God.

Lo, you are ascending

From

The human life to the Life divine.

You have started loving God.

Lo, you are singing and playing

With

God’s Silence-Height

And

God’s Oneness-Light.

#+END_VERSE


* 7. A Full And Complete Truth

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I love God.

Indeed,

This is a stark lie.

I think of God.

Indeed,

This is a quarter truth.

I do not find faults with God.

Indeed,

This is a half truth.

I never doubt God’s existence.

Indeed,

This is a full and complete truth.

#+END_VERSE


* 8. Believe Me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There is no difference

And

There can be no difference

Between

Love-touch and God-Touch.

Believe me, O aspiration-soul!

There is no difference

And

There can be no difference

Between

Fear-touch and death-touch.

Believe me, O desire-life!

#+END_VERSE


* 9. Yesterday, Today And Tomorrow

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday

I cried to live

In God’s Dream-Boat.

Today

I long to reach

God’s Reality-Shore.

Tomorrow

I shall pine to be

God’s Perfection-Smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 10. You Want To Know

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You want to know my past:

I was an unparalleled escapist.

You want to know my present.

I am a shameless opportunist:

You want to know my future:

I shall become

And end my life

As a false idealist.

#+END_VERSE


* 11. A Series Of Mysteries

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Earth-body

Is a mystery of loveliness.

Earth-vital

Is a mystery of strangeness.

Earth-mind

Is a mystery of loneliness.

Earth-heart

Is a mystery of holiness.

Earth-soul

Is a mystery of perfectiveness.

#+END_VERSE


* 12. My Three Dreams

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My old dream

Is nothing but a faded flower.

My new dream

Is nothing but a wild animal.

My God-Dream

Is nothing but a climbing life.

#+END_VERSE


* 13. My Mind-Life, My Heart-Life, My Soul-Life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind-life

Is a useless thing to ignore.

My heart-life

Is a holy thing to see.

My soul-life

Is a perfect thing to offer.

#+END_VERSE


* 14. I Do Not Know

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Where is God?

I do not know.

Who is God?

I do not know.

Shall I become

Someday

Another God?

I do not know.

But,

One thing I do know:

He loves me

And

He is all for me.

#+END_VERSE


* 15. When I Escaped

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I escaped

From ignorance-river

The ocean of ignorance

Captured me, my earth-body

And

My heaven-soul.

When I escaped

From the ocean of ignorance

The Infinity of God-Love

And

God-Pride

Embraced me, my all.

#+END_VERSE


* 16. God-Power Is Always Within Me

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I have no money-power

Yet

I am unmistakably happy.

I have no earth-power

Yet

I am exceedingly happy.

I have no friend-power

Yet

I am constantly happy.

How?

Just because God-power is always within me.

#+END_VERSE


* 17. Are You A Seeker

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Are you a seeker

Of silence-life?

Then come in, please.

Indeed,

This is the right place.

Are you a seeker

Of sound-life?

Then leave this place immediately.

Indeed,

This place can never welcome you.

#+END_VERSE


* 18. The Boundaries

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Daughter, these are

The boundaries of your unseen life:

Your black ego-life

Your red jealousy-life

Your brown doubt-life

Your gray insecurity-life.

Daughter, these are

The ever-transcending boundaries of your life

unknowable:

Your green aspiration-life

Your blue realisation-life

Your gold revelation-life

Your white perfection-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 19. Nothing Is Impossible

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Nothing is impossible

When

I have an iota of love for God.

Nothing is impossible

When

I have a fragment of sacrifice for man.

Nothing is impossible

When

I realise I am oneness-perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 20. Why, My Daughter, Why

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Father, I love your Eyes.

Why, my daughter, why?

Because they elevate my heart-breath.

Father, I love your Hands.

Why, my daughter, why?

Because they protect my earth-life.

Father, I love your Heart.

Why, my daughter, why?

Because it feeds my Heaven-soul.

#+END_VERSE


* 21. Thought And Despair

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Thought is transitory;

Therefore

Don’t worry.

It will die soon.

Despair is stationary;

Therefore

Don’t worry.

You can leave despair alone

And triumphantly run away.

#+END_VERSE


* 22. Noise, Voice And Choice

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What do I like?

A noiseless noise.

What do I love?

A voiceless voice.

What do I admire?

A faultless choice.

#+END_VERSE


* 23. Your Three Enemies

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The enemy of your past life:

Pride, a sky-vast pride.

The enemy of your present life:

Jealousy, a sea-deep jealousy.

The enemy of your future life:

Insecurity, an ant-feeble insecurity.

#+END_VERSE


* 24. Alas, You Are Deceived And Disappointed

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Your vision of man

Has deceived me.

Alas, what can you do now?

You are doomed to stay with man.

Your realisation of the cosmic Gods

Has disappointed you.

Alas, what can you do now?

You are compelled to stay with them.

#+END_VERSE


* 25. Do You Know

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My heart,

Do you know what you are?

You are my daily hope.

My soul,

Do you know what you are?

You are my daily promise.

My God,

Do you know who you are?

You are my daily Saviour.

#+END_VERSE


* 26. His Life-Chronicle

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

His body is a village

Of the sleepers.

His vital is a town

Of the fighters.

His mind is a city

Of the dreamers.

His heart is a province

Of the lovers.

His life is a country

Of the sufferers.

#+END_VERSE


* 27. Earth-Voice And Heaven-Voice

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My earth-voice proclaims:

“God may be of Heaven

But

He is for earth.”

My heaven-voice proclaims:

“God may be for earth

But

His Breath is in Heaven.”

#+END_VERSE


* 28. A New Discovery And A New Invention

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A new discovery;

I am using my heart

To see the Face of God.

A sublime invention;

God needs me.

He really needs my life of love.

#+END_VERSE


* 29. Thought Will Surrender

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O face of thought,

You are my abysmal disgrace.

O heart of will,

You are my supernal lustre.

O soul of surrender,

You are my sempiternal pride.

#+END_VERSE


* 30. Mind-Night And Heart-Light

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O mind of night,

I am tired of seeing

Your ugly face.

O heart of light,

Where are you?

Do you really live?

Or is it all my imagination?

O soul of perfection,

Do you really carry

The message of perfection-light?

#+END_VERSE


* 31. The Miracle Power Of Infinitesimal Grain

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

An infinitesimal grain of Truth

Can save you and your life.

An infinitesimal grain of Peace.

Can perfect you and your life.

An infinitesimal grain of Love

Can immortalise you and your life.

#+END_VERSE


* 32. When You Live

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When you live

In the room of your thought,

Your life is a mighty, fruitless plan.

When you live

In the house of your will,

Your life is a solid, splendid achievement.

When you live

In the palace of God-love,

Your life is a flood of glowing,

illumining ecstasy.

#+END_VERSE


* 33. The Story Of Time

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The Life of Time:

Aspiration-night.

The Heart of Time:

Silence-Light.

The Soul of Time:

Liberation-Delight.

The God of Time:

Compassion-Height.

#+END_VERSE


* 34. Do You Know

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Daughter, do you know

What I owe you?

I owe you my Perfection-Life on earth.

Father, do you know

What I owe you?

I owe you my aspiration-soul of Heaven.

So Father, it is a mutual debt.

No, Daughter, it is a mutual self-satisfaction.

#+END_VERSE


* 35. The Miracles Of Your Surrender-Life

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Look at the miracles

Of your surrender-life.

You are remembering

Your forgotten bliss.

You are enjoying

Your cosmic silence-sleep.

#+END_VERSE


* 36. Don’t Hide

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Don’t hide, don’t hide.

If you hide, you can never be

A master sailor on the flow of Time.

Seek, seek,

Yours will be the experience-sea,

Yours will he the realisation-mountain,

Yours will be the Perfection-Sun.

#+END_VERSE


* 37. Three Powers

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Fear-power tells you:

Death is at your door.

Courage-power tells you:

Immortality is in your room.

Love-power tells you:

Death never cares for you,

And

Immortality desperately needs you.

#+END_VERSE


* 38. Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday

I lived in the body of outer glory.

Today

I am living in the heart of inner glory.

Tomorrow

I shall be living in God’s unhorizoned Glory.

#+END_VERSE


* 39. If You

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you

Love man in God,

The adventure of consciousness-light

Will embrace you without fail.

If you

Serve God in man

The consciousness-light of adventure

Will claim you and treasure you.

#+END_VERSE


* 40. When I Think Of You

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Heart, my heart,

When I think of you,

I see an aspiring height.

Life, my life,

When I think of you,

I hear a thunderous voice.

God, my God,

When I think of You,

I make my supreme choice.

#+END_VERSE


* 41. Sad Music And Glad Music

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O sad music of humanity,

Even God is tired of you.

O glad music of divinity,

Even earth-ignorance needs you

Sincerely

Desperately

And

Immediately.

#+END_VERSE


* 42. You Want To Know

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You want to know

Where I was born.

My sincere heart speaks:

I was born in the field of toil.

You want to know

Where I now live.

My sincerity speaks:

I live on prostrate soil.

You want to know

Where will be my future home.

My sincerity speaks:

My future home will be

In my earth-bound foil.

#+END_VERSE


* 43. Unerring, Unending And Uncompromising

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Unerring,

Heaven-promise is unerring.

Unending,

Earth-sorrow is unending.

Uncompromising,

My challenge to ignorance-night

Is uncompromising.

#+END_VERSE


* 44. Three Friends And My Lord

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Three friends are with me now:

Yesterday, today and tomorrow.

Yesterday offers me a lingering world.

Today offers me a dying world.

Tomorrow offers me an aspiring world.

Ah, my Lord has just appeared.

He offers me His ever-fulfilling world.

#+END_VERSE


* 45. My Life-Story

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My body-life

Is a land of poverty.

My vital-life

Is a land of audacity.

My mind-life

Is a land of curiosity.

My heart-life

Is a land of austerity.

My soul-life

Is a land of absurdity.

#+END_VERSE


* 46. Slow Process And Quick Process

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O slow process of mortality,

Sleep not, wake up and strive.

I shall admire your new life.

O quick process of immortality,

Run not, slow down and rest.

I shall adore your compassion-height.

#+END_VERSE


* 47. In You

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

In you I saw

The light of the rising sun

And spontaneously, I saluted you.

In you I shall dare not see

The tears of the setting sun

For I shall immediately die.

#+END_VERSE


* 48. Her Feet Have Descended

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Her feet have descended;

Therefore

Earth-life can treasure hope.

Her eyes have ascended;

Therefore

Heaven-soul can fulfill its august promise.

Her heart has transcended;

Therefore

God-Goal can manifest its Light supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 49. You Want To Know

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You want to know

What my mind is:

My mind is unlit ignorance.

You want to know

What my life is:

My life is half-lit ignorance.

You want to know

What my goal is:

My goal is ignorance-transformation.

You want to know

Who my Lord is:

My Lord is Compassion-Perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 50. I Need You

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O hiding hands of love,

I need you.

O smiling eyes of love,

I need you.

O illumining heart of love,

I need you.

O revealing soul of love,

I need you.

#+END_VERSE


* 51. Teach Me How To Become

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:51
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Father, do teach me

How to become.

Why, my daughter, why?

Father, if you teach me

How to become

Only then shall I be able

To become

Your egoless child,

Your surrendered child,

Your perfect child.

#+END_VERSE


* 52. Don’t Assail

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:52
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Don’t assail humanity.

After all,

Humanity is frail and weak.

Don’t assail divinity.

After all,

Divinity is very audacious and callous.

#+END_VERSE


* 53. Father, Who Am I

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:53
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Father, am I a sleeping fool?

No, daughter, you are a crying fool.

Father, am I a binding fool?

No, daughter, you are an unloving fool.

Father, am I an unpardonable fool?

No, daughter you are an unparalleled fool.

#+END_VERSE


* 54. Hungry Lives

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:54
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My body’s hungry life

Has an endless need.

My soul’s hungry life

Has two childlike needs:

God-Beauty

And

Love-necessity.

My God’s hungry life

Has only one need:

My shadowless smile.

#+END_VERSE


* 55. The Voice Of God And The Choice Of God

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:55
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Love the world.

You will be the Voice of God

Unmistakably.

Serve the world.

You will be the Choice of God

Immediately.

#+END_VERSE


* 56. I Need You

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:56
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O Heaven-born freedom,

Where are you?

O earth-hound bondage,

Where are you not?

O God-promised land,

I need you

And

I need you.

#+END_VERSE


* 57. When And How

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:57
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Father, when shall I realise You?

Daughter, you have already realised me.

Father, when? Father, how?

When? The day you joined my Dream-Boat.

How? Through your surrender-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 58. No, My Daughter, No

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:58
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Father, shall I worship You?

No, my daughter, no.

Father, shall I adore you?

No, my daughter, no.

Father, shall I admire you?

No, my daughter, no.

Father, shall I become like You?

Yes, my sweet daughter, yes.

#+END_VERSE


* 59. Only One Thing

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:59
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

“O Father, Father of my soul,

Only one thing I do not need.”

Daughter, daughter of my heart,

What is it?

“Life-consuming sound.”

Granted, my daughter, granted.

“Father, Father of my soul,

Only one thing I do need.”

Daughter, daughter of my heart.

What is it?

“Life-liberating silence.”

Granted, my daughter, granted.

#+END_VERSE


* 60. Four Universes

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FDP:60
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

What is really huge?

Material universe.

What is really powerful?

Spiritual universe.

What is always in the Goal?

Silent-universe.

What is always the Goal?

Transcending universe.

#+END_VERSE
