  :genre:    poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:8291
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Come out of your mind.
Let your heart love you
And claim you.
You will then feel
What true happiness is.

#+END_VERSE


* 8292

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:    poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:8292
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Allow not your active vital
To fly with the winds
And dance with the waves
Of emotion.

#+END_VERSE

Every divine thought
Is precious!

Every day the Lord Supreme
Is gracious!

  :keywords:
  :cite-key: AP:8299

#+BEGIN_VERSE

See for yourself
Who is the stronger of the two:
Your doubting mind
Or
Your aspiring heart.

#+END_VERSE
