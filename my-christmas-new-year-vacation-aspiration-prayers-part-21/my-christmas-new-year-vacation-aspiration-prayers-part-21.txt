/Cairns, Australia/



* 1 Today I Shall Have To Be ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1563
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Today I shall have to be
My true self
So that my Lord can use me
In a very special way.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 If My Love Of God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1564
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If my love of God
Is genuine,
Then I cannot have
Burdening anxieties.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 If I Cannot Forgive Myself ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1565
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If I cannot forgive myself
For all the blunders
That I have made over the years,
Then how can I proceed?
How can I ever dream
Perfection-dreams?
Move, I must, forward.
Fly, I must, upward.
Dive, I must, inward,
To be once more
What I truly am
And shall forever remain.

#+END_VERSE

  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1566
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

* 56 My Lord, I Miss You ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:1618
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord, I miss You.
“My child, I miss you too.”
My Lord, I love You.
“My child, I love you too.”
My Lord, I need You.
“My child, I need you too.”
My Lord,
Will I ever be able
To become like You?
“My child,
That is My only Dream,
My only Dream,
My only Dream.
I want you to be like Me,
Another God.”
My Lord,
Will I be happy then?
“My child,
That I do not know
And I do not want to know.”
My Lord,
Will I be miserable then?
“My child,
That I do not know
And I do not want to know.”
God, my Lord, my Father,
My Eternity’s All,
Such being the case,
I do not want to be another God.
You remain what You eternally are
And please allow me to remain
What I am.
I want to mingle
With the dust of Your Feet
Throughout Eternity.

#+END_VERSE
