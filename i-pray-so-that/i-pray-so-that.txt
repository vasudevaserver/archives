* 1 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I can play
With my Lord's Compassion-Eye
Every day at least for an hour.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I can become
An illumining ray
Of my Lord Supreme
In His Task
Of world-transformation.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I do not remain
An unaspiring, meaningless
And useless
Lump of clay.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that every day
I can celebrate
As God's Victory-Day
Inside my gratitude-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that my Lord's Way
Becomes my way,
My only way.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I can realise
That God-Compassion
And God-Forgiveness
Pay my wages
For my aspiration and dedication
Infinitely more than I deserve.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I do not have to delay
When the final hour strikes
For the complete transformation
Of my human nature.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I do not allow
The sword of my impurity-mind
To slay my purity-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 9 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that my mind of complexity
Can stay
Inside my heart of simplicity
Which is nothing other
Than visible divinity on earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I never betray
My ever-indulgent
Compassion-Lord Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that every day I can feel
The same joy
As I feel on my birthday.
Then every day
I can make a soulful promise
To become a choice instrument
Of my Lord Supreme,
As I do on my birthday.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I never
Become disheartened
While I essay to bring about
The complete transformation
Of my nature.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I can compel
My earth-bound mind
To obey
My Heaven-free soul.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I do not
Give up working
On my nature's transformation
Only halfway
As many ill-fated seekers do.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 I Pray ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I do not doubt
When my heart is eager
To convey
Blessingful messages to me
From my Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

By dark doubts.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray

Aspiration-tree

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray
So that I do not become a prey

Even in my unconscious moments
And unconscious movements.

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: PST:19
  :END:

My sweet Lord
Even when my impure vital,
My dark mind
And my insecure heart
Torture me ruthlessly.

#+END_VERSE
