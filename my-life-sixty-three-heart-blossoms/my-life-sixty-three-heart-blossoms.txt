* One

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:1
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
On my birthday I am praying to You
To make me a member
Of Your inner Circle.

#+END_VERSE


* Two

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:2
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
On my birthday I am praying to You
To make me realise
That my Master is my soul-heart-life-lover
And not my soul-heart-life-dictator.

#+END_VERSE


* Three

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:3
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
On my birthday I am praying to You
To make me realise
That I am Your Heart's golden Dream,
That You are my life's only Reality.

#+END_VERSE


* Four

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:4
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
On my birthday I am praying to You
To grant me the capacity
To keep my mind-telephone-line
Open only to you.

#+END_VERSE


* Five

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:5
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
On my birthday I am praying to You
To make me realise
That my life is not a false hope's dead end
But a true, illumining and fulfilling hope's
Continuous journey.

#+END_VERSE


* Six

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:6
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
On my birthday I am praying to You
To give me the capacity
To stop broadcasting
My mind's giant-ego-news
Once and for all.

#+END_VERSE


* Seven

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:7
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
On my birthday I am praying to You
To compel my fighting mind
To completely surrender
To my embracing heart.

#+END_VERSE


* Eight

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:8
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord Beloved Supreme,
O my birthday I am praying to You
To torture me, to punish me
In the forest
Of my disobedience-repentance-tears.

#+END_VERSE


* Nine

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:9
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

On my birthday
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Is commanding me
Not to underestimate
My God-manifestation-capacity.

#+END_VERSE


* Ten

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:10
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

On my birthday
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Is telling me
That although He is omnipotent,
Although He is the Possessor
Of infinite Power,
He is my heart-happiness-Beggar
And my life-smile-Dreamer.

#+END_VERSE


* Eleven

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:11
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

On my birthday
My Lord Beloved Supreme
Wants me to answer
A few Compassion-questions:
"My child, who told you
To swim in the sea of stupidity?
Who prevented your life
From progressing?
Who gave you the capacity
To decide your fate?
Who compelled you to sing
Your life's failure-song?
And who compelled you to stop dancing
Your life's victory-dances?"

#+END_VERSE


* Twelve

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: MLHB:12
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I am thirsty
For my Lord's Compassion-Eye.
My Lord is hungry
For my satisfaction-heart.

#+END_VERSE
