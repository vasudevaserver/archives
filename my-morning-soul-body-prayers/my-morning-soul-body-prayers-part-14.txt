#+BEGIN_VERSE

1 May 2000

7:23 a.m.

Before doing standing

double-dumbbell lift

with 1,300 lbs.

4 times

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!

I have only one prayer:

Do give me the capacity

To utter Your Name prayerfully,

Soulfully and self-givingly

With every heartbeat of mine.

My Supreme, my Supreme, my Supreme!
