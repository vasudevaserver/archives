/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Xiamen, China in December 2004./

/20 December 2004/

/Xiamen, China/



* 1 Anything That Is Human ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2560
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Anything that is human
Is fleeting.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2561
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Is everlasting.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:2562
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Between truth and falsehood,
Rest assured,
The ultimate victory
Goes to the truth.

#+END_VERSE

  :END:

Destroys our love of God.
