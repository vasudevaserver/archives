* 1 If We Believe In Our Own...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If we believe in our own
Self-transcendence-task
Then there can be
No unreachable goal.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 The Mind's Expectation-Danger...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The mind's expectation-danger
Destroys the heart's
Surrender-oneness-joy
To the Will of God.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Our Goal Is Not To Have...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Our goal is not to have
A problem-free life.
But to conquer all the problems
As they appear
Along our life-road.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 We All Must Exterminate...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We all must exterminate
The doubt-mind-insects
If we really want
True happiness in our life.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 God Does Not Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God does not mind
If you complain
Against God to humanity.
But God does mind
If you do not take proper care
Of your own inner life.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 It Is Only...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

It is only
The oneness-heart-joy
That expands and grows
And becomes the vastness-sky.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 The Mind's Thought-Barrier...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The mind's thought-barrier
Is the source of impossibility.
Let us pray and meditate
To go far beyond
The extremities of the mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 If We Are Not Alive...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If we are not alive
To all our opportunities,
How can we ever proceed
In our God-loving aspiration-life?

#+END_VERSE


* 9 To Whom Shall We Give...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To whom shall we give
Our stupidity-mind,
If not to God?
To whom shall we give
Our sincerity-heart.
If not to God?

#+END_VERSE


* 10 If We Are Not Hungry...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If we are not hungry
For the Light Supreme,
We are bound to hanker after
Pleasure-illusion-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 Each Time My Lord Supreme...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each time My Lord Supreme
Fulfils my earth-bound desire,
I feel that my aspiration-heart
Enters deeper into
The jungle of despair.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 O My Sleeping Body, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my sleeping body,
O my striving vital,
O my searching mind,
O my crying heart,
God has not forgotten you.
Before long He will bless you
With His complete
Satisfaction-Smiles.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 I Have Betrayed My Inner Life....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I have betrayed my inner life.
My Lord of Compassion
Has forgiven me.
But I cannot and I must not
Forgive myself.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 God Gives Himself Completely...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God gives Himself completely
To a seeker's surrender-life
And
To his gratitude-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 When God...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When God
Challenges ignorance-night
On my behalf,
Then only I am going to succeed.
Otherwise, never.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 I Love My Heart's Rainbow-Dreams, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I love my heart's rainbow-dreams,
For they carry me
Lovingly and safely
To the Golden Shore.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 Those Who Have...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Those who have
Astonishing capacities
For self-deception
Will need countless years
For self-perfection.

#+END_VERSE


* 18 O My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind,
You are such a fool
That you are trying to rebuild
Your collapsing ego-building.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 A Seeker Of The Highest Order...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A seeker of the highest order
Has a silence-mind
To entertain him
All the time.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 Earth-Achievements...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Earth-achievements
Are no match
Even for an iota
Of Heaven-enlightenment.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 A Doubtful And Suspicious Mind...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A doubtful and suspicious mind
Must carry a very heavy load
On a very long journey.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 An Aspiring Heart...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

An aspiring heart
Every day must renew
Its contract
With God's Compassion-Eye.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 We Must Vehemently And Equally...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We must vehemently and equally
Defy the pride of
Our mind's wrath
And
Our body's sloth.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 O My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind,
God will be here in a minute.
Are you ready to receive God?
O my heart,
God will be here in a minute.
Are you willing to receive God?
O my life,
God will be here in a minute.
Are you eager to receive God?

#+END_VERSE


* 25 God Was Not Successful With...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God was not successful with
His Compassion-Height-approach.
He is now tying a new approach:
His Justice-Light.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 The Seekers Who Live...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The seekers who live
For the divine Cause
Will definitely receive
A thunderous applause
From the denizens
Of the higher world.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 The Day I Expect...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The day I expect
Nothing from the world,
I receive both
The heart-flower
And
The soul-fragrance
Of the world.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 Our Failure-Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Our failure-life
Comes into being
From our own confusion-mind
And not from anything else.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 The Joy Of A Self-Giving Life...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The joy of a self-giving life
Can neither be measured
Nor be expounded.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 He Who Thinks...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He who thinks
That there is no room
For progress-delight
Is utterly mistaken.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 The Ups And Downs...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The ups and downs
Of God-manifestation
Will entirely depend
On the seeker's
Sincerity-heart-aspiration
And
Simplicity-mind-inspiration.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 He Who Serves God Self-Givingly...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He who serves God self-givingly
Will always remain close
To the Heart of God.

#+END_VERSE


* 33 Faith Is Wisdom....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Faith is wisdom.
Faith is power.
Faith is heart-fragrance.
Faith is the sun-child
Of the Beyond.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 I Shall Plant...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I shall plant
All my sufferings
In my soul's
Patience-light-field.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 The Wall On Which...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The wall on which
Humanity has to lean
Is
The wall of self-dedication
And not
The wall of self-admiration.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 The World Is Growing...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The world is growing
Worse and worse every day,
Precisely because
It has lost the fragrance
Of its God-surrender-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 True, We Cannot Reform...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

True, we cannot reform
Our forefathers,
But we can and we must reform
Our own children
And their own children.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 When I Converse...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When I converse
With the ancient heart,
We always talk
About the goodness-depth
Of human beings.
When I converse
With the modern mind,
We only talk
About the greatness-length
Of human life.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 We Show Ourselves...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We show ourselves
As we wish
To be seen.
God shows Himself
As we wish Him
To be seen.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 O Greatness, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O greatness,
You are the product
Of my transitory mind.
O goodness,
You are the product
Of my God-repository-heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 The Divine Prosperity...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The divine prosperity
Will come into existence
Only when sincerity-heart talks-
Here, there and everywhere.

#+END_VERSE


* 42 Ignorance-Sleep...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Ignorance-sleep
Is universal.
Wisdom-delight
Is transcendental.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 You Will Be Happy...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

You will be happy
Only when you think
What God wants you to think
And not think of yourself
The way the whole world
Thinks about you.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 If You Are Not Ready...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you are not ready
To endure sufferings
On your way to God's Palace,
How will you ever
Discover God,
The only Satisfaction?

#+END_VERSE


* 45 If You Do Not Disobey...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you do not disobey
Your doubting mind,
How can your aspiring heart
Ever carry you
To your Destination supreme?

#+END_VERSE


* 46 If You Do Not Sharpen...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you do not sharpen
Your discrimination-sword,
Your mind-thorns
Will ruthlessly torture you.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 We Take Pride...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We take pride
In our mind's mortal nest.
God wants us
To take pride
In our heart's immortal palace.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 Because Of Aspiration-Shortage, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Because of aspiration-shortage,
Man is compelled
By God the Justice-Light
To have
A fearful heart
And
A tearful life.

#+END_VERSE


* 49 Every Day...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Every day
The aspiring heart
Must dream a new
God-manifestation-dream.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 O Dreamers Of Peace, Come....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: NUG:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O dreamers of peace, come.
Let us walk together.
O lovers of peace, come,
Let us run together.
O servers of peace, come.
Let us grow together.

#+END_VERSE
