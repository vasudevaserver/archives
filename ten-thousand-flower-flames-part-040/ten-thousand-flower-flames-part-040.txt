* 3901 My Heart's Only Choice

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre:  poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: FF:3901
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Lord,
True, You do not show me Your Face.
But that does not mean
I cannot feel Your Heart.

My Lord,
True, I do not feel Your Heart.
But that does not mean
I do not know who You are -
You are my heart's only choice.

#+END_VERSE
