/Sri Chinmoy offered the following prayers during his visit to Kijal, Malaysia in January 2006./

/5 January 2006/

/Kijal, Malaysia/



* 1 O Seeker ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3420
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O seeker,
Do not slow down!
God’s Heart
Is eagerly waiting
For you.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 We Think Of God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3421
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We think of God.
We speak about God.
But, alas,
How rarely
We stand up for God!

#+END_VERSE


* 3 God Firmly Says To My Body ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3422
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God firmly says to my body,
“You must never think
Of your
Old-age retirement-pleasure!”

#+END_VERSE


* 4 Peace-Gong ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3423
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Peace-gong
The mind cannot hear.
Peace-flute
The heart hears,
And the melody of the flute
The heart shares lovingly
And self-givingly
With the rest of the world.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 My Devotion-Heart-Eye ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3424
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My devotion-heart-eye
Is the only eye
That can see
My Lord’s golden Feet.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Alas ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3425
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Alas,
The human mind
At times enjoys confusion,
Even dissatisfaction.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: prayer
  :CITE-KEY: APR:3426
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

To see God’s real Smile.
Alas,
God’s real Smile
Is nowhere to be found.

#+END_VERSE

  :CITE-KEY: APR:3427
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

Is much greater
Than the power of evil.
