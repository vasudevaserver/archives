To my extreme joy,
There is no such thing
As a last chance
To please God!

My mind-prayer cries
For God the Power.
My heart-meditation dreams
Of God the Love.
My soul-surrender dines
With God the Satisfaction.

In the depths of my love.

Of my willingness-heart,

Unconditional surrender
To God's Will.
