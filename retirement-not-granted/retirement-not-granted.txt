* 1 God Does Not Want Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: RNG:1
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God does not want me
To have any retirement,
Not to speak of
Early retirement.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 God With The Form ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: RNG:2
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God with the Form
Awakens me.
God without the Form
Beckons me.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Conches Blew ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: RNG:3
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

And trumpets blared
In Heaven
When my life became
An unconditional surrender
To God's Will.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:

  :CITE-KEY: RNG:4
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

My Lord's Eye,
But I sing inside
My Lord's Heart.

#+END_VERSE

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE: poem
  :CITE-KEY: RNG:8
  :KEYWORDS:
  :END:

Whomever I meet
On my way to God,
I ask only one thing:
Does God really love me?
This question has completely
Occupied my mind.

#+END_VERSE

God is waiting for me?
