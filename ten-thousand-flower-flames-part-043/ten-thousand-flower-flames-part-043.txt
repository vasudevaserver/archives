* 4201 God Shares His Greatness-Height

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:  poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: FF:4201
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God shares His Greatness-Height
With my daily prayers.
God shares His Goodness-Light
With my daily meditations.
God shares His Oneness-Delight
With my daily surrender.

#+END_VERSE
