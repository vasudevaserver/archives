* 20401

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:    poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: AP:20401
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Enlightenment
Comes from commitment.
Commitment
Comes from eagerness.
Eagerness
Comes from soulfulness.
Soulfulness
Comes from purity.
Purity
Comes from sincerity.
Sincerity
Comes from necessity,
My God-necessity.

#+END_VERSE

* 20500

  :PROPERTIES:
  :GENRE:    poem
  :KEYWORDS:
  :CITE-KEY: AP:20500
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Absolute Lord Supreme,
May my mind today and every day
Think of You only.
May my heart today and every day
Love You only.
May my life today and every day
Serve You only.
May I today and every day
Place my success-joy and my progress-peace
At Your Compassion-Feet.

#+END_VERSE
