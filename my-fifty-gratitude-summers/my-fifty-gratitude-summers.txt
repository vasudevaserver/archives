* 1 Aspiration Begins....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:1
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Aspiration begins.
Surrender continues.
Gratitude concludes.

#+END_VERSE


* 2 Enthusiasm Is ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:2
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Enthusiasm is
God's God-Beauty
In His seeker-children.

#+END_VERSE


* 3 Sincerity Is My Heart's ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:3
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Sincerity is my heart's
Silver dawn.
Purity is my soul's
Golden sunrise.

#+END_VERSE


* 4 We Know And We Do Not Know....

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:4
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

We know and we do not know.
We know how to roam,
But we do not know
How to return Home.

#+END_VERSE


* 5 My Beloved Supreme, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:5
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My Beloved Supreme,
I prayed to You to make me
A realised soul.
Alas! Instead of making me
A realised soul,
You have made me
A mechanised human body.

#+END_VERSE


* 6 Aspiration ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:6
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Aspiration
Is my heart's beauty.
Illumination
Is my soul's beauty.
Satisfaction
Is my Lord's Beauty.

#+END_VERSE


* 7 Forget What God Has ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:7
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Forget what God has
Already forgotten:
Your insincerity-mind,
Your impurity-heart
And
Your ingratitude-life.

#+END_VERSE


* 8 How Can I Have ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:8
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

How can I have
A new king: my God,
In my heart-kingdom
If I do not dethrone
The present king: my ego?

#+END_VERSE


* 9 My Psychic Gladness ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:9
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My psychic gladness
And
My vital madness
Never live together.

#+END_VERSE


* 10 Be ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:10
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be
A God-dreamer.
All your fear-tortures
Will leave you.

#+END_VERSE


* 11 When ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:11
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When
I am sincere,
My Beloved Supreme
Is pleased with me.
When
I am pure,
I am satisfied
With my Beloved Supreme.

#+END_VERSE


* 12 God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:12
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God
Is for
My aspiring heart.
Spirituality
Is for
My searching mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 13 I Pray To God ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:13
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I pray to God
For His Absolute Vision
And not for
His Possession-Universe.

#+END_VERSE


* 14 My Mind's Discovery: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:14
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My mind's discovery:
God has given me
Heavy burdens.
My heart's discovery:
God has given my
Strong shoulders.

#+END_VERSE


* 15 The Gift Of My Outer Life ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:15
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The gift of my outer life
To my inner life
Is my constantly soulful
Service.
The gift of my inner life
To my outer life
Is my sleeplessly fruitful
Love.

#+END_VERSE


* 16 Yesterday ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:16
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Yesterday
My mind was
An uneducated and ignorant fool.
Today
My mind is
An educated and learned fool.
Tomorrow
My mind shall be
A sleepless ignorance-hunter.

#+END_VERSE


* 17 Am I Ready To Scold ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:17
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Am I ready to scold
My impurity-mind?
Am I?
Am I ready to warn
My insecurity-heart?
Am I?

#+END_VERSE


* 18 Because ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:18
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Because
You are a childlike believer,
Your God is a constant
Giver.

#+END_VERSE


* 19 He Had To Enter Into ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:19
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

He had to enter into
His own spiritual grave.
Why? Because he was
Sleeplessly fond of his
Confusion-mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 20 I May Not Know ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:20
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I may not know
The beginning. But
I shall definitely know
The end.

#+END_VERSE


* 21 Simplicity ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:21
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Simplicity
Is and will forever remain
A perfect stranger
To the modern mind.

#+END_VERSE


* 22 A Purity-Life ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:22
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

A purity-life
And
A gratitude-heart
Are immediate neighbours.

#+END_VERSE


* 23 The Most Glorious Beginning ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:23
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The most glorious beginning
Of my miracle-life: I have
Started loving God, my Beloved
Supreme, in His own way.

#+END_VERSE


* 24 God Is Ready To Give Me ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:24
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God is ready to give me
Another chance.
Alas, am I ready?
Will I ever be ready
To accept it?

#+END_VERSE


* 25 Each Aspiring Life ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:25
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each aspiring life
Is a prolonged and
Extensive Dream
Of God.

#+END_VERSE


* 26 My Past Was Completely ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:26
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My past was completely
Destroyed on the day
I employed my surrendering
Thoughts to help me
Reach my God.

#+END_VERSE


* 27 O My Vital, Do Not Complain;...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:27
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my vital, do not complain;
You may be totally wrong.
O my mind, do not assert;
You too may be
Totally wrong.

#+END_VERSE


* 28 Be Not Afraid Of God, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:28
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Be not afraid of God,
Be not afraid.
Your sweet reunion
Need not be delayed.

#+END_VERSE


* 29 Reveal Constantly ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:29
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Reveal constantly
What you are: a supremely
Chosen instrument of God.
Conceal powerfully
What you are not:
A giant prince of
Ignorance-dream.

#+END_VERSE


* 30 An Uninspiring Thought, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:30
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

An uninspiring thought,
Short or long, is spiritually
Wrong.
An unaspiring life,
Short or long, is spiritually
Wrong.

#+END_VERSE


* 31 My Wisdom-Flames ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:31
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

My wisdom-flames
Pray to God.
My stupidity-seas
Pray for God.

#+END_VERSE


* 32 Self-Perfection: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:32
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Self-perfection:
Yes!
Self-annihilation:
No!

#+END_VERSE


* 33 I Export ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:33
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I export
Aspiration-flames
From earth to Heaven.
I import
Satisfaction-suns
From Heaven to earth.

#+END_VERSE


* 34 Each Time ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:34
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Each time
You have a chance,
Hurl your wisdom-lance
At ignorance-tiger.

#+END_VERSE


* 35 The Divine ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:35
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The divine
Will definitely conquer
The human, the way
The human
Has conquered the animal.

#+END_VERSE


* 36 If You Accept ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:36
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

If you accept
Fantasies,
Ecstasy will not
Receive you.

#+END_VERSE


* 37 O My Mind, ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:37
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

O my mind,
You have talked enough.
Now begin to listen.
I have got a superb teacher
For you. You will simply
Love your new teacher: heart.

#+END_VERSE


* 38 Confusion ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:38
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Confusion
Cannot last.
Just change
Your direction.

#+END_VERSE


* 39 When Told That All Human Beings ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:39
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

When told that all human beings
Are by nature ungrateful,
Everybody thinks that
He is an exception.

#+END_VERSE


* 40 I See You ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:40
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

I see you
As an ordinary man.
You see yourself
As a demigod.
God sees you
As another God.

#+END_VERSE


* 41 Do You Want To Be ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:41
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Do you want to be
The real winner,
Do you?
Then lose to Truth,
Lose!

#+END_VERSE


* 42 It Is Not Enough ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:42
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

It is not enough
To love God
In the God-lover.
We must also love God
In the God-hater.

#+END_VERSE


* 43 The Illumining Soul ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:43
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

The illumining soul
Uncovers,
And
The aspiring heart
Discovers.

#+END_VERSE


* 44 Success ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:44
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Success
Is an incident.
Progress
Is an experience.

#+END_VERSE


* 45 Make Your Choice: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:45
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Make your choice:
Do you want to be
The servant of a book
Or
A good student
Of your soul?

#+END_VERSE


* 46 God-Seekers ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:46
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

God-seekers
Feast
On God's great
Silence.

#+END_VERSE


* 47 There Is Only ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:47
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

There is only
One perfect road
And
That road is ahead of
You,
Always ahead of
You.

#+END_VERSE


* 48 Only ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:48
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

Only
The God-centered souls
Can spread
Their victory-trophies
At God's Feet.

#+END_VERSE


* 49 His Mind Lives ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:49
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

His mind lives
inside the secret
Sorrow-cave.
His heart lives
Inside the sacred
Sorrow-home.

#+END_VERSE


* 50 His Life's Late-Evening Discovery: ...

  :PROPERTIES:
  :genre: poem
  :keywords:
  :cite-key: MFGS:50
  :END:


#+BEGIN_VERSE

His life's late-evening discovery:
God's Forgiveness-Power
Has smashed
God's Justice-Tower.

#+END_VERSE
